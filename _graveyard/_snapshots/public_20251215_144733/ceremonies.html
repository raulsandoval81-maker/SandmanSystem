Ceremonies

Sandman ceremonies are where hard work becomes visible.
Athletes earn XP through discipline — the ceremony is the moment it’s recognized. This page explains the idea. All eligibility and scripts stay behind coach login.

[ Ceremony board / tier banners placeholder ]

What We Celebrate

  • Foundry 8 (Youth) — athletes completing key steps in the Zero→Hero journey
  • Foundry 4 (Combat) — teens and adults finishing a tier on the Quest→Legend path
  • Leadership Milestones — athletes stepping into mentorship and service roles
  • Discipline Highlights — attendance streaks, character wins, and parent feedback
How It Fits Into the System

Sandman runs on three major arcs: Zero→Hero for youth, Quest→Legend for older athletes, and Master’s Quest for leadership development.
Ceremonies happen when an athlete finishes a tier or completes a major phase in one of these arcs.
