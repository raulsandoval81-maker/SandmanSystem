Welcome

Welcome to Lompoc High School Wrestling. This page is the starting point for coaches and parents to stay aligned on expectations, communication, and support for our athletes.

Our goal is simple: a healthy, competitive program where wrestlers work hard, treat others with respect, and learn habits that stay with them long after the season is over.

How to Use Para-Comms / Cómo Usar Para-Comms

English: Para-Comms is our bilingual communication tool. Use it when you have questions about schedules, expectations, grades, transportation, or anything related to the Lompoc HS Wrestling program.

  • Step 1: Open Para-Comms Home or the Messages page.
  • Step 2: Click “Open Para-Comms” to go to the message console.
  • Step 3: Type your message in your preferred language (English or Spanish).
  • Step 4: Send. A coach will respond as soon as possible.

Please use phone or text only for urgent situations. Para-Comms is best for questions, updates, and communication that can wait for a thoughtful response.

Español: Para-Comms es nuestra herramienta de comunicación bilingüe. Úsela cuando tenga preguntas sobre horarios, expectativas, calificaciones, transporte o cualquier cosa relacionada con el programa de Lompoc HS Wrestling.

  • Paso 1: Abra la página de Para-Comms o la página de Mensajes.
  • Paso 2: Haga clic en “Open Para-Comms” para ir a la consola de mensajes.
  • Paso 3: Escriba su mensaje en el idioma que prefiera (inglés o español).
  • Paso 4: Envíe el mensaje. Un entrenador responderá lo antes posible.

Para situaciones urgentes, comuníquese por teléfono o mensaje de texto directamente con un entrenador. Para-Comms es mejor para preguntas, avisos y comunicación que puede esperar una respuesta con calma.

Program Culture

A few simple ideas guide how we show up every day:

  • We work hard, finish what we start, and take pride in our effort.
  • We speak to others with respect — teammates, opponents, officials, teachers, and families.
  • We own our choices, learn from mistakes, and move forward together.
  • We carry the same standard in the room, on campus, and anywhere we wear Lompoc gear.

Parent Expectations (Quick View)

  • Support your athlete with consistent attendance, rest, and healthy habits.
  • Use the meeting portal and Para-Comms for questions and updates, rather than sideline conversations.
  • Help us model calm, respectful behavior at practices, meets, and tournaments.

Full details are available in the Team Policy and the Standard Athletic Agreement.
