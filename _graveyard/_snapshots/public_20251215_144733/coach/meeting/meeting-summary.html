Team Policy & Conduct

  • Attendance and effort are expected at every practice and event.
  • Athletes arrive prepared, on time, and in proper gear.
  • Respect for coaches, teammates, officials, and opponents at all times.

We are building habits that carry far beyond wrestling.

Schedule & Travel

  • Practice and competition schedules are updated as needed.
  • The team travels together and follows school/district guidelines.
  • Parent support with rides, food, and supervision is appreciated.

Please check announcements and Para-Comms regularly for changes.

Safety, Wellness & Hydration

  • We follow CIF and school safety guidelines in practice and competition.
  • Healthy sleep, nutrition, and hydration are part of the expectation.
  • Injuries or health concerns should be reported directly to coaches.

Health comes first. Let us know early if something is wrong.

Team Parents & Support Roles

  • Team parents help with logistics, fundraising, and event support.
  • They support communication but do not make coaching decisions.
  • Unified sideline behavior sets the tone for our athletes.

Thank you for volunteering your time and representing Lompoc with class.

Communication with Coaches

  • For questions or concerns, contact coaches directly by text, call, or email.
  • Please avoid sending messages through athletes or social media threads.
  • No sideline coaching from the stands during matches or practice.

For general information, please use the Para-Comms Parent Portal.

Practice Boundaries & Contact

  • Wrestling practices are closed to parents and spectators.
  • Please avoid approaching coaches 30 minutes before or after practice.
  • Email is preferred to schedule meetings about individual concerns.
  • Phone calls should be reserved for time-sensitive or urgent needs.

These boundaries protect focus, safety, and clear communication.

Program Culture & Values

  • We emphasize respect, effort, discipline, and growth every day.
  • Wrestling is a tool to build character, not just wins and losses.
  • A united message at home and on the mat helps athletes thrive.

Thank you for supporting the standard and trusting the process.

Braves Wrestling • United. Focused. Relentless.
