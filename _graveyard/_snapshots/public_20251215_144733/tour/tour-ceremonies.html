The Bonsai — A Living Emblem

The tree grows as the athlete grows. Each tier adds strength, color, and presence.

  • Growth by Tier: Tier 0–1 faint/ghosted → mid-tiers fuller canopy → Tier 7–8 wide, rooted, confident.
  • Color Journey: White → Yellow → Orange → Green → Blue → Purple → Brown → Gold → Black.
  • Why it matters: Athletes see progress they can wear and show. Parents see the story. Coaches keep the standard.
“Better to be a warrior in a garden than a gardener in a war.” — We grow warriors with roots.
