🥋 ShadowTrainer — Version 1

Beginner Combat Weapon

Unlock Stripe: 0 (given automatically)
Identity Path: Combat
Role: Entry-level solo drill weapon

🔥 Purpose

ShadowTrainer v1 teaches the beginner the Sandman fundamentals: stance, motion, level change, and fake mechanics. No combos. No attacks. No scramble. Just foundations and discipline.

🧱 Core Structure (v1)

1) Stance & Motion (Base)

  • Stance Hold — 10 seconds
  • Forward Motion
  • Back Motion
  • Circled Motion (L/R)

2) Level Change (Foundation)

  • Slow level change × 10
  • Controlled level change × 10
  • Add motion → level change

3) Fake Mechanics (Intro)

  • Static fake
  • Fake with step
  • Fake with level change

4) Down-Block (Basics)

  • Light sprawl motion
  • Quick hands

🕒 Session Format

  • 3 rounds
  • 45 seconds on / 30 seconds off
  • Light sweat, no burnout
  • Focus on posture and discipline

🎯 Progression Markers

  • Maintains clean stance for full round
  • Level changes without rising up
  • Fake mechanics look believable
  • No breaks in focus
