FAQ

What age groups do you serve?

Youth athletes train in Foundry 8. Teens and young adults train in Foundry 4 Combat and Leadership. Both lanes use the same XP backbone, adjusted for age and responsibility.

How do ceremonies work?

Athletes earn XP toward tier goals. Once requirements are met and coach-approved, promotions and ceremonies are scheduled so the work is recognized the right way.

Is the XP system visible to parents?

Yes. Each tier has a clear XP cap and visual bar. Parents and athletes can see how close they are to the next stripe or tier, instead of guessing.

Can I volunteer or help coach?

Yes. Share a little about your background, experience, and which lane you’d like to support (youth, combat, or leadership). Opportunities open first inside the pilot.
