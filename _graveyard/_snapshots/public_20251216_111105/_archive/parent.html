Parent FAQs

How do stripes and badges work?

Each athlete earns three stripes: Discipline (attendance/effort), Skill (technique), and Character (teamwork/leadership). When all three are earned, they receive the badge for that level.

What is XP?

XP is a simple point system for progress. Showing up, trying hard, and nailing skills adds XP. ~250 XP usually equals a stripe.

When are ceremonies?

Promotions happen around Week 6 cycles. Check Ceremonies & Recognition for dates.

      What is XP?

      XP = effort points. Training sessions, technical wins, and leadership reps all add up. Stripes every 250 XP.

      Badges vs. Shirts vs. Belts

      Badges = system milestones. Shirts = strength levels. Belts = combat proficiency. All are earned, never bought.
