A) Team Expectations & Standards

  • Attendance & effort are required at every practice and event.
  • Athletes arrive on time, in proper gear, and ready to work when practice begins.
  • Respect teammates, coaches, opponents, and officials at all times.
  • No profanity, bullying, harassment, or unsafe behavior in or around the program.
  • Phones stay put away during practice, weigh-ins, and team events unless a coach approves otherwise.
  • Injuries, sickness, or family absences are reported directly to a coach before practice or competition.
  • Maintain grades and classroom behavior that meet school and district eligibility standards.
  • Follow travel expectations on buses, in hotels, and at venues — curfews and supervision rules are non-negotiable.
  • Social media posts should reflect school pride — no posts that embarrass teammates, staff, or the program.
  • Represent Lompoc Wrestling with class on and off campus; the uniform and warm-ups are earned and must be worn with respect.

B) Coach Standards Pledge

  • Prepared & On-Time — coaches arrive ready with a practice plan and keep the room structured and safe.
  • Respect & Sportsmanship — coaches model calm, respectful behavior toward athletes, officials, and other programs.
  • Communication — coaches provide clear information on schedules, expectations, and concerns, and respond as promptly as possible.

These standards protect our room and our kids: calm, consistent, united.
