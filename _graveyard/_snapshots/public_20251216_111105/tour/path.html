How it Works

  • Every practice & event earns XP. XP unlocks Badges (skills), adds Shirt stripes (consistency), progresses Belts (rank), and opens the door to Honor (character & leadership).
  • Youth climb a 9-tier ladder from Shadow to Legend.
  • Foundry4 (Teen/Adult) focus on five tiers from Apprentice to Legend.
  • Promotion = skill tests + service + sportsmanship. No shortcuts.
