Tournament Flow

Weigh-ins, brackets, communication, recovery — predictable flow so athletes perform.

Problem → Solution

Problem: “Where do we meet? What time?” chaos at every event.

Solution: One event brief: meet spot, mats, first bout ETA, coach on duty, parent role.

Problem: Bracket surprises and panic cuts.

Solution: Simple weight plan week-of + bracket watchlist + night-before checklist.

Problem: Emotions blow up after a loss.

Solution: Post-match protocol: 2 keeps, 1 fix, hydrate, reset; parents get a script for support.

Problem: Nothing is logged afterwards.

Solution: Quick entry: opponent, result, 1 clip, 1 focus tag → rolls into the ladder.
