What is the Quest?

The Quest is Sandman’s training journey. It’s simple on purpose: Show up Do the work Earn the next mark

This page is public. It explains the journey without exposing private training tools or XP data.

Path to Glory

A practical ladder that any athlete can climb. Everyone starts at zero; every step is earned.

Step 1 — Roots: Learn the Creed. Live for others and let them live for you.
Step 2 — Trunk: Honor the Code. We are all Martial Artists—forever a student to the craft.
Step 3 — Branches: Set your Goal. Prepare for life’s quest (Zero to Hero).
Step 4 — Leaves: Mission mindset. Build heroes who build heroes.

Progress is visible, steady, and coach-verified. No shortcuts.

  • Attendance & Effort: show up, give effort, log your work.
  • Milestones: complete core skills, earn stripes/marks.
  • Reflections: short notes on what you learned and how you led.
  • Coach Checks: evaluation & promotion when you’re ready.

Badges (How We Mark the Journey)

Badges represent visible milestones. They celebrate consistency, skill growth, character, and leadership.

How It Works

1) Train — Show up, practice, compete, help your team.

2) Track — Coaches confirm progress; you see your next step.

3) Advance — Hit the standard, earn the stripe or badge, move up.

Character First. Strength and honor. Leadership counts.

Transparent Path. You always know what “next” looks like.

Earned, not given. Zero2Hero—one step at a time.
