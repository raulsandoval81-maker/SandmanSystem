🥋 ShadowTrainer — Version 2

Intermediate Combat Weapon

Unlock Stripe: 1
Identity Path: Combat

🔥 Purpose

ShadowTrainer v2 builds the athlete’s engine. Footwork becomes tighter, fake mechanics become faster, and Level Change → Shot Position is introduced. This is where the athlete starts moving like a real combatant.

⚙️ Core Structure (v2)

1) Stance → Motion Flow

  • Crisp stance transitions
  • Motion + level changes
  • Fake → Circle → Re-center

2) Shot Position (Entry)

  • Penetration step
  • Knee-over-toe entry
  • Penetration + back-out

3) Fake → Level Change → Entry

  • Short fake → step
  • Fake → level change → touch
  • Fake → re-attack angle

4) Down Block → Circle Out

  • Quick block
  • Circle & reset

🕒 Session Format

  • 4 rounds
  • 60 seconds on / 30 seconds off
  • Increased pace — control over chaos

🎯 Progression Markers

  • Enters shot position cleanly
  • Fake → entry transitions are crisp
  • Pace control is visible
  • Circles out instinctively
