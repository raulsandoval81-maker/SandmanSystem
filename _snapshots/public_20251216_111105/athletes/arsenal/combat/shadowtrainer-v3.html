🥋 ShadowTrainer — Version 3

Advanced Combat Weapon

Unlock Stripe: 2
Identity Path: Combat Specialist
Lane: Combat

🔥 Purpose

ShadowTrainer v3 transforms solo combat practice into a true weapon. At this level the athlete develops instinctual flow, scrambling reactions, pace control, and positional mastery — preparing them for real fight rhythm.

🧬 Core Structure (v3)

1) Chain Reaction Drills (Flow Mode)

  • Level Change → Attack → Sprawl Reaction → Circle Out
  • Fake Shot → Snap → Go-Behind → Breakdown Ride
  • Jab → Slip → Level Change → Shadow Finish

3 flow routes per session, 30–45 sec each, 3–5 rounds.

2) Pace Switching (Gears Mode)

  • Gear 1 — slow, technical
  • Gear 3 — controlled fight pace
  • Gear 5 — explosive burst

20s / 20s / 10s rhythm. Repeat x4.

3) Positional Mastery (Stance Lock)

  • Stance + Motion
  • Down Block
  • Sprawl
  • Ride Position
  • Finish Angles

Hold 15s, transition instantly. 4–6 transitions/min.

4) Scramble Simulator (Battle Flow)

  • Shot stuffed → spin behind → re-attack
  • Fake lift → reset angle → finish
  • Kick-out scramble → re-enter attack

1 min on / 1 min off × 3 rounds.

🧠 Mental Layer

  • “React, don’t freeze.”
  • “Recover inside chaos.”
  • “See the move before it happens.”
  • Quiet narration strengthens flow and timing.

🎯 Progression Markers (Stripe 2 → Stripe 4)

  • Cleans 3 chain-routes at 70% speed
  • Maintains motion 90 seconds
  • Completes mixed-gears rounds smoothly
  • Executes 4 scramble flows in a row
  • Transitions cleanly through all core positions
