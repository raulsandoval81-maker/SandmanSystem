Volunteer Role Expectations

  • Be present, punctual, and prepared.
  • Follow all safety, conduct, and supervision protocols.
  • Be a role model — attitude, language, energy, and effort matter.
  • Support the coaching staff and athletes without disruption.
  • Report any incidents or concerns immediately.

All volunteers are expected to represent the Sandman System with professionalism, humility, and pride. We lead by example.
