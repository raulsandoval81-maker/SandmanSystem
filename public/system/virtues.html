Unified Sandman Virtues

These virtues apply across the entire Sandman System. Youth are taught and guided in them. Older athletes are held accountable to them.
MintTag format example: F4_CB####_STEWARDSHIP

V

HONOR

Do the right thing even when it costs you. Carry yourself in a way that builds trust.

MintTag format: F#_CB####_HONOR
V

COURAGE

Act despite fear. Step forward when it’s hard and speak when silence would be easier.

MintTag format: F#_CB####_COURAGE
V

DISCIPLINE

Keep standards daily. Discipline is the promise you keep to your future self.

MintTag format: F#_CB####_DISCIPLINE
V

INTEGRITY

Do what’s right when nobody is watching. Let your actions match your words.

MintTag format: F#_CB####_INTEGRITY
V

PATIENCE

Trust the long road. Growth takes time, and pressure does not rush mastery.

MintTag format: F#_CB####_PATIENCE
V

WISDOM

Learn quickly and choose well. Apply experience with restraint and clarity.

MintTag format: F#_CB####_WISDOM
V

STRENGTH

Power with control. Build strength to protect, serve, and uphold standards.

MintTag format: F#_CB####_STRENGTH
V

TENACITY

Hold the line when it’s hard. Finish with the same standard you started with.

MintTag format: F#_CB####_TENACITY
V

ACCOUNTABILITY

Own outcomes without excuses. Responsibility begins where blame ends.

MintTag format: F#_CB####_ACCOUNTABILITY
V

STEWARDSHIP

Protect what’s been entrusted to you—people, culture, time, and standards.

MintTag format: F#_CB####_STEWARDSHIP
