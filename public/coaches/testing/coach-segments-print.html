2. Neutral Defense

Coach calls: Head & hands, chest & hips, low-level defense
Watch: Don’t get caught, see it early, hips back, block shots, recover position

3. Top

Coach calls: Top pressure, breakdown, keep working
Watch: Weight on hands, heavy control, effective breakdowns, chain to score
Room language: Left side. Right side. Position breakdowns. If this → then that. Two-on-one. Pinning series.
Quick notes

4. Bottom

Coach calls: Whistle, build, clear hands, get out
Watch: Explosive first move, strong base, clear elbow, win hands, escape, no panic
Room language: Stand up. Don’t stay flat. Hold if stuck. No panic.
Quick notes

5. Ride

Coach calls: Deep waist ride, back to mat
Watch: Establish ride, lock and settle, finish returns, stay on top through reactions
Room language: Don’t give escapes. They move → you adjust. Stay on top no matter what.
Quick notes

6. Effort — Finish When It’s Hard

Coach calls: Partner lets you in. Partner fights setup.
Watch: Can they finish when resistance shows up? Do they keep working? Do they stay in the fight?
Room language: Easy in → hard finish. Hard in → hard finish. Don’t stop. Push pace.
