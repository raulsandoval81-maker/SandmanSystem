House Standard
Coach asks: What are the house standards?

Athlete response: Focus, effort, attitude, respect.

1. Neutral Offense

Coach calls

Square stance, staggered stance, favorite tie, favorite shot.

Watch

Stance, motion, entry, pressure, scoring intent, finish.

Room language

Easy in, no finish. Easy in, easy finish. Hard in, easy finish. Hard in, hard finish.

2. Neutral Defense

Coach calls

Head & hands, chest & hips, low-level defense.

Watch

Don’t get caught, early reaction, hips back, block shots, recover position.

Room language

Go behind. Reshot. Reattack.

3. Top

Coach calls

Top pressure, breakdown, keep working.

Watch

Weight on hands, heavy control, effective breakdowns, chaining, scoring intent.

Room language

Left side. Right side. Position breakdowns. If this → then that. Two-on-one. Pinning series.

4. Bottom

Coach calls

Whistle, build, clear hands, get out.

Watch

Explosive first move, strong base, clear elbow, win hands, escape, no panic.

Room language

Stand up. Don’t stay flat. Hold if stuck. No panic.

5. Ride

Coach calls

Deep waist ride, back to mat.

Watch

Establish ride, lock and settle, finish returns, stay on top through reactions.

Room language

Don’t give escapes. They move → you adjust. Stay on top no matter what.

6. Effort — Finish When It’s Hard

Coach calls

Partner lets you in. Partner fights setup.

Watch

Can they finish when resistance shows up? Do they keep working? Do they stay in the fight?

Room language

Easy in → hard finish. Hard in → hard finish. Don’t stop. Push pace.
