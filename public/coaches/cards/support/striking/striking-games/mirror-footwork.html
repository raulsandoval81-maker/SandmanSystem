Mirror Footwork

Striking Game

Goal

Build stance discipline, reactions, movement control, and distance awareness.

Setup

Athletes pair up facing each other in stance. One athlete leads movement while the other mirrors.

Play

  • Stay balanced and in stance
  • Hands stay active and up
  • Mirror all movement directions
  • No crossing feet
  • Switch leader every 30–45 seconds

Coach Notes

Focus on rhythm, movement quality, posture, and reaction timing.
