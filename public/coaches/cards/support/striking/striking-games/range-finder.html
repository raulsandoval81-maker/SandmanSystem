Striking Game

Goal

Improve distance awareness, movement timing, and clean range management.

Setup

Athletes pair up in stance with controlled movement.

Play

  • Only light touches allowed
  • Win exchanges through positioning and range
  • No heavy shots
  • Move continuously
  • Reset after clean exchanges

Coach Notes

Focus on distance control, foot placement, and patience.
