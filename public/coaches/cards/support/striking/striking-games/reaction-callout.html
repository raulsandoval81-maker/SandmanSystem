Reaction Callout

Striking Game

Goal

Improve reaction timing, listening skills, movement speed, and striking awareness.

Setup

Athletes spread across the room in stance awaiting coach commands.

Play

  • Coach calls movement or strike commands
  • Athletes react immediately
  • Maintain stance and posture
  • Reset quickly between commands
  • Incorrect reactions perform quick resets

Coach Notes

Keep pace high and commands unpredictable to build awareness and reactions under pressure.
