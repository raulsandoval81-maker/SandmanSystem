Jab Tag

Striking Game

Goal

Develop jab timing, reactions, distance management, and movement discipline.

Setup

Athletes pair up with jabs only.

Play

  • Only lead-hand jabs allowed
  • Light touch contact only
  • Move continuously
  • Score with clean jab touches
  • No power shots

Coach Notes

Focus on timing, movement, posture, and controlled accuracy.
