Touch & Move

Striking Game

Goal

Improve movement, reactions, light contact timing, and distance control.

Setup

Athletes pair up in stance with light movement only.

Play

  • Light shoulder or glove touches only
  • No hard contact
  • Move continuously
  • Score with clean controlled touches
  • Reset if intensity rises

Coach Notes

Emphasize movement quality, reactions, and controlled tempo.
