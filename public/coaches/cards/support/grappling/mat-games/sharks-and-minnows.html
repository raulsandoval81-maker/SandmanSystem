Sharks & Minnows

Game

Goal

Build movement, reaction, mat awareness, and athletic play.

Setup

One or two sharks start in the center. Everyone else begins on one edge of the mat.

Play

  • On the whistle, minnows attempt to cross the mat
  • If a shark tags a minnow, they become a shark next round
  • Continue until only one minnow remains

Coach Notes

Keep the pace high and rounds short. Great for youth energy, movement, and spatial awareness.
