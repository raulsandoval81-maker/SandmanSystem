King of the Mat

Game

Goal

Compete live with simple rules while building toughness, position, and confidence.

Setup

Create a circle or small mat space. One athlete starts in as the king while others rotate in quickly.

Play

  • Use short live goes (10–20 seconds)
  • Coach sets the win condition
  • Win conditions can include push out, knee touch, takedown, or control
  • Winner stays in
  • Loser rotates out

Coach Notes

This is the bridge from games into real wrestling. Keep the pace high and rotations fast. Control intensity through rule selection.
