Land, Sea, Air

Game

Goal

Build reaction speed, listening discipline, movement, and body control.

Setup

Athletes line up facing the coach with clear movement space. Coach establishes movement directions before starting.

Play

  • Coach calls “Land,” “Sea,” or “Air”
  • Each command matches a movement or action
  • Land = move one direction
  • Sea = move opposite direction
  • Air = jump, sprawl, or freeze based on coach rules
  • Wrong reactions trigger a reset task or elimination

Coach Notes

Keep commands fast and unpredictable. Add stance rules, level changes, sprawls, or shots to match practice focus. Great for youth energy and listening under pressure.
