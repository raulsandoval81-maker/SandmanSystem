Clinch Break Game

MMA Game

Goal

Improve clinch exits, posture, and re-entry awareness.

Setup

Partners begin in controlled clinch positions.

Play

  • Fight for clean breaks
  • Reset to striking stance after exits
  • Re-enter under control
  • No wild movement

Coach Notes

Focus on posture, hand fighting, and disciplined exits.
