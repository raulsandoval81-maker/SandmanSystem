Sprawl And Return

MMA Game

Goal

Build fast defensive reactions, recovery posture, and return-to-striking discipline.

Setup

Partners move continuously while mixing fake shots, level changes, and sprawls.

Play

  • Defend level changes immediately
  • Sprawl with hips and posture
  • Recover quickly back to stance
  • Reset movement after every defense
  • Maintain controlled pace

Coach Notes

Focus on reactions, hip pressure, stance recovery, and smooth transitions back to striking range.
