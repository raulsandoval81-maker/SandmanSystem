Ground To Stand

MMA Game

Goal

Improve urgency and technical recovery from grounded positions.

Setup

Athletes begin seated or grounded before recovering to stance.

Play

  • Recover quickly to standing position
  • Protect posture during stand-up
  • Reset and repeat continuously
  • Stay technical under fatigue

Coach Notes

Emphasize technical recovery, posture, and urgency.
