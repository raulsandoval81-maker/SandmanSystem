Skill 19 — Ride Control System

Tree: Top

Tier: Teen T2 — Champion

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Establish Control

B — Maintain Pressure

C — Transition Control

Coaching Cues

  • “Control before attack”
  • “Heavy hips, no space”
  • “Flow with movement”

Common Mistakes

  • Losing position during transitions
  • Riding too high
  • No pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Ride flow drills
  • Continuous top control
  • Pressure + follow drill

Progression

  • Add turn setups
  • Connect to spiral ride
