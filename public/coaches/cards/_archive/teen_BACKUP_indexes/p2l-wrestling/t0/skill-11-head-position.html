Skill — Head Position

Tree: Neutral Defense

Tier: Teen T0 — Apprentice

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Inside control

B — Pressure

C — Stay safe

Coaching Cues

  • “Win the forehead fight”
  • “Head leads the body”
  • “Position first, then pressure”

Common Mistakes

  • Head outside with no control
  • Pressure without balance
  • Losing head position while hand fighting

Drill Ideas

  • Head position pummel drill
  • Forehead pressure drill
  • Short hand-fight rounds

Progression

  • Add tie control
  • Add attack from head position
