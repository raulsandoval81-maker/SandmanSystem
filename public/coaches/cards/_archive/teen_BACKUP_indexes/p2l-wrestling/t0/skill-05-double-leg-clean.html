Skill — Double Leg (Clean)

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Teen T0 — Apprentice

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Entry

B — Penetration

C — Finish

Coaching Cues

  • “Level change before you step”
  • “Head up, chest up”
  • “Drive through, don’t stop on contact”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping to knees with no drive
  • Head down on entry
  • Stopping after contact instead of finishing

Drill Ideas

  • Penetration step reps
  • Partner double leg finish drill
  • Shot line drill

Progression

  • Add setup before entry
  • Add finish under resistance
