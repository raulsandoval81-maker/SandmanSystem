Skill — Down Block

Tree: Neutral Defense

Tier: Teen T0 — Apprentice

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Block

B — Stop shot

C — Go behind

Coaching Cues

  • “Hands first”
  • “Block the line, not the air”
  • “Move your feet after the stop”

Common Mistakes

  • Reaching late
  • Blocking but not moving feet
  • Stopping the shot but not scoring off it

Drill Ideas

  • Down block reaction drill
  • Shot-stop-go-behind drill
  • Coach call reaction reps

Progression

  • Add re-shot
  • Add front headlock option
