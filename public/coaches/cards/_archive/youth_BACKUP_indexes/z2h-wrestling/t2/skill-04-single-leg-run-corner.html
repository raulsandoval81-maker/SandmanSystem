Skill 4 — Single Leg (Run Corner)

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Youth T2 — Combatant

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Secure Leg

B — Stand Up

C — Run the Corner

Coaching Cues

  • “Hold tight”
  • “Get to your feet”
  • “Run the corner”

Common Mistakes

  • Loose grip on the leg
  • Staying on knees
  • Trying to finish straight on

Drill Ideas

  • Single leg finish reps
  • Run-corner drills
  • Live finish situations

Progression

  • Connect to snap to score
