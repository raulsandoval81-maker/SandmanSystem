Skill 12 — Chain Finish

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Youth T2 — Combatant

Structure (A/B/C)

A — First Attack

B — Immediate Transition

C — Finish the Score

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t stop at the first shot”
  • “Flow to the next attack”
  • “No reset—keep going”
  • “Score through the chain”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after first attempt
  • Resetting instead of transitioning
  • Waiting instead of flowing

Drill Ideas

  • Shot to re-shot chains
  • Double → single → go-behind sequences
  • Continuous attack rounds

Progression

  • Connect to full chain wrestling sequences
