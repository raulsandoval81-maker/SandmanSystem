Skill 12 — Front Headlock to Score

Tree: Neutral Defense

Tier: Youth T7 — Hero

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Secure Front Headlock

B — Apply Heavy Pressure

C — Finish the Score

Coaching Cues

  • “Head down, hips heavy”
  • “Control first”
  • “Score from the front”

Common Mistakes

  • Loose front headlock
  • No pressure through the position
  • Letting opponent recover underneath

Drill Ideas

  • Front headlock control reps
  • Spin-behind finish drills
  • Live front headlock rounds
