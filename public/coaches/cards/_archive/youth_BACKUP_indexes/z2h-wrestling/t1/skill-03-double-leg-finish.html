Skill 3 — Double Leg Finish

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Youth T1 — Recruit

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Secure Legs

B — Drive

C — Finish to Mat

Coaching Cues

  • “Head up”
  • “Run your feet”
  • “Finish strong”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after contact
  • Head down
  • Weak drive

Drill Ideas

  • Double leg finish reps
  • Drive through drills
  • Live finish rounds

Progression

  • Connect to single leg finish
