Skill 14 — Hip Heist to Score

Tree: Bottom

Tier: Youth T5 — Champion

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Post Strong

B — Turn Hips Fast

C — Score From Movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Post and turn hard”
  • “Move your hips fast”
  • “Finish the score”

Common Mistakes

  • Weak post
  • Slow hip turn
  • Recovering without scoring intent

Drill Ideas

  • Hip heist reps
  • Heist to reversal drills
  • Bottom scoring rounds

Progression

  • Connect to base under pressure
