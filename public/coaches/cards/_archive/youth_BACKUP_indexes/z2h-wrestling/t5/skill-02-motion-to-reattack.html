Skill 1 — Pressure Motion

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Youth T5 — Champion

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Forward Position

B — Controlled Pressure

C — Motion With Intent

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure without reaching”
  • “Stay in stance while moving forward”
  • “Make him carry you”

Common Mistakes

  • Leaning instead of pressuring
  • Standing too tall
  • Walking forward without control

Drill Ideas

  • Partner pressure walks
  • Stance-motion pressure drill
  • Push-pull with forward recovery

Progression

  • Connect to motion to re-attack
