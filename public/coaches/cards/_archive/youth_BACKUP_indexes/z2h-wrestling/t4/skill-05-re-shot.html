Skill 5 — Re-Shot

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Youth T4 — Warrior

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Opponent Shoots

B — Defend

C — Shoot Back

Coaching Cues

  • “Defend then go”
  • “Stay ready”
  • “Attack immediately”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after defense
  • Slow reaction
  • Backing away instead of attacking

Drill Ideas

  • Defend to re-shot drills
  • Reaction rounds
  • Live counter situations

Progression

  • Connect to arm drag
