Skill 2 — Distance Control

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Youth T4 — Warrior

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Stay in Range

B — Control Space

C — Enter Clean

Coaching Cues

  • “Too far = no shot”
  • “Too close = danger”

Common Mistakes

  • Shooting from too far
  • Standing too close without control
  • Poor spacing

Drill Ideas

  • In/out movement drills
  • Distance reaction games

Progression
