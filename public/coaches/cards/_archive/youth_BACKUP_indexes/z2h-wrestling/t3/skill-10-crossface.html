Skill 10 — Crossface

Tree: Top

Tier: Youth T3 — Competitor

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Arm Across Face

B — Pressure In

C — Control Position

Coaching Cues

  • “Across the face”
  • “Stay tight”
  • “Use pressure”

Common Mistakes

  • Soft crossface
  • Wide elbow
  • No follow-up control

Drill Ideas

  • Crossface reps
  • Top pressure drills
  • Crossface to control rounds

Progression

  • Connect to cradle
