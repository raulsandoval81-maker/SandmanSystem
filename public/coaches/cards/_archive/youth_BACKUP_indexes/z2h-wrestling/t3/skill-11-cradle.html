Skill 11 — Cradle

Tree: Top

Tier: Youth T3 — Competitor

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Lock Head and Knee

B — Pull Tight

C — Roll and Control

Coaching Cues

  • “Lock it tight”
  • “Head and knee together”
  • “Roll with control”

Common Mistakes

  • Loose lock
  • No control before roll
  • Losing position after turn

Drill Ideas

  • Cradle lock reps
  • Crossface to cradle drills
  • Controlled turn rounds

Progression

  • Connect to ride control
