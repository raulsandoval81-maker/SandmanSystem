Skill 2 — Motion to Attack

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Youth T3 — Competitor

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create Motion

B — Move Opponent

C — Attack Opening

Coaching Cues

  • “Move first, then go”
  • “Make the opening”
  • “Attack on reaction”

Common Mistakes

  • Attacking from still position
  • No setup before shot
  • Telegraphing the attack

Drill Ideas

  • Motion to shot reps
  • Fake and go drills
  • Reaction entry rounds

Progression

  • Connect to head position
