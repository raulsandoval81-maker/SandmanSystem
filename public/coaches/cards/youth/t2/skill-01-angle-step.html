Skill 1 — Stance & Motion Control

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Youth T1 — Recruit

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Stay Low

B — Move Controlled

C — Stay Balanced

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay in stance”
  • “Small controlled steps”
  • “Don’t pop up”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing tall during movement
  • Crossing feet
  • Losing balance

Drill Ideas

  • Circle + stance drills
  • Coach direction calls
  • Mirror motion drill

Progression

  • Connect to level change to shot
