Skill 5 — Snap to Score

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Youth T2 — Combatant

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control Head

B — Snap Down

C — Score Behind

Coaching Cues

  • “Snap hard”
  • “Move right away”
  • “Score behind”

Common Mistakes

  • Weak snap
  • Watching after the snap
  • Not getting behind

Drill Ideas

  • Snap to go-behind reps
  • Head control drills
  • Reaction scoring rounds

Progression

  • Connect to down block
