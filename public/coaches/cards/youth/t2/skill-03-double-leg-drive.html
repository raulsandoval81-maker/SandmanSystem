Skill 3 — Double Leg (Drive)

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Youth T2 — Combatant

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Level Change

B — Penetrate

C — Drive Through Finish

Coaching Cues

  • “Head up”
  • “Drive your feet”
  • “Go through, not to”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping on contact
  • Head down
  • Weak foot drive

Drill Ideas

  • Double leg drive reps
  • Drive through pad drills
  • Partner finish rounds

Progression

  • Connect to single leg run corner
