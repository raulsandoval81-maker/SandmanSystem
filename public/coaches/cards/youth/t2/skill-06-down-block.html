Skill 6 — Down Block

Tree: Neutral Defense

Tier: Youth T2 — Combatant

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Block Head/Shoulders

B — Stop Forward Attack

C — Create Space

Coaching Cues

  • “Block and move”
  • “Meet the shot”
  • “Stay low”

Common Mistakes

  • Blocking late
  • Standing up on defense
  • No movement after block

Drill Ideas

  • Down block reps
  • Shot reaction drills
  • Block and recover rounds

Progression

  • Connect to sprawl to spin
