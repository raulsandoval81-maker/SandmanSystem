Skill 3 — Pressure Footwork

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Youth T6 — Commander

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Controlled Steps

B — Maintain Pressure

C — Direct Opponent

Coaching Cues

  • “Feet drive pressure”
  • “Don’t lean—step”
  • “Walk him where you want”

Common Mistakes

  • Leaning instead of stepping
  • Overstepping
  • Losing stance

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure walk drills
  • Push-pull footwork
  • Control movement rounds

Progression

  • Connect to tempo control
