Skill 11 — Front Headlock System

Tree: Neutral Defense

Tier: Youth T6 — Commander

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control Head

B — Apply Pressure

C — Score From Position

Coaching Cues

  • “Head down, hips heavy”
  • “Control before attack”
  • “Score from front headlock”

Common Mistakes

  • No pressure
  • Letting opponent escape easily

Drill Ideas

  • Front headlock reps
  • Snap to control drills
  • Spin-behind scoring rounds

Progression

  • Connect to whizzer control
