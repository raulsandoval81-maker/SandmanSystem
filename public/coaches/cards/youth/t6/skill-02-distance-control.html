Skill 2 — Distance Control

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Youth T6 — Commander

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize Range

B — Adjust Distance

C — Control Entry

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the space”
  • “In or out on purpose”
  • “Enter clean”

Common Mistakes

  • Shooting from bad range
  • Standing too close without control
  • No adjustment to opponent

Drill Ideas

  • In/out control drills
  • Distance reaction games
  • Controlled entry rounds

Progression

  • Connect to pressure footwork
