Skill 14 — Turn Flow

Tree: Top

Tier: Youth T7 — Hero

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Setup Position

B — Attempt Turn

C — Flow to Next Attack

Coaching Cues

  • “Flow between turns”
  • “Never stop attacking”
  • “Stay tight throughout”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after first turn
  • Loose control between moves
  • Rushing without setup

Drill Ideas

  • Turn flow drills
  • Multi-turn rounds
  • Top chain situations

Progression

  • Connect to chain turns
