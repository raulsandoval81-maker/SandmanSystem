Skill 4 — Motion to Re-Attack

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Youth T7 — Hero

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create Motion

B — Force Reaction

C — Re-Attack

Coaching Cues

  • “First move sets the next”
  • “Stay active”
  • “Attack again immediately”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after first attack
  • No movement before re-attack
  • Predictable pattern

Drill Ideas

  • Attack → re-attack chains
  • Continuous motion rounds
  • Reaction drills

Progression

  • Connect to multi-chain wrestling
