Skill 01 — Stance & Motion

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Youth T1 — Recruit

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Stance (low, balanced, ready)

B — Motion (forward/backward, circle)

C — Level change (controlled, not dropping)

Coaching Cues

  • “Move your feet, not your body”
  • “Stay low, stay ready”
  • “Don’t click your heels”
  • “Hands in front, eyes up”

Goal

Athletes can move in stance without crossing feet, popping up, or losing balance.
