Skill 11 — Front Headlock

Tree: Neutral

Tier: Youth T1 — Recruit

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Secure Head Control

B — Keep Weight Forward

C — Circle to Finish

Coaching Cues

  • “Snap and secure”
  • “Weight forward”
  • “Circle, don’t chase”
  • “Spin, go-behind, or snap again”

Common Mistakes

  • Letting the head pop free
  • Standing too tall
  • Chasing instead of circling

Drill Ideas

  • Snap to front headlock reps
  • Circle and spin-behind drills
  • Front headlock control rounds

Progression

  • Connect to go-behind and re-snap finishes
