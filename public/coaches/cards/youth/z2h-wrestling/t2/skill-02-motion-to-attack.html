Skill 2 — Motion to Attack

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Youth T2 — Combatant

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Move Opponent

B — Create Opening

C — Attack Right Away

Coaching Cues

  • “Move first, then go”
  • “Make the opening”
  • “Attack on reaction”

Common Mistakes

  • Shooting from still position
  • No setup before attack
  • Telegraphing the shot

Drill Ideas

  • Motion to shot reps
  • Fake and go drills
  • Reaction entry rounds

Progression

  • Connect to double leg drive
