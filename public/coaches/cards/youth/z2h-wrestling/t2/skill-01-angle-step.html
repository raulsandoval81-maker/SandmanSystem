Skill 1 — Angle Step

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Youth T2 — Combatant

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Step Off Line

B — Create New Angle

C — Stay Ready to Attack

Coaching Cues

  • “Step off, don’t stay square”
  • “Move your feet, then attack”
  • “Create angle with balance”

Common Mistakes

  • Stepping too big
  • Crossing feet
  • Moving without staying in stance

Drill Ideas

  • Angle step shadow reps
  • Coach point-and-step drill
  • Angle step to shot entry

Progression

  • Connect angle step to motion-to-shot
