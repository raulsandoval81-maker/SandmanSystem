Skill 11 — Front Headlock Control

Tree: Neutral

Tier: Youth T2 — Combatant

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Secure Head + Arm

B — Apply Forward Pressure

C — Circle to Control or Score

Coaching Cues

  • “Snap and secure”
  • “Weight forward”
  • “Circle, don’t chase”
  • “Spin or snap again”

Common Mistakes

  • Losing head control
  • Standing up out of pressure
  • Chasing instead of circling

Drill Ideas

  • Snap to front headlock reps
  • Circle + spin-behind drills
  • Front headlock control rounds

Progression

  • Connect to go-behind and chain finishes
