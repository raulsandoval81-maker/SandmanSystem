Skill 6 — Single Leg

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Youth T0 — Shadow

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Grab Leg

B — Stand Up

C — Finish

Coaching Cues

  • “Hold tight”
  • “Stand up strong”
  • “Finish clean”

Common Mistakes

  • Loose grip
  • Staying on knees
  • Not finishing

Drill Ideas

  • Single leg reps
  • Lift and finish drill
  • Balance drills

Progression

  • Connect to snap/go behind
