Skill 8 — Tie to Score

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Youth T7 — Hero

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Secure Tie

B — Create Opening

C — Score

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t leave ties empty”
  • “Every tie leads to offense”
  • “Score from control”

Common Mistakes

  • Holding ties without purpose
  • No attack from position
  • Letting opponent break free

Drill Ideas

  • Tie to attack drills
  • Control to score rounds
  • Live tie wrestling

Progression

  • Connect to scramble winning
