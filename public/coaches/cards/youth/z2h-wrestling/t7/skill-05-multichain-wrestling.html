Skill 5 — Multi-Chain Wrestling

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Youth T7 — Hero

Structure (A/B/C)

A — First Attack

B — Read Reaction

C — Multiple Chains

Coaching Cues

  • “Keep flowing”
  • “Chain until you score”
  • “React, don’t force”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping mid-chain
  • Forcing same finish
  • Breaking rhythm

Drill Ideas

  • 3–4 attack chains
  • Reaction-based chaining
  • Continuous scoring rounds

Progression

  • Connect to re-attack system
