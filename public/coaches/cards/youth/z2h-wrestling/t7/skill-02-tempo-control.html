Skill 2 — Tempo Control

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Youth T7 — Hero

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Set Pace

B — Break Rhythm

C — Control Exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “You control the match pace”
  • “Change speeds at will”
  • “Make him react”

Common Mistakes

  • Same rhythm entire match
  • Predictable timing
  • No control of pace

Drill Ideas

  • Tempo rounds
  • Rhythm break drills
  • Slow-fast control games

Progression

  • Connect to position awareness
