Skill 6 — Arm Drag

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Youth T4 — Warrior

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control Arm

B — Pull Across

C — Move Behind

Coaching Cues

  • “Pull, don’t push”
  • “Step with it”
  • “Get behind fast”

Common Mistakes

  • Weak pull
  • No foot movement
  • Stopping before getting behind

Drill Ideas

  • Arm drag reps
  • Drag to go-behind drills
  • Live tie situations

Progression

  • Connect to head & arm defense
