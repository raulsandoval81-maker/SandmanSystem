Skill 5 — Double Leg (Finish)

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Youth T3 — Competitor

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Penetrate Deep

B — Secure Both Legs

C — Finish Through

Coaching Cues

  • “Head up”
  • “Drive your feet”
  • “Finish through the hips”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping on contact
  • Head down
  • Weak finish drive

Drill Ideas

  • Double leg finish reps
  • Drive-through drills
  • Partner finish rounds

Progression

  • Connect to snap down to go behind
