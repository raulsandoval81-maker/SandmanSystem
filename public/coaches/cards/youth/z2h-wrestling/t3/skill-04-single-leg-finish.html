Skill 4 — Single Leg (Finish)

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Youth T3 — Competitor

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Secure Leg

B — Stand Up

C — Finish Clean

Coaching Cues

  • “Hold tight”
  • “Get up strong”
  • “Finish under control”

Common Mistakes

  • Loose grip
  • Staying on knees
  • Rushing the finish

Drill Ideas

  • Single leg finish reps
  • Stand-up finish drills
  • Live finish rounds

Progression

  • Connect to double leg finish
