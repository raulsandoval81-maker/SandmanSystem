Skill 1 — Angle Step

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Youth T3 — Competitor

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Step Off Line

B — Stay Balanced

C — Create Attack Angle

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t stay square”
  • “Small clean step”
  • “Angle before attack”

Common Mistakes

  • Stepping straight ahead
  • Standing up during step
  • Overstepping and losing stance

Drill Ideas

  • Angle step reps
  • Circle to angle drills
  • Coach call movement drill

Progression

  • Connect to motion to attack
