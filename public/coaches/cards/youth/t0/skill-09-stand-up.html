Skill 9 — Stand Up

Tree: Bottom

Tier: Youth T0 — Shadow

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build Base

B — Stand Up

C — Turn Away

Coaching Cues

  • “Hands first”
  • “Stand strong”

Progression
