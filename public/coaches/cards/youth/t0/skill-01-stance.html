Skill 1 — Stance

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Youth T0 — Shadow

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Feet Set

B — Knees Bent

C — Hands Ready

Coaching Cues

  • “Low and ready”
  • “Hands in front”
  • “Eyes up”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing too tall
  • Hands down
  • Feet too close together

Drill Ideas

  • Stance hold
  • Coach check positions
  • Mirror stance drill

Progression

  • Connect to motion
