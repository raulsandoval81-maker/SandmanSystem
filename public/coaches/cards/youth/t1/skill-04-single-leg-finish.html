Skill 4 — Single Leg Finish

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Youth T1 — Recruit

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Secure Leg

B — Stand Up

C — Finish

Coaching Cues

  • “Hold tight”
  • “Stand up strong”
  • “Finish clean”

Common Mistakes

  • Loose grip
  • Staying on knees
  • Not finishing the attack

Drill Ideas

  • Single leg finish reps
  • Lift and finish drills
  • Balance control drills

Progression

  • Connect to snap down
