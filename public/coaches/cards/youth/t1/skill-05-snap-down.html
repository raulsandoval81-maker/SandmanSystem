Skill 5 — Snap Down

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Youth T1 — Recruit

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control Head

B — Snap Down

C — Move Behind

Coaching Cues

  • “Snap hard”
  • “Head down”
  • “Move right away”

Common Mistakes

  • Weak snap
  • No movement after snap
  • Standing still

Drill Ideas

  • Snap reps
  • Snap to go-behind drills
  • Reaction drills

Progression

  • Connect to go behind
