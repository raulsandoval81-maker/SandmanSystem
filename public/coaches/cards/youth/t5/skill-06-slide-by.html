Skill 6 — Slide By

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Youth T5 — Champion

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Secure Contact

B — Pull and Slide

C — Get Behind

Coaching Cues

  • “Pull him past you”
  • “Slide, don’t force”
  • “Get behind clean”

Common Mistakes

  • Pushing instead of pulling
  • No foot movement
  • Stopping halfway through

Drill Ideas

  • Slide-by reps
  • Pull-and-go drills
  • Live tie-to-score rounds

Progression

  • Connect to re-shot system
