Skill 8 — Front Headlock Control

Tree: Neutral Defense

Tier: Youth T5 — Champion

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control Head

B — Apply Pressure

C — Hold Position

Coaching Cues

  • “Head down, hips heavy”
  • “Control first”
  • “Don’t let him up easy”

Common Mistakes

  • Loose control
  • No hip pressure
  • Letting opponent posture up

Drill Ideas

  • Front headlock reps
  • Pressure hold rounds
  • Spin-behind control drills

Progression

  • Connect to sprawl to score
