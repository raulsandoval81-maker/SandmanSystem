Skill 4 — Chain Wrestling

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Youth T5 — Champion

Structure (A/B/C)

A — First Attack

B — Read Reaction

C — Second Attack

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t stop at one”
  • “Flow to the next shot”
  • “Score off reactions”

Common Mistakes

  • Single attack only
  • Pausing between attempts
  • Forcing the same finish

Drill Ideas

  • 2-attack chain reps
  • Reaction-based finishes
  • Chain wrestling rounds

Progression

  • Connect to underhook series
