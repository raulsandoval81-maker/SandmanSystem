Skill 13 — Hip Heist

Tree: Bottom

Tier: Youth T4 — Warrior

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Post Hand

B — Turn Hips

C — Face Opponent

Coaching Cues

  • “Post and turn”
  • “Move your hips fast”
  • “Face him again”

Common Mistakes

  • Slow hip turn
  • No strong post
  • Stopping halfway

Drill Ideas

  • Hip heist reps
  • Base to heist drills
  • Bottom movement rounds

Progression

  • Connect to avoid pin
