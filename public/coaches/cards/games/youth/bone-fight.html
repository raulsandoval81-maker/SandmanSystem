Bone Fight

Game

Goal

Teach push-pull feel, hand fighting, and controlled competition.

Setup

Use a towel, shirt, noodle, or similar object as the “bone.” Two athletes compete for control.

Play

  • Start neutral
  • Athletes fight for control of the object
  • Win position or pull the object cleanly under control
  • Reset and rotate quickly

Coach Notes

Keep the rules tight and intensity controlled. Great for feel, pressure, grip fighting, and competition without over-coaching.
