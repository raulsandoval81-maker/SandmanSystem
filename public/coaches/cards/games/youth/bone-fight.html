Bone Fight

Game

Goal

Teach push-pull feel, hand fighting, and compete-with-control.

Setup

Use a towel, shirt, noodle, or similar object as the “bone.” Two athletes compete for control.

Play

Start neutral. Athletes fight for the object and try to win position or pull it cleanly under control.

Coach Notes

Keep rules tight. Good for feel, pressure, and play without over-coaching.
