Fox Tail

Game

Goal

Build reaction, movement, stance discipline, and awareness.

Setup

Each athlete tucks a towel or “tail” into the back of their shorts. Everyone is live in the space.

Play

  • On the whistle, athletes try to steal tails
  • If your tail is pulled, you are out or complete a quick reset task
  • Last athlete with a tail wins

Coach Notes

Emphasize stance, level changes, and movement. Keep the pace high but controlled. Great early or mid-practice energy piece.
