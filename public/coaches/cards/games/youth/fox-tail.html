Fox Tail

Game

Goal

Reaction, movement, stance discipline, and awareness.

Setup

Each athlete tucks a towel (“tail”) into the back of their shorts. Everyone is live in the space.

Play

  • On whistle, athletes try to steal tails
  • If your tail is pulled, you’re out or do a quick reset task
  • Last athlete with a tail wins

Coach Notes

Emphasize stance, level, and movement. Keep it fast and controlled. Great early or mid-practice energy piece.
