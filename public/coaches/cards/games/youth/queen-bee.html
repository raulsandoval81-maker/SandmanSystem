Queen Bee

Game

Goal

Develop balance, position, and competitive reactions.

Setup

One athlete starts in as the queen. Others rotate in one at a time or through a short line.

Play

  • Use short live rounds
  • Coach sets a simple win condition
  • Win conditions can include push out, knee touch, or controlled score
  • Winner stays in
  • Rotate quickly between reps

Coach Notes

Keep the win condition simple and intentional. Match the rules to the feeling and behavior you want from the room.
