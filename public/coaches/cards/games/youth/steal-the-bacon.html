Steal the Bacon

Game

Goal

Build reaction, speed, and quick decision-making under pressure.

Setup

Split athletes into two teams. Assign each athlete a number and place an object (“bacon”) in the center.

Play

  • Coach calls a number
  • Matching athletes sprint to the center
  • Grab the object and return to your side
  • The opponent can tag before you cross back safely

Coach Notes

Add stance requirements, shots only, or touch rules to match your focus. Keep rounds fast, competitive, and high energy.
