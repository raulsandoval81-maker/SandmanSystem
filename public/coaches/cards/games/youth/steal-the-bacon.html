Steal the Bacon

Game

Goal

Reaction, speed, and quick decision-making under pressure.

Setup

Split into two teams. Each athlete has a number. Place an object (“bacon”) in the center.

Play

  • Coach calls a number
  • Those athletes sprint to the center
  • Grab the object and return to your side
  • Opponent can tag before you cross back

Coach Notes

Add stance requirement, shots only, or touch rules to match your focus. Keep rounds fast and high energy.
