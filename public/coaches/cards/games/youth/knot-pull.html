Knot Pull

Game

Goal

Grip fight, reaction, stance discipline, and quick compete.

Setup

Use a towel or band with a knot. Two athletes start in stance with shared access to the object.

Play

On whistle, athletes fight to secure the knot and win the rep under control.

Coach Notes

Fast reps. Reset quickly. Good for younger kids when you want competitive feel without full wrestling.
