Knot Pull

Game

Goal

Build grip fighting, reaction, stance discipline, and quick competition.

Setup

Use a towel or band with a knot. Two athletes start in stance with shared access to the object.

Play

  • Start in stance
  • On the whistle, athletes fight to secure the knot
  • Win the rep under control
  • Reset quickly and rotate

Coach Notes

Keep reps fast and controlled. Great for younger athletes when you want competitive feel without full wrestling intensity.
