Cowboys & Bulls

Game

Goal

Team pressure, control, and controlled chaos.

Setup

  • Split athletes into two teams
  • Bulls start in the center
  • Cowboys start around the outside

Play

  • On the whistle, Cowboys enter and try to control or ride the Bulls
  • Bulls fight to escape or stay on their feet
  • Switch roles each round

Rules

  • Choose feet only or controlled mat work before starting
  • No dangerous throws
  • Keep rounds short (10–20 seconds)

Coach Notes

Set boundaries before starting. This can escalate quickly if the pace gets out of control. Emphasize teamwork, awareness, and safe pressure.
