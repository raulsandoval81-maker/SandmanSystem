Cowboys & Bulls

Game

Goal: Team pressure, control, and controlled chaos.

Setup:

  • Split into two teams
  • One side = Bulls (in center)
  • Other side = Cowboys (outside)

Play:

  • On whistle, Cowboys enter and try to control or “ride” Bulls
  • Bulls fight to escape or stay on feet
  • Switch roles each round

Rules (coach control):

  • Stay on feet OR controlled mat only (pick one)
  • No dangerous throws
  • Short rounds (10–20 sec)

Focus: pressure, teamwork, control, compete.

Set boundaries before starting—this can get wild fast.
