Snake Pit

Game

Goal

Break opponents down, build pressure, and survive chaos.

Setup

One athlete starts on all fours in the center. Others line up around the pit.

Play

  • One athlete starts on all fours
  • The attacker starts on feet
  • Attacker drags until hips or knees touch the mat
  • Once broken, that athlete joins the snake
  • Next athlete steps in immediately
  • The snake grows each round
  • Continue until the room is eliminated

Coach Notes

Keep it controlled. Fast pace, quick rotations. This ramps intensity quickly.
