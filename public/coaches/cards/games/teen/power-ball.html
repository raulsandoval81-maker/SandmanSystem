Power Ball

Game

Goal

Team competition, movement, pressure, and controlled chaos.

Setup

  • Split into two teams
  • Place a ball or object in the center
  • Define a scoring zone on each side

Play

  • On the whistle, teams fight for control of the ball
  • Score by carrying or moving the ball into your zone
  • Wrestling is allowed within coach control rules

Rules

  • No dangerous throws
  • Stay on feet if required
  • Limit contact intensity when needed

Coach Notes

This is controlled chaos. Set boundaries before starting. Emphasize awareness, teamwork, and safe pressure.
