Slap Back

Game

Goal

Hand speed, reaction, and defensive awareness.

Setup

  • Partners face each other in stance
  • Hands relaxed in ready position

Play

  • One athlete initiates a light slap attempt
  • The other athlete reacts and defends
  • Switch roles or go live reaction both ways

Coach Notes

Keep it controlled. This is reaction and timing, not striking. Eyes up, quick hands, controlled reaction.
