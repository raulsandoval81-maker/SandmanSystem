Skill 13 — Counter Finishing

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Use defensive reactions to create finishing opportunities

B — Counter with accuracy, timing, and controlled pressure

C — Continue offense only while structure stays clean

Coaching Cues

  • “Make them miss, make them pay.”
  • “Counter with control.”
  • “See the finish.”
  • “Stay balanced after.”

Common Mistakes

  • Countering too wide
  • Forcing finish after one shot
  • Ignoring defensive recovery
  • Throwing before reading the opening

Drill Ideas

  • Counter-finish mitt rounds
  • Slip-counter-pressure drills
  • Reaction finish sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Defensive Composure
  • Connect to Opponent Breaking
