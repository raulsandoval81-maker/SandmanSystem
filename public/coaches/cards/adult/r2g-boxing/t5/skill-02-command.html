Skill 02 — Command

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control pace, range, and emotional tone of the round

B — Force opponent reactions through pressure and positioning

C — Stay disciplined while leading every exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “Command the round.”
  • “Lead the exchange.”
  • “Make them react.”
  • “Stay disciplined.”

Common Mistakes

  • Confusing command with recklessness
  • Chasing instead of controlling
  • Losing patience under resistance
  • Forcing offense without setup

Drill Ideas

  • Command sparring rounds
  • Pressure-control drills
  • Round leadership scenarios

Progression

  • Connect to Pressure Mastery
  • Connect to Round Command
