Skill 30 — Legacy Standard

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Demonstrate complete R2G boxing maturity and discipline

B — Model command, composure, safety, pressure, and system control

C — Represent the standard others can follow

Coaching Cues

  • “Be the standard.”
  • “Legacy is how you move.”
  • “Command with discipline.”
  • “Make the room better.”

Common Mistakes

  • Chasing status instead of standard
  • Letting ego replace discipline
  • Forgetting partner safety
  • Performing only when watched

Drill Ideas

  • Legacy evaluation rounds
  • Leadership live rounds
  • Full-system standard test

Progression

  • Connect to Adult R2G mastery
  • Connect to coaching/leadership responsibility
