Skill 10 — Transition Mastery

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Move smoothly between offense, defense, pressure, and exits

B — Turn defensive moments into offensive control

C — Maintain composure and structure through every transition

Coaching Cues

  • “Flow through phases.”
  • “Defense becomes offense.”
  • “No dead space.”
  • “Transition with control.”

Common Mistakes

  • Pausing between phases
  • Losing stance during transitions
  • Rushing offense after defense
  • Failing to reset structure

Drill Ideas

  • Defense-to-offense live rounds
  • Transition mitt circuits
  • Phase-change sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Pocket Command
  • Connect to System Application
