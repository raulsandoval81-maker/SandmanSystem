Skill 01 — Presence

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Enter rounds with calm authority and controlled body language

B — Use stance, guard, and movement to project control

C — Maintain composure regardless of pressure or chaos

Coaching Cues

  • “Look in control.”
  • “Calm wins.”
  • “Own the room.”
  • “Presence before punches.”

Common Mistakes

  • Showing panic under pressure
  • Rushing to prove dominance
  • Losing posture after exchanges
  • Letting emotion control tempo

Drill Ideas

  • Composure entry rounds
  • Silent command sparring
  • Pressure posture drills

Progression

  • Connect to Command
  • Connect to Fight Command
