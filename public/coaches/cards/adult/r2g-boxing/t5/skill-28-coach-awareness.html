Skill 28 — Coach Awareness

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Understand coach cues, room standards, and partner safety live

B — Adjust quickly when given correction or tactical direction

C — Show leadership by modeling clean system behavior

Coaching Cues

  • “Hear the corner.”
  • “Adjust immediately.”
  • “Model the standard.”
  • “Lead through discipline.”

Common Mistakes

  • Ignoring coach correction during live work
  • Letting ego override instruction
  • Failing to protect partner safety
  • Needing repeated reminders for the same issue

Drill Ideas

  • Coach-command sparring
  • Immediate-adjustment rounds
  • Leadership standard drills

Progression

  • Connect to Mastery Rounds
  • Connect to Legacy Standard
