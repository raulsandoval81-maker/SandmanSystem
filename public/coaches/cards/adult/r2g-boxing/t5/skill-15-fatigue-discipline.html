Skill 15 — Fatigue Discipline

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Preserve boxing structure when exhausted

B — Keep decision-making clean during late-round pressure

C — Maintain composure, guard, breathing, and footwork under fatigue

Coaching Cues

  • “Discipline tired.”
  • “Structure when exhausted.”
  • “Breathe and box.”
  • “No sloppy survival.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping hands late
  • Throwing wild tired punches
  • Standing flat-footed
  • Making emotional decisions

Drill Ideas

  • Late-round discipline sparring
  • Fatigue decision rounds
  • Conditioning-to-boxing circuits

Progression

  • Connect to Weapon Adaptation
  • Connect to High Chaos Rounds
