Skill 20 — Fight Command

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control the full fight picture: pace, range, pressure, and reactions

B — Make composed tactical decisions across changing live situations

C — Lead exchanges without losing discipline or safety

Coaching Cues

  • “Command the fight.”
  • “See the whole picture.”
  • “Control every layer.”
  • “Stay disciplined in charge.”

Common Mistakes

  • Trying to dominate through emotion
  • Ignoring opponent adjustments
  • Forcing the same plan too long
  • Losing composure while leading

Drill Ideas

  • Fight-command sparring
  • Multi-round tactical scenarios
  • Coach-scored command rounds
