Skill 17 — Opponent Breaking

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize when opponent confidence, posture, or pace weakens

B — Apply disciplined pressure to deepen the break

C — Finish control without losing composure or defense

Coaching Cues

  • “Break them with discipline.”
  • “Stay composed while they fade.”
  • “Pressure smart.”
  • “No reckless chasing.”

Common Mistakes

  • Getting reckless when opponent fades
  • Burning energy chasing the finish
  • Ignoring defensive recovery
  • Missing the moment to increase pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Opponent-breaking scenarios
  • Late-round pressure rounds
  • Composed finish sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Live Leadership
  • Connect to Fight Command
