Skill 09 — Chain Dominance

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Link pressure, punches, angles, and resets into one flow

B — Continue attacking through opponent reactions and escapes

C — Maintain control across multiple connected exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Chain everything.”
  • “Don’t stop after one answer.”
  • “Keep control moving.”
  • “Layer the dominance.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after one scoring moment
  • Forcing pressure without balance
  • Breaking defense during chains
  • Failing to reset between layers

Drill Ideas

  • Chain dominance sparring
  • Multi-exchange mitt rounds
  • Pressure reset circuits

Progression

  • Connect to Transition Mastery
  • Connect to Finish Discipline
