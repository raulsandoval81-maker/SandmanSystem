Skill 23 — Pace Manipulation

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Change speed, rhythm, and pressure to control opponent timing

B — Break rhythm at key moments to create clean openings

C — Maintain composure and structure through tempo shifts

Coaching Cues

  • “Manipulate the pace.”
  • “Change gears with purpose.”
  • “Break the rhythm.”
  • “Don’t rush the shift.”

Common Mistakes

  • Changing pace without a reason
  • Fighting at one speed
  • Rushing after tempo breaks
  • Losing balance during speed changes

Drill Ideas

  • Fast-slow-fast sparring
  • Tempo manipulation mitt rounds
  • Pace-control live rounds

Progression

  • Connect to System Application
  • Connect to High Chaos Rounds
