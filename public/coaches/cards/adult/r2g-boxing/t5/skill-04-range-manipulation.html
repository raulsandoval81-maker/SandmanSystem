Skill 04 — Range Manipulation

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Change distance to create advantages before exchanges

B — Pull opponents into bad range or deny their preferred range

C — Use footwork, jab, and angles to control distance dynamically

Coaching Cues

  • “Control the distance.”
  • “Make them reach.”
  • “Enter and exit clean.”
  • “Range creates weapons.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing at one fixed range
  • Backing straight up
  • Entering without setup
  • Ignoring angle exits

Drill Ideas

  • Range-control sparring
  • Pull-counter distance drills
  • Jab and angle rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Fight Tempo
  • Connect to Ring Command
