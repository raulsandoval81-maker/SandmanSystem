Skill 03 — Pressure Mastery

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply pressure with timing, balance, and purpose

B — Layer feints, footwork, and offense without overcommitting

C — Break resistance while staying defensively responsible

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure with mastery.”
  • “Cut, don’t chase.”
  • “Layer the pressure.”
  • “Stay sharp forward.”

Common Mistakes

  • Walking into counters
  • Smothering offensive work
  • Burning energy too early
  • Dropping defense while advancing

Drill Ideas

  • Master pressure sparring
  • Cutoff and trap rounds
  • Forward composure circuits

Progression

  • Connect to Range Manipulation
  • Connect to Pressure Layering
