Skill 25 — High Chaos Rounds

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Perform under unpredictable, high-pressure live exchanges

B — Preserve stance, guard, breathing, and decision-making

C — Rebuild control immediately after mistakes or momentum shifts

Coaching Cues

  • “Calm in high chaos.”
  • “Recover the system.”
  • “No panic boxing.”
  • “Control what changes.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing wild punches under chaos
  • Freezing after mistakes
  • Losing defensive shape
  • Letting pressure erase decision-making

Drill Ideas

  • High-chaos sparring rounds
  • Random scenario live rounds
  • Mistake-recovery pressure drills

Progression

  • Connect to Instinct Refinement
  • Connect to Mastery Rounds
