Skill 12 — Finish Discipline

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize finishing moments without rushing emotionally

B — Apply controlled pressure with defensive responsibility

C — Finish exchanges with composure, structure, and awareness

Coaching Cues

  • “Finish disciplined.”
  • “Don’t chase wild.”
  • “Stay sharp finishing.”
  • “Control the close.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overcommitting after hurting someone
  • Dropping defense during pressure
  • Throwing wild combinations
  • Burning energy chasing finishes

Drill Ideas

  • Controlled finish scenarios
  • Composed pressure rounds
  • Finish-with-defense mitt drills

Progression

  • Connect to Counter Finishing
  • Connect to Opponent Breaking
