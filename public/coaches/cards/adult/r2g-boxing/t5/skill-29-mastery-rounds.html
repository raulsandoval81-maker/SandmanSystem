Skill 29 — Mastery Rounds

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Demonstrate full R2G boxing control across live rounds

B — Maintain presence, command, pressure, defense, and awareness

C — Show consistency without coach dependency or emotional drift

Coaching Cues

  • “Mastery is consistency.”
  • “Run the system.”
  • “Stay composed live.”
  • “Lead every round.”

Common Mistakes

  • Showing flashes instead of consistency
  • Forgetting system layers under pressure
  • Needing coach direction to reset
  • Letting emotion override command

Drill Ideas

  • Full mastery live rounds
  • Coach-silent evaluation sparring
  • System standard review rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Legacy Standard
  • Connect to Adult R2G completion
