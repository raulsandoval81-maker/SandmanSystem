Skill 05 — Fight Tempo

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Dictate speed, rhythm, and activity level of exchanges

B — Change pace to disrupt opponent timing

C — Maintain composure while shifting tempo under pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the tempo.”
  • “Change gears.”
  • “Speed up with purpose.”
  • “Slow them down.”

Common Mistakes

  • Fighting at one speed
  • Rushing without setup
  • Letting opponent dictate pace
  • Breaking structure during tempo changes

Drill Ideas

  • Tempo-change sparring
  • Fast-slow mitt rounds
  • Pace command live rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Chaos Control
  • Connect to Pace Manipulation
