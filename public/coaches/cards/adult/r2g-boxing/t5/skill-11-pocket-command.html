Skill 11 — Pocket Command

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control close-range exchanges with balance and awareness

B — Use short offense, defense, and positioning together

C — Exit, reset, or continue pressure with command

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the pocket.”
  • “Stay compact.”
  • “Work and protect.”
  • “Command close range.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing square inside
  • Throwing blind punches
  • Freezing under close pressure
  • Failing to exit after exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Pocket command sparring
  • Short-range reaction drills
  • Inside offense-defense rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Finish Discipline
  • Connect to Counter Finishing
