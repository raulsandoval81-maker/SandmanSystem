Skill 06 — Chaos Control

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Stay composed during unpredictable high-pressure exchanges

B — Rebuild stance, guard, and tactics immediately after disruption

C — Turn chaotic moments into controlled scoring opportunities

Coaching Cues

  • “Control the chaos.”
  • “Recover structure.”
  • “Don’t panic punch.”
  • “Find the answer.”

Common Mistakes

  • Freezing after mistakes
  • Throwing wild reactions
  • Losing defensive shape
  • Letting chaos control decisions

Drill Ideas

  • Random chaos rounds
  • Pressure reset drills
  • Unpredictable live scenarios

Progression

  • Connect to Ring Command
