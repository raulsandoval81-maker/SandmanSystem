Skill 16 — Weapon Adaptation

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Adjust punch selection based on opponent reactions

B — Shift weapons as range, pace, and pressure change

C — Blend tools without losing stance, guard, or intent

Coaching Cues

  • “Choose the right weapon.”
  • “Adapt the attack.”
  • “Read then fire.”
  • “Flow between tools.”

Common Mistakes

  • Repeating one favorite punch
  • Ignoring opponent adjustments
  • Forcing power shots
  • Breaking balance during weapon changes

Drill Ideas

  • Coach-call weapon rounds
  • Adaptive mitt work
  • Reaction-based sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Opponent Breaking
  • Connect to Pace Manipulation
