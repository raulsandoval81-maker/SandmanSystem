Skill 08 — Inside Mastery

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control posture, balance, and punch selection inside

B — Blend compact offense with defensive responsibility

C — Exit, reset, or continue pressure from close range

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the inside.”
  • “Short shots win.”
  • “Protect while working.”
  • “Stay compact.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing wide punches inside
  • Standing tall during close exchanges
  • Ignoring defensive responsibility
  • Smothering offense without control

Drill Ideas

  • Inside mastery rounds
  • Compact punch circuits
  • Close-range command sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Chain Dominance
  • Connect to Pocket Command
