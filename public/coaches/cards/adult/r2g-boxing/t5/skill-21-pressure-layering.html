Skill 21 — Pressure Layering

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Stack pressure through presence, footwork, feints, and offense

B — Force repeated reactions without rushing or losing structure

C — Control the opponent’s exits, rhythm, and confidence

Coaching Cues

  • “Layer the pressure.”
  • “Make them react again.”
  • “Stay patient forward.”
  • “Break space with control.”

Common Mistakes

  • Pressuring in one predictable rhythm
  • Chasing instead of cutting off
  • Dropping defense while advancing
  • Overforcing exchanges emotionally

Drill Ideas

  • Layered pressure rounds
  • Feint-pressure-control drills
  • Exit-denial sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Reaction Control
  • Connect to Pace Manipulation
