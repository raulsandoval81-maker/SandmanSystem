Skill 27 — Fight Awareness

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Track range, position, pace, pressure, and opponent condition

B — Recognize when to attack, defend, reset, or command space

C — Stay aware across full rounds without losing composure

Coaching Cues

  • “See the whole fight.”
  • “Know where you are.”
  • “Read the moment.”
  • “Stay present.”

Common Mistakes

  • Losing track of ropes and corners
  • Ignoring opponent fatigue or reactions
  • Fighting only from emotion
  • Failing to adjust during momentum swings

Drill Ideas

  • Awareness-command rounds
  • Scenario recognition drills
  • Coach-question live rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Coach Awareness
  • Connect to Mastery Rounds
