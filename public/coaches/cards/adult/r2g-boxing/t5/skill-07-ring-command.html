Skill 07 — Ring Command

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control ring position through footwork, pressure, and exits

B — Dictate where exchanges happen

C — Use ropes, corners, and center space intelligently

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the ring.”
  • “Choose the space.”
  • “Don’t be moved.”
  • “Control position first.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing into ropes without purpose
  • Losing center control
  • Chasing instead of cutting off
  • Ignoring corner opportunities

Drill Ideas

  • Ring command sparring
  • Center-control rounds
  • Corner and rope position drills

Progression

  • Connect to Inside Mastery
  • Connect to Round Command
