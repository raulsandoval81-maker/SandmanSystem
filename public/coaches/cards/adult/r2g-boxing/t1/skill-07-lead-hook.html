Skill 07 — Lead Hook

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Lead elbow rises level with the punch path

B — Rotate hips and lead foot through the hook

C — Return immediately to stance and guard

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn the hip.”
  • “Short and tight.”
  • “Hook around, not across.”
  • “Recover fast.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing the hook too wide
  • Dropping the opposite hand
  • Standing flat-footed
  • Overrotating past balance

Drill Ideas

  • Lead hook mitt repetitions
  • Hook-only heavy bag rounds
  • Jab-hook shadowboxing

Progression

  • Connect to Rear Hook
  • Connect to Basic Combo
