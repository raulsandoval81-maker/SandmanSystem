Skill 18 — Bag Work

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Strike with proper balance and positioning

B — Maintain movement and guard during rounds

C — Focus on clean technique over raw power

Coaching Cues

  • “Sharp shots.”
  • “Move your feet.”
  • “Don’t admire punches.”
  • “Work with purpose.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing flat in front of the bag
  • Throwing wild power shots
  • Dropping hands after combinations
  • Ignoring footwork and movement

Drill Ideas

  • Timed combo rounds
  • Movement and jab rounds
  • Defense-to-offense bag drills

Progression

  • Connect to Partner Drill
  • Connect to Boxing Standard
