Skill 05 — Cross

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Rear hand drives straight from the guard

B — Rotate hips and shoulders through the strike

C — Return to balanced stance and guard after contact

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn the hip.”
  • “Rear shoulder through.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Bring it home.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overcommitting weight forward
  • Dropping the lead hand
  • Throwing wide instead of straight
  • Failing to reset stance

Drill Ideas

  • Cross mechanics shadowboxing
  • Jab-cross mitt rounds
  • Heavy bag cross rounds

Progression

  • Connect to One Two
  • Connect to Basic Combo
