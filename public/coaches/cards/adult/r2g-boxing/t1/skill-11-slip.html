Skill 11 — Slip

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Move the head slightly outside the punch line

B — Keep eyes on the opponent while slipping

C — Return balanced and ready to counter

Coaching Cues

  • “Small movement.”
  • “Move the head, not the feet.”
  • “Eyes forward.”
  • “Slip and answer.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overleaning out of position
  • Dropping the hands
  • Closing the eyes during defense
  • Standing tall while slipping

Drill Ideas

  • Slip line drill
  • Partner jab slip drill
  • Shadowboxing defensive rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Roll
  • Connect to Countering
