Skill 04 — Jab

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Lead hand fires straight from the guard

B — Rotate shoulder to protect the chin during extension

C — Snap the jab back to guard quickly

Coaching Cues

  • “Straight out, straight back.”
  • “Snap it.”
  • “Shoulder protects chin.”
  • “Fast recovery.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping the rear hand
  • Pushing instead of snapping
  • Leaning too far forward
  • Failing to recover guard

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-only shadowboxing
  • Focus mitt jab rounds
  • Distance jab drill

Progression

  • Connect to Cross
  • Connect to One Two
