Skill 02 — Guard

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Hands high with elbows protecting the body

B — Rear hand protects chin while lead hand controls range

C — Recover guard immediately after every exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “Hands home fast.”
  • “Protect the chin.”
  • “Elbows tight.”
  • “See through the gloves.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping hands after punching
  • Wide elbows exposing the body
  • Looking down while defending
  • Overreaching with the lead hand

Drill Ideas

  • Guard hold rounds
  • Touch defense drills
  • Shadowboxing with guard recovery

Progression

  • Connect to Jab
  • Connect to Defensive Reactions
