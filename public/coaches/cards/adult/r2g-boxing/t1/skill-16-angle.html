Skill 16 — Angle

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Step off the center line after striking or defending

B — Maintain stance and balance during directional changes

C — Reposition to safer attack lanes and exits

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t stay in front.”
  • “Step and turn.”
  • “Move after punching.”
  • “Create new lanes.”

Common Mistakes

  • Crossing the feet while moving
  • Standing still after combinations
  • Turning the back during exits
  • Losing stance while circling

Drill Ideas

  • Pivot step drills
  • Angle-out shadowboxing
  • Mitt work with exits

Progression

  • Connect to Basic Combo
  • Connect to Countering
