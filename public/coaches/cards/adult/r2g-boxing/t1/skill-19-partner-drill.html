Skill 19 — Partner Drill

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Work controlled offense and defense with a partner

B — Maintain composure, balance, and communication

C — Practice timing, range, and clean execution safely

Coaching Cues

  • “Controlled work.”
  • “Take care of your partner.”
  • “Stay technical.”
  • “Good pace, good balance.”

Common Mistakes

  • Going too hard too early
  • Ignoring defense during drills
  • Standing still during exchanges
  • Losing control of spacing

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-only partner rounds
  • Defense-reaction drills
  • Combo call-and-response drills

Progression

  • Connect to Boxing Standard
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
