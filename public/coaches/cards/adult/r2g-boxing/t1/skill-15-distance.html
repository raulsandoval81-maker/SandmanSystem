Skill 15 — Distance

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Understand safe punching and defensive range

B — Use footwork to enter and exit cleanly

C — Maintain balance while adjusting range

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the range.”
  • “Don’t crowd yourself.”
  • “Punch from balance.”
  • “In and out.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing too close without defense
  • Reaching from too far away
  • Crossing feet while adjusting range
  • Backing straight up repeatedly

Drill Ideas

  • Range touch drills
  • Jab distance rounds
  • Movement and entry shadowboxing

Progression

  • Connect to Angle
  • Connect to Basic Combo
