Skill 13 — Parry

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Redirect the incoming punch with a small hand motion

B — Keep the opposite hand protecting the chin

C — Return immediately to guard or counter position

Coaching Cues

  • “Small touch.”
  • “Don’t reach.”
  • “Stay protected.”
  • “Parry and answer.”

Common Mistakes

  • Swatting too wide
  • Dropping the opposite hand
  • Overextending the arm
  • Standing flat-footed after defense

Drill Ideas

  • Jab parry partner drill
  • Parry-counter mitt rounds
  • Reaction hand drills

Progression

  • Connect to Block
  • Connect to Countering
