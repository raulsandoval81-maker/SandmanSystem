Skill 08 — Rear Hook

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Rear side loads through hip and shoulder rotation

B — Punch travels tight across the target line

C — Recover rear hand back to guard immediately

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn and fire.”
  • “Stay compact.”
  • “Rear shoulder through.”
  • “Finish balanced.”

Common Mistakes

  • Looping the punch too wide
  • Leaning excessively forward
  • Dropping the lead hand
  • Overturning the hips

Drill Ideas

  • Rear hook pad rounds
  • Cross-hook combination work
  • Heavy bag hook power rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Uppercut
  • Connect to Basic Combo
