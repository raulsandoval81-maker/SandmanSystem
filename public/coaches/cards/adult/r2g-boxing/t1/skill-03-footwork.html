Skill 03 — Footwork

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Step without crossing the feet

B — Maintain stance while moving forward, backward, and laterally

C — Move with balance before and after striking

Coaching Cues

  • “Feet under you.”
  • “Don’t click the heels.”
  • “Move smooth.”
  • “Stay ready to punch.”

Common Mistakes

  • Crossing the feet
  • Standing upright while moving
  • Taking steps too large
  • Losing stance during movement

Drill Ideas

  • Line movement drills
  • Ring circle footwork
  • Shadowboxing with movement only

Progression

  • Connect to Distance
  • Connect to Angle
