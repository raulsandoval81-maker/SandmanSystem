Skill 10 — Body Shot

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Lower level without collapsing posture

B — Rotate hips through the body target

C — Exit safely back to stance and guard

Coaching Cues

  • “Bend the legs, not the waist.”
  • “Dig through the target.”
  • “Protect the chin.”
  • “Punch and recover.”

Common Mistakes

  • Reaching too low with posture broken
  • Dropping both hands during entry
  • Standing still after the punch
  • Overcommitting weight forward

Drill Ideas

  • Body shot mitt drills
  • Body-head combination rounds
  • Heavy bag body attack rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Angle
  • Connect to Basic Combo
