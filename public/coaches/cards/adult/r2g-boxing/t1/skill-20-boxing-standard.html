Skill 20 — Boxing Standard

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Demonstrate stance, guard, movement, and basic punches correctly

B — Show control, composure, and safe partner interaction

C — Maintain effort and discipline through full rounds

Coaching Cues

  • “Sharp and controlled.”
  • “Protect yourself at all times.”
  • “Move with purpose.”
  • “Stay composed under work.”

Common Mistakes

  • Losing structure under fatigue
  • Dropping guard during exchanges
  • Standing square while moving
  • Rushing combinations without control

Drill Ideas

  • Full-round technical circuits
  • Coach evaluation rounds
  • Partner technical review drills

Progression

  • Connect to Adult R2G T2
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
