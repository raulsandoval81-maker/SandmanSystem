Skill 06 — One Two

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Lead jab establishes range and vision

B — Rear cross follows directly behind the jab

C — Reset stance and guard after the combination

Coaching Cues

  • “Jab opens, cross lands.”
  • “Straight line punches.”
  • “Stay balanced through both shots.”
  • “Hands recover fast.”

Common Mistakes

  • Pausing between punches
  • Dropping hands during the combo
  • Overreaching the cross
  • Standing square after punching

Drill Ideas

  • One-two shadowboxing rounds
  • Focus mitt one-two repetitions
  • Heavy bag one-two movement drill

Progression

  • Connect to Lead Hook
  • Connect to Basic Combo
