Skill 12 — Roll

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Bend knees and lower level under the punch

B — Roll underneath while maintaining balance and vision

C — Rise back into stance prepared to counter

Coaching Cues

  • “Bend the legs.”
  • “Stay compact.”
  • “Eyes on target.”
  • “Come up ready.”

Common Mistakes

  • Bending at the waist only
  • Dropping the eyes downward
  • Rolling too wide
  • Losing stance after movement

Drill Ideas

  • Slip rope rolling drill
  • Hook defense partner drill
  • Roll-counter shadowboxing

Progression

  • Connect to Parry
  • Connect to Countering
