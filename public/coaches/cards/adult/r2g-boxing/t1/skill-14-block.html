Skill 14 — Block

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Keep guard compact and elbows controlled

B — Absorb punches with gloves and forearms

C — Stay balanced and ready to return fire

Coaching Cues

  • “Strong frame.”
  • “Elbows tight.”
  • “Don’t panic.”
  • “Defend and respond.”

Common Mistakes

  • Opening the guard under pressure
  • Reaching instead of covering
  • Standing rigid without movement
  • Closing the eyes during impact

Drill Ideas

  • High guard defense rounds
  • Body block partner drills
  • Coach touch reaction drills

Progression

  • Connect to Distance
  • Connect to Defensive Reactions
