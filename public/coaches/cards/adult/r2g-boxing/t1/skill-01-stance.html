Skill 01 — Stance

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Balanced boxing stance with knees bent and chin protected

B — Lead foot forward, rear heel light, hands ready to strike and defend

C — Return to stance after movement, punches, or defense

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Hands home.”
  • “Chin down, eyes up.”
  • “Ready to move.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing too tall
  • Feet too narrow or too wide
  • Hands dropping after punches
  • Weight leaning too far forward

Drill Ideas

  • Mirror stance checks
  • Coach movement calls
  • Shadowboxing with stance resets

Progression

  • Connect to Guard
  • Connect to Footwork
