Skill 09 — Uppercut

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Bend knees slightly to load the punch

B — Drive upward with hips and shoulder rotation

C — Recover guard and stance after the strike

Coaching Cues

  • “Drive from the legs.”
  • “Short path upward.”
  • “Stay tight.”
  • “Eyes up.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dipping too low before punching
  • Swinging the punch wide
  • Dropping the opposite hand
  • Losing balance upward

Drill Ideas

  • Uppercut mitt work
  • Body-head uppercut drills
  • Heavy bag close-range rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Body Shot
  • Connect to Basic Combo
