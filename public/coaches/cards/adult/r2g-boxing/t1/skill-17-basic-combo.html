Skill 17 — Basic Combo

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Chain punches together with balance and rhythm

B — Maintain guard during transitions between strikes

C — Finish combinations with movement or defense

Coaching Cues

  • “Punches flow together.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Hands recover between shots.”
  • “Move when finished.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing every punch full power
  • Pausing between strikes
  • Standing square after combinations
  • Dropping hands while punching

Drill Ideas

  • Three-punch shadowboxing rounds
  • Mitt combination progressions
  • Heavy bag combo rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Bag Work
  • Connect to Partner Drill
