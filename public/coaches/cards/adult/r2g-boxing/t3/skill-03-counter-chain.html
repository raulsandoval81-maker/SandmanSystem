Skill 03 — Counter Chain

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Defend initial offense with vision and balance

B — Chain multiple counters together during openings

C — Recover positioning while continuing tactical pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Counter in layers.”
  • “Stay sharp.”
  • “Defend then answer.”
  • “Flow into the next shot.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing counters too wide
  • Pausing after first return shot
  • Ignoring defensive recovery
  • Overcommitting emotionally

Drill Ideas

  • Multi-counter mitt drills
  • Reaction chain sparring
  • Defense-to-offense circuits

Progression

  • Connect to Reaction Training
  • Connect to Counter Punching
