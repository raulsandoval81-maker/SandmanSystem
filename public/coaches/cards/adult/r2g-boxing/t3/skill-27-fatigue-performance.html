Skill 27 — Fatigue Performance

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Preserve boxing structure while tired

B — Keep footwork, guard, breathing, and decision-making active

C — Continue performing without panic or sloppy overcommitment

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay sharp tired.”
  • “Breathe and box.”
  • “Structure under fatigue.”
  • “Don’t get sloppy.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping hands when tired
  • Standing flat-footed late in rounds
  • Throwing wild punches to survive
  • Forgetting breathing and movement

Drill Ideas

  • Fatigue live rounds
  • Bag-to-spar transition rounds
  • Late-round technical finish drills

Progression

  • Connect to Fight Awareness
  • Connect to Chaos Drills
