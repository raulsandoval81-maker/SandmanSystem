Skill 18 — Angle Traps

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Use footwork and positioning to limit opponent exits

B — Force movement into predictable attack lanes

C — Score safely while maintaining angle control

Coaching Cues

  • “Cut the lane off.”
  • “Trap with the feet.”
  • “Guide the movement.”
  • “Don’t chase.”

Common Mistakes

  • Crossing feet while trapping angles
  • Overcommitting into counters
  • Standing square during pressure
  • Ignoring exits after scoring

Drill Ideas

  • Angle trap sparring drills
  • Corner-cutting movement rounds
  • Mitt work with pivot traps

Progression

  • Connect to Rope Escapes
  • Connect to Counter Punching
