Skill 01 — Ringcraft

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control positioning through movement, angles, and spacing

B — Guide exchanges toward favorable ring locations

C — Maintain tactical awareness while managing pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the space.”
  • “Guide the exchange.”
  • “Don’t get trapped.”
  • “Move with purpose.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing to ropes unnecessarily
  • Standing flat during pressure
  • Ignoring angle opportunities
  • Moving without tactical intent

Drill Ideas

  • Center-control rounds
  • Ring movement sparring
  • Angle management drills

Progression

  • Connect to Pressure Flow
  • Connect to Corner Pressure
