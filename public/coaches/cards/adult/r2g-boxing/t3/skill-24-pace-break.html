Skill 24 — Pace Break

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Interrupt opponent rhythm through timing and movement shifts

B — Change speed and pressure unexpectedly during exchanges

C — Recover structure immediately after tempo disruption

Coaching Cues

  • “Break the rhythm.”
  • “Change gears suddenly.”
  • “Disrupt then score.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Changing pace without purpose
  • Rushing emotionally after tempo shifts
  • Stopping movement after attacks
  • Becoming predictable during resets

Drill Ideas

  • Tempo disruption sparring
  • Fast-slow mitt rounds
  • Rhythm break shadowboxing

Progression

  • Connect to Weapon Selection
  • Connect to Live Rounds
