Skill 20 — Counter Punching

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Read offensive openings during opponent attacks

B — Deliver accurate counters with timing and balance

C — Recover defensively after exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Make them miss first.”
  • “Short reactions score.”
  • “Counter with balance.”
  • “See before firing.”

Common Mistakes

  • Countering too early
  • Throwing wide return shots
  • Ignoring defense after counters
  • Overcommitting emotionally

Drill Ideas

  • Slip-counter rounds
  • Reaction sparring drills
  • Counter-only mitt sessions

Progression

  • Connect to Pocket Awareness
  • Connect to Defensive Layers
