Skill 12 — Rear Hook System

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build rear hook entries off cross, roll, or close-range pressure

B — Rotate through the rear hip while staying compact

C — Recover safely into defense, angle, or next punch

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn through the shot.”
  • “Stay tight.”
  • “Hook after the setup.”
  • “Recover your guard.”

Common Mistakes

  • Looping the rear hook too wide
  • Overrotating past balance
  • Dropping the lead hand
  • Throwing without position

Drill Ideas

  • Cross-to-rear-hook mitt drills
  • Roll-rear-hook counter rounds
  • Close-range hook flow drills

Progression

  • Connect to Body Attack
  • Connect to Combination Defense
