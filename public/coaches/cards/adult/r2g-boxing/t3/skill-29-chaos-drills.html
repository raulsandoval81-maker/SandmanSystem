Skill 29 — Chaos Drills

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Respond to unpredictable boxing situations with control

B — Maintain stance, guard, and awareness during rapid changes

C — Recover structure after mistakes, pressure, or surprise attacks

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay calm in chaos.”
  • “Recover fast.”
  • “Structure first.”
  • “Solve the moment.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking under unpredictable pressure
  • Throwing wild punches without control
  • Freezing after mistakes
  • Losing defensive shape

Drill Ideas

  • Random coach-call drills
  • Unpredictable partner reaction rounds
  • Pressure reset circuits

Progression

  • Connect to Second Effort
  • Connect to Adult R2G T4
