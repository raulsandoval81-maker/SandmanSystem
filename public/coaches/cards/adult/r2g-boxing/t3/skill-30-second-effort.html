Skill 30 — Second Effort

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Continue working after first exchange, mistake, or failed attack

B — Rebuild position and offense immediately after disruption

C — Finish rounds with effort, composure, and technical discipline

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t quit after one exchange.”
  • “Recover and go again.”
  • “Second effort wins rounds.”
  • “Finish composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after missed punches
  • Accepting bad position too long
  • Dropping effort late in rounds
  • Panicking after mistakes

Drill Ideas

  • Second-effort live rounds
  • Mistake-recovery drills
  • Late-round finish circuits

Progression

  • Connect to Adult R2G T4
  • Connect to Finish Standard
