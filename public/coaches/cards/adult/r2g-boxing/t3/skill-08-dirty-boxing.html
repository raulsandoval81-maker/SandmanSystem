Skill 08 — Dirty Boxing

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Work controlled short punches during close contact situations

B — Manage hand position and posture in tight exchanges

C — Maintain awareness while creating scoring opportunities

Coaching Cues

  • “Short and sharp.”
  • “Control the space.”
  • “Stay tight inside.”
  • “Protect while working.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing wide punches in close range
  • Losing posture during contact
  • Ignoring defensive hand position
  • Overcommitting emotionally

Drill Ideas

  • Short-range pad drills
  • Controlled contact rounds
  • Body-head inside exchanges

Progression

  • Connect to Advanced Jab
  • Connect to Pocket Awareness
