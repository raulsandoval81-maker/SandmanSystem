Skill 19 — Rope Escapes

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize danger positions along the ropes

B — Escape using pivots, angles, and defensive movement

C — Recover center positioning after the exit

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t stay trapped.”
  • “Angle out fast.”
  • “Move before pressure builds.”
  • “Recover center.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing straight into ropes
  • Freezing under pressure
  • Crossing feet during exits
  • Throwing wild punches while trapped

Drill Ideas

  • Rope escape sparring drills
  • Corner-exit movement rounds
  • Pressure-response partner drills

Progression

  • Connect to Counter Punching
  • Connect to Pocket Awareness
