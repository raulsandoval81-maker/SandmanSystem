Skill 28 — Fight Awareness

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Track position, pressure, opponent reactions, and round flow

B — Make clear decisions while under live boxing stress

C — Stay composed and tactical through changing situations

Coaching Cues

  • “Know where you are.”
  • “Read the round.”
  • “Think under pressure.”
  • “Stay present.”

Common Mistakes

  • Losing track of ropes and corners
  • Fighting emotionally instead of tactically
  • Ignoring opponent patterns
  • Failing to adjust during live exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Position-awareness sparring
  • Coach-command live rounds
  • Scenario-based boxing rounds
