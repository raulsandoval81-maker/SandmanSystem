Skill 14 — Head Movement

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Move the head off the center line before and after punches

B — Keep eyes forward and stance underneath the movement

C — Blend slips, rolls, and resets into offensive rhythm

Coaching Cues

  • “Head off line.”
  • “Small movements.”
  • “Eyes stay up.”
  • “Move then answer.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overleaning out of stance
  • Dropping hands while moving
  • Looking down during rolls
  • Moving head without foot balance

Drill Ideas

  • Slip rope rounds
  • Punch-slip-punch drills
  • Head movement shadowboxing

Progression

  • Connect to Combination Defense
  • Connect to Defensive Layers
