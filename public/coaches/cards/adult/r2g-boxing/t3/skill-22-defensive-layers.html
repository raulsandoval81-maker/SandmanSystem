Skill 22 — Defensive Layers

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Blend blocks, slips, rolls, parries, and footwork together

B — Defend continuously through multi-shot exchanges

C — Transition from defense into counters or exits

Coaching Cues

  • “Defense stacks together.”
  • “Keep moving.”
  • “Don’t stop after one reaction.”
  • “Defend then answer.”

Common Mistakes

  • Using only one defensive reaction repeatedly
  • Freezing under pressure
  • Dropping stance during defense
  • Failing to recover after exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Multi-defense partner drills
  • Continuous reaction rounds
  • Defense-chain sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Offensive Layers
  • Connect to Advanced Sparring
