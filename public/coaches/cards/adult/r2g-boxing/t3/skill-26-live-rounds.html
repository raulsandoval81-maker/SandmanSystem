Skill 26 — Live Rounds

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply boxing skills during full live round structure

B — Maintain composure, defense, and tactical awareness under pressure

C — Make adjustments between exchanges and across the round

Coaching Cues

  • “Box through the round.”
  • “Stay technical live.”
  • “Adjust as it changes.”
  • “Protect and score.”

Common Mistakes

  • Abandoning technique once the round goes live
  • Overthrowing punches emotionally
  • Forgetting defensive recovery
  • Failing to adjust after exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Technical live rounds
  • Situational live boxing rounds
  • Coach-scored application rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Fatigue Performance
  • Connect to Fight Awareness
