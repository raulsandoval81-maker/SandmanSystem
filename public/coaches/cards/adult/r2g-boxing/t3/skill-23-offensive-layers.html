Skill 23 — Offensive Layers

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Layer setups, combinations, pressure, and movement together

B — Attack from multiple rhythms and angles

C — Maintain defensive readiness while applying offense

Coaching Cues

  • “Build the attack.”
  • “Layer the pressure.”
  • “Stay balanced offensive.”
  • “Keep them reacting.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing predictable combinations
  • Forcing offense emotionally
  • Ignoring exits after attacks
  • Dropping defensive structure

Drill Ideas

  • Layered combo sparring
  • Multi-phase mitt drills
  • Pressure-to-angle circuits

Progression

  • Connect to Pace Break
  • Connect to Weapon Selection
