Skill 25 — Weapon Selection

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Identify the correct punch or tool for each situation

B — Choose attacks based on range, angle, and opponent reaction

C — Transition smoothly between offensive options during exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Use the right tool.”
  • “Read the opening.”
  • “Don’t force shots.”
  • “Choose with purpose.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing the same punches repeatedly
  • Ignoring distance and positioning
  • Forcing power shots unnecessarily
  • Failing to adapt during exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Situational punch selection rounds
  • Coach-call weapon drills
  • Reaction-based mitt work

Progression

  • Connect to Live Rounds
  • Connect to Fight Awareness
