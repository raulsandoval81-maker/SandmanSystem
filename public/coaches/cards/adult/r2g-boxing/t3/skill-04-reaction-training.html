Skill 04 — Reaction Training

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Identify attacks and openings instantly

B — Respond with efficient defensive or offensive actions

C — Maintain composure during rapid exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “See it fast.”
  • “Short reactions.”
  • “Stay calm.”
  • “React with purpose.”

Common Mistakes

  • Freezing under pressure
  • Overreacting emotionally
  • Closing eyes during exchanges
  • Breaking stance during reactions

Drill Ideas

  • Coach-call reactions
  • Randomized mitt drills
  • Fast-response sparring games

Progression

  • Connect to Setup System
  • Connect to Defensive Layers
