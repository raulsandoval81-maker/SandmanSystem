Skill 06 — Corner Pressure

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Cut exits and control opponent movement toward corners

B — Apply balanced offensive pressure without smothering

C — Maintain defensive awareness during close exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Cut the exits off.”
  • “Pressure with control.”
  • “Don’t rush the finish.”
  • “Stay balanced forward.”

Common Mistakes

  • Charging recklessly into counters
  • Standing square in front of the opponent
  • Throwing without setup
  • Losing defensive structure while attacking

Drill Ideas

  • Corner trap sparring drills
  • Pressure-entry mitt rounds
  • Controlled corner exchanges

Progression

  • Connect to Inside Boxing
  • Connect to Rope Escapes
