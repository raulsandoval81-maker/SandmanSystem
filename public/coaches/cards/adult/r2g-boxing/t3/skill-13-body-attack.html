Skill 13 — Body Attack

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Enter safely before attacking the body

B — Change levels with knees while protecting the chin

C — Exit or build back upstairs after body offense

Coaching Cues

  • “Bend the legs.”
  • “Body opens the head.”
  • “Protect on entry.”
  • “Dig and move.”

Common Mistakes

  • Bending at the waist
  • Reaching from too far away
  • Leaving the head exposed
  • Standing still after body shots

Drill Ideas

  • Body-head mitt combinations
  • Body attack bag rounds
  • Partner entry-to-body drills

Progression

  • Connect to Head Movement
  • Connect to Offensive Layers
