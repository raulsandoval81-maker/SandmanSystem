Skill 09 — Advanced Jab

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Use varied jab speeds, rhythms, and targets

B — Blend jabs into setups, counters, and movement

C — Maintain balance and defensive structure throughout

Coaching Cues

  • “The jab controls everything.”
  • “Change the look.”
  • “Touch, blind, score.”
  • “Keep the feet alive.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing every jab the same way
  • Overreaching and leaning
  • Dropping the rear hand
  • Stopping movement after jabbing

Drill Ideas

  • Multi-rhythm jab rounds
  • Feint-jab reaction drills
  • Advanced jab sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Advanced Cross
  • Connect to Offensive Layers
