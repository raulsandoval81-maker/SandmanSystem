Skill 10 — Advanced Cross

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Deliver the cross through setups, timing, and angle creation

B — Blend power with balance and tactical positioning

C — Recover immediately into movement or defensive layers

Coaching Cues

  • “Straight through the target.”
  • “Set it up first.”
  • “Stay balanced after impact.”
  • “Recover fast.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing naked crosses without setup
  • Overrotating and falling forward
  • Dropping the lead hand
  • Standing still after throwing

Drill Ideas

  • Cross-counter drills
  • Set-up mitt combinations
  • Angle-cross sparring rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Lead Hook System
  • Connect to Counter Punching
