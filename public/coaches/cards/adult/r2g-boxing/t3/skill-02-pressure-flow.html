Skill 02 — Pressure Flow

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply forward pressure with controlled footwork

B — Blend offense, defense, and positioning together

C — Sustain intelligent pressure without overcommitting

Coaching Cues

  • “Cut space away.”
  • “Stay balanced forward.”
  • “Flow through exchanges.”

Common Mistakes

  • Smothering combinations
  • Dropping defense during pressure
  • Walking into counters
