Skill 05 — Setup System

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build offensive opportunities through feints, rhythm, and movement

B — Draw reactions before committing attacks

C — Transition smoothly into scoring combinations

Coaching Cues

  • “Create the opening.”
  • “Don’t force shots.”
  • “Set traps first.”
  • “Think ahead.”

Common Mistakes

  • Attacking without preparation
  • Using predictable setups
  • Overfeinting without offense
  • Losing balance during entries

Drill Ideas

  • Feint-to-score rounds
  • Reaction setup sparring
  • Mitt trap drills

Progression

  • Connect to Corner Pressure
  • Connect to Offensive Layers
