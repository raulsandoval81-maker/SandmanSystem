Skill 16 — Tempo Control

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Change pace intentionally during exchanges

B — Use rhythm shifts to create offensive openings

C — Maintain composure while controlling round flow

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the pace.”
  • “Change the rhythm.”
  • “Don’t stay predictable.”
  • “Slow-fast-slow.”

Common Mistakes

  • Moving at one constant speed
  • Rushing combinations emotionally
  • Freezing between tempo changes
  • Forcing exchanges without setup

Drill Ideas

  • Slow-fast sparring rounds
  • Tempo mitt drills
  • Rhythm disruption shadowboxing

Progression

  • Connect to Counter Punching
  • Connect to Advanced Sparring
