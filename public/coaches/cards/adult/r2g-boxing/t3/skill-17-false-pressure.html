Skill 17 — False Pressure

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Show forward pressure without fully committing offense

B — Force reactions through positioning, feints, and footwork

C — Create openings from opponent overreactions

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure without rushing.”
  • “Make them react first.”
  • “Control the space.”
  • “Stay patient.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overcommitting into counters
  • Walking straight forward predictably
  • Showing pressure without setups
  • Standing tall during entries

Drill Ideas

  • Feint-pressure rounds
  • Footwork pressure drills
  • Reaction-based sparring entries

Progression

  • Connect to Angle Traps
  • Connect to Rope Escapes
