Skill 15 — Combination Defense

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Defend multiple punches without losing stance

B — Blend blocks, slips, rolls, and footwork together

C — Exit or counter after the defensive sequence

Coaching Cues

  • “Defend in layers.”
  • “Don’t freeze.”
  • “Hands and head work together.”
  • “Exit or answer.”

Common Mistakes

  • Shelling up without movement
  • Dropping stance during pressure
  • Only defending the first punch
  • Failing to exit after defense

Drill Ideas

  • Three-punch defense drills
  • Block-slip-roll partner rounds
  • Defense-to-counter mitt work

Progression

  • Connect to Tempo Control
  • Connect to Defensive Layers
