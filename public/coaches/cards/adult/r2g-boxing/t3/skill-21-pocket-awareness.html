Skill 21 — Pocket Awareness

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize safe and dangerous positions inside exchanges

B — Maintain balance and defensive readiness in the pocket

C — Exit, counter, or continue offense intelligently

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay aware inside.”
  • “Don’t get stuck.”
  • “Work and move.”
  • “Protect while attacking.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing square inside exchanges
  • Throwing blindly under pressure
  • Ignoring exits and angles
  • Freezing after combinations

Drill Ideas

  • Pocket sparring rounds
  • Inside reaction drills
  • Short-range combination circuits

Progression

  • Connect to Advanced Sparring
  • Connect to Ring Leadership
