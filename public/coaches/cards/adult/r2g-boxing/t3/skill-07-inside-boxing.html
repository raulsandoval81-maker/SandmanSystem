Skill 07 — Inside Boxing

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain balance and structure at close range

B — Work compact punches while controlling position

C — Defend and exit safely during tight exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay compact.”
  • “Short punches score.”
  • “Protect yourself inside.”
  • “Don’t smother your work.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing wide punches inside
  • Standing too tall in exchanges
  • Ignoring defensive positioning
  • Freezing under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Inside sparring rounds
  • Short-hook mitt drills
  • Body-shot exchange drills

Progression

  • Connect to Dirty Boxing
  • Connect to Pocket Awareness
