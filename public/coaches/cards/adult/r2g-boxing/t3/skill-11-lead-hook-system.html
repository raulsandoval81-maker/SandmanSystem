Skill 11 — Lead Hook System

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build the lead hook from jab, slip, roll, or angle entries

B — Use hip rotation and compact mechanics to score safely

C — Recover into guard, exit angle, or follow-up offense

Coaching Cues

  • “Set the hook up.”
  • “Short and sharp.”
  • “Turn the hip.”
  • “Don’t admire it.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing the hook naked without setup
  • Looping the punch too wide
  • Dropping the rear hand
  • Standing still after landing

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-to-lead-hook mitt rounds
  • Slip-hook counter drills
  • Angle-hook heavy bag rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Rear Hook System
  • Connect to Body Attack
