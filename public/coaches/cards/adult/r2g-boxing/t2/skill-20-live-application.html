Skill 20 — Live Application

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply technical skills during realistic live exchanges

B — Adapt under pressure while maintaining composure

C — Use movement, defense, and offense together fluidly

Coaching Cues

  • “Trust your training.”
  • “Stay technical live.”
  • “See and react.”
  • “Stay balanced.”

Common Mistakes

  • Abandoning structure during pressure
  • Overthrowing punches emotionally
  • Stopping movement during exchanges
  • Ignoring defensive recovery

Drill Ideas

  • Situational sparring rounds
  • Coach-directed live drills
  • Reaction-based partner rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Ring IQ
  • Connect to Finish Standard
