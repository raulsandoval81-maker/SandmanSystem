Skill 25 — Finish Standard

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Demonstrate technical consistency across full live rounds

B — Maintain composure, defense, and ring awareness under fatigue

C — Apply R2G T2 skills effectively in realistic boxing situations

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay technical tired.”
  • “Control the round.”
  • “Think while fighting.”
  • “Finish composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Losing structure under pressure
  • Overthrowing emotionally late in rounds
  • Ignoring defense during fatigue
  • Stopping movement after exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Coach-evaluation sparring
  • Fatigue pressure circuits
