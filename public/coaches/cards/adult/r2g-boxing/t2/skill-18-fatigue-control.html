Skill 18 — Fatigue Control

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain structure and breathing under fatigue

B — Continue moving and defending while tired

C — Stay mentally composed during hard rounds

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay sharp tired.”
  • “Breathe and move.”
  • “Keep the structure.”
  • “Don’t panic.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping hands under fatigue
  • Standing flat-footed while tired
  • Holding breath during exchanges
  • Throwing reckless punches

Drill Ideas

  • High-output bag rounds
  • Conditioning spar circuits
  • Fatigue shadowboxing drills

Progression

  • Connect to Round Management
  • Connect to Finish Standard
