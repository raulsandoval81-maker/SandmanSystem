Skill 19 — Round Management

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control pace and energy across full rounds

B — Make tactical adjustments during exchanges

C — Finish rounds with composure and awareness

Coaching Cues

  • “Think through the round.”
  • “Don’t waste energy.”
  • “Control the pace.”
  • “Stay aware.”

Common Mistakes

  • Starting too fast emotionally
  • Burning energy early
  • Forgetting defense late in rounds
  • Failing to adjust tactics

Drill Ideas

  • Paced sparring rounds
  • Situational round management drills
  • Controlled conditioning circuits
