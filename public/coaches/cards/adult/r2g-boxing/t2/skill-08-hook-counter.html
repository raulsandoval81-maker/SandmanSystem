Skill 08 — Hook Counter

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Defend or evade the incoming strike

B — Fire a compact hook into the opening created

C — Exit safely after the counter lands

Coaching Cues

  • “Tight and sharp.”
  • “Counter off defense.”
  • “Turn the hip.”
  • “Move after scoring.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing hooks too wide
  • Countering without vision
  • Standing square after punching
  • Dropping the opposite hand

Drill Ideas

  • Roll-hook drills
  • Partner hook-counter rounds
  • Mitt reaction combinations

Progression

  • Connect to Body Head Combo
  • Connect to Combination Flow
