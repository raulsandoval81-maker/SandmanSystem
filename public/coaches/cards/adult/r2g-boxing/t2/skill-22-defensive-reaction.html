Skill 22 — Defensive Reaction

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize incoming attacks quickly

B — Respond with appropriate defensive movement or coverage

C — Recover into offensive or neutral position immediately

Coaching Cues

  • “See and react.”
  • “Short movements.”
  • “Stay calm.”
  • “Defend first.”

Common Mistakes

  • Freezing during pressure
  • Overreacting defensively
  • Closing the eyes during exchanges
  • Dropping stance while defending

Drill Ideas

  • Reaction partner drills
  • Coach-call defense rounds
  • Slip-roll-parry circuits

Progression

  • Connect to Combination Pressure
  • Connect to Live Situations
