Skill 05 — Double Jab

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Fire the first jab to establish range or disrupt defense

B — Follow immediately with a second sharper jab

C — Maintain stance and movement through both shots

Coaching Cues

  • “Touch then score.”
  • “Second jab sharper.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Recover the hands fast.”

Common Mistakes

  • Pausing between jabs
  • Leaning too far forward
  • Dropping the rear hand
  • Standing still after entry

Drill Ideas

  • Double jab movement rounds
  • Mitt work with jab entries
  • Heavy bag double jab circuits

Progression

  • Connect to Jab Entry
  • Connect to Range Control
