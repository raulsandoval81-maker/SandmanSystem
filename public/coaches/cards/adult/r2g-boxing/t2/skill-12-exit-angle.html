Skill 12 — Exit Angle

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Finish exchanges with lateral movement

B — Step away from the opponent’s center line

C — Reset safely into stance and guard

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t leave straight back.”
  • “Exit with purpose.”
  • “Punch then move.”
  • “Stay ready after the exit.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing straight up repeatedly
  • Standing still after combinations
  • Crossing feet during exits
  • Dropping guard while moving

Drill Ideas

  • Angle-out mitt drills
  • Combo-and-exit partner drills
  • Shadowboxing exit rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Pressure Step
  • Connect to Ring Position
