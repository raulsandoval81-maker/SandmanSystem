Skill 02 — Rhythm

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control pace through movement and punch cadence

B — Break predictable attack patterns

C — Maintain composure while changing tempo

Coaching Cues

  • “Change the beat.”
  • “Don’t stay predictable.”
  • “Relax and flow.”
  • “Own the pace.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing every punch at one speed
  • Rushing combinations
  • Freezing between exchanges
  • Moving stiff and robotic

Drill Ideas

  • Tempo shadowboxing
  • Slow-fast mitt rounds
  • Rhythm interruption partner drills

Progression

  • Connect to Feint
  • Connect to Combination Flow
