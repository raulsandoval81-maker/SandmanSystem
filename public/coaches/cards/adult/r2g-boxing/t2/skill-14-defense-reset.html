Skill 14 — Defense Reset

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recover stance and guard immediately after exchanges

B — Reestablish defensive awareness under pressure

C — Prepare for the next offensive or defensive action

Coaching Cues

  • “Back to structure.”
  • “Hands home.”
  • “Reset your feet.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Leaving hands low after punching
  • Standing square after exchanges
  • Freezing after taking shots
  • Recovering too slowly

Drill Ideas

  • Combo-reset shadowboxing
  • Defense recovery mitt drills
  • Partner exchange-reset rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Combination Flow
  • Connect to Defensive Reaction
