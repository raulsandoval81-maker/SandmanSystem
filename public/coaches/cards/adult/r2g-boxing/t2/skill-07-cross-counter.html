Skill 07 — Cross Counter

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Defend the incoming jab or straight punch

B — Fire the rear cross immediately into the opening

C — Recover stance and defensive position after the exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “See it and answer.”
  • “Straight down the middle.”
  • “Short reaction.”
  • “Stay balanced.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing before defending
  • Looping the cross wide
  • Dropping the lead hand
  • Standing still after the counter

Drill Ideas

  • Slip-cross partner drill
  • Reaction mitt rounds
  • Counter-only sparring drills

Progression

  • Connect to Hook Counter
  • Connect to Defensive Reaction
