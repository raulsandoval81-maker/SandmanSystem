Skill 06 — Jab Entry

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Use the jab to safely enter striking range

B — Step behind the jab while maintaining balance

C — Continue offense or angle out after entry

Coaching Cues

  • “Jab your way in.”
  • “Feet and hands together.”
  • “Stay protected.”
  • “Enter with purpose.”

Common Mistakes

  • Walking in without defense
  • Overreaching the jab
  • Crossing feet during entry
  • Stopping movement after entry

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-entry shadowboxing
  • Partner range-entry drills
  • Mitt work with forward pressure

Progression

  • Connect to Cross Counter
  • Connect to Range Control
