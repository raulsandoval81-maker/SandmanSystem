Skill 16 — Ring Position

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain strong position relative to ropes and corners

B — Use footwork to control center space

C — Escape bad positions with angles and movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the center.”
  • “Don’t get trapped.”
  • “Move before pressure builds.”
  • “Control the space.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing straight to the ropes
  • Standing flat in corners
  • Ignoring escape angles
  • Overcommitting while pressured

Drill Ideas

  • Corner escape drills
  • Center-control partner rounds
  • Ring movement shadowboxing

Progression

  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
  • Connect to Ring IQ
