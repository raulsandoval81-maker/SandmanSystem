Skill 17 — Controlled Sparring

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply techniques in live but controlled exchanges

B — Maintain composure, defense, and pace under pressure

C — Show discipline and partner awareness throughout rounds

Coaching Cues

  • “Technical work first.”
  • “Stay composed.”
  • “Protect your partner.”
  • “See the openings.”

Common Mistakes

  • Going too hard emotionally
  • Abandoning defense during exchanges
  • Throwing wild combinations
  • Freezing after getting hit

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-only sparring
  • Technical body-head rounds
  • Controlled situational sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Fatigue Control
  • Connect to Live Application
