Skill 09 — Body Head Combo

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Attack the body to change opponent reactions

B — Transition immediately to head-level offense

C — Maintain balance and guard throughout combinations

Coaching Cues

  • “Change levels.”
  • “Body opens the head.”
  • “Stay tight.”
  • “Flow through the combo.”

Common Mistakes

  • Pausing between levels
  • Dipping too low for body shots
  • Dropping hands during transitions
  • Standing square after combinations

Drill Ideas

  • Body-head mitt drills
  • Heavy bag level-change rounds
  • Partner combo flow drills

Progression

  • Connect to Range Control
  • Connect to Combination Flow
