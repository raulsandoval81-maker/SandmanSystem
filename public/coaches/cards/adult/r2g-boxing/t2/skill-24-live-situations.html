Skill 24 — Live Situations

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Work realistic boxing scenarios under controlled pressure

B — Apply offense, defense, and movement together

C — Make tactical decisions during unpredictable exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Solve the situation.”
  • “Stay composed live.”
  • “Use your tools.”
  • “Keep moving.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking under pressure
  • Forgetting defensive structure
  • Standing still during exchanges
  • Throwing without setup

Drill Ideas

  • Rope escape situations
  • Corner-pressure drills
  • Situational sparring rounds
