Skill 13 — Pressure Step

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Advance behind controlled offense and footwork

B — Cut space without overreaching or rushing

C — Maintain balance and defensive readiness while pressuring

Coaching Cues

  • “Walk them down smart.”
  • “Pressure with balance.”
  • “Hands ready.”
  • “Take space away.”

Common Mistakes

  • Charging forward recklessly
  • Crossing feet while advancing
  • Dropping hands during pressure
  • Overcommitting to offense

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure footwork rounds
  • Corner-cutting partner drills
  • Heavy bag forward-pressure rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Defense Reset
  • Connect to Combination Pressure
