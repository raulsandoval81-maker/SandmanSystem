Skill 23 — Combination Pressure

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply sustained offense behind balanced footwork

B — Force reactions through controlled combinations

C — Maintain defensive awareness while advancing

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure smart.”
  • “Punch and move.”
  • “Stay balanced forward.”
  • “Don’t chase recklessly.”

Common Mistakes

  • Smothering punches while advancing
  • Dropping hands during pressure
  • Overthrowing combinations emotionally
  • Walking into counters

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure bag rounds
  • Corner-control spar drills
  • Forward-combo partner drills

Progression

  • Connect to Live Situations
  • Connect to Finish Standard
