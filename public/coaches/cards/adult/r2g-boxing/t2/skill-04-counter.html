Skill 04 — Counter

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Defend incoming offense with balance and vision

B — Fire immediate return offense into the opening

C — Reset safely after the exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “Defend then answer.”
  • “Short reactions.”
  • “See the opening.”
  • “Stay under control.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing counters without defense first
  • Countering too late
  • Overcommitting during exchanges
  • Standing still after counters

Drill Ideas

  • Slip-counter drills
  • Parry-return rounds
  • Reaction mitt work

Progression

  • Connect to Cross Counter
  • Connect to Hook Counter
