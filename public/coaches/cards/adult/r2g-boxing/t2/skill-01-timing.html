Skill 01 — Timing

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize openings during opponent movement and strikes

B — Deliver punches during vulnerable moments

C — Recover balance and position after exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t rush the shot.”
  • “See the opening first.”
  • “Punch with the moment.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing too early
  • Throwing late after the opening closes
  • Forcing punches without setup
  • Overcommitting during exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Coach-call reaction drills
  • Partner timing rounds
  • Counter timing mitt work

Progression

  • Connect to Rhythm
  • Connect to Counter
