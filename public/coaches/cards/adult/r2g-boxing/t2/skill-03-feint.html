Skill 03 — Feint

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Show believable movement or attack cues

B — Draw defensive reactions from the opponent

C — Follow immediately with real offense

Coaching Cues

  • “Sell the fake.”
  • “Watch the reaction.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Fake then fire.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overexaggerating the feint
  • Failing to follow with offense
  • Losing balance during movement
  • Repeating the same fake constantly

Drill Ideas

  • Feint-to-jab drills
  • Reaction partner rounds
  • Shadowboxing with feint entries

Progression

  • Connect to Counter
  • Connect to Jab Entry
