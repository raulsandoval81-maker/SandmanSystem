Skill 10 — Range Control

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control distance with footwork and jab management

B — Keep opponents at favorable punching range

C — Exit unsafe exchanges with movement and angles

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the space.”
  • “Use the jab.”
  • “Move your feet first.”
  • “Don’t get trapped.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing at one fixed range
  • Backing straight up repeatedly
  • Entering without setup
  • Crossing feet during exits

Drill Ideas

  • Range touch drills
  • Jab-and-move rounds
  • Partner distance control drills

Progression

  • Connect to Pivot
  • Connect to Ring Position
