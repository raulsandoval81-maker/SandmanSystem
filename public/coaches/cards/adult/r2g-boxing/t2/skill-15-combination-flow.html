Skill 15 — Combination Flow

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Link punches together with rhythm and balance

B — Transition smoothly between offense, movement, and defense

C — Finish exchanges under control and ready to continue

Coaching Cues

  • “Flow, don’t force.”
  • “Punches connect together.”
  • “Stay relaxed.”
  • “Move after combinations.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing combinations too stiff
  • Pausing between punches
  • Overcommitting to power
  • Standing still after offense

Drill Ideas

  • Flow shadowboxing rounds
  • Continuous mitt combinations
  • Heavy bag rhythm rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
  • Connect to Live Application
