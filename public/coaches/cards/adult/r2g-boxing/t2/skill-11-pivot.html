Skill 11 — Pivot

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Rotate off the lead foot while maintaining stance

B — Create a new angle after offense or defense

C — Reestablish balanced position after movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn off the foot.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Don’t cross the feet.”
  • “Angle out clean.”

Common Mistakes

  • Crossing feet during pivots
  • Standing upright while turning
  • Turning too wide and losing balance
  • Stopping movement after the pivot

Drill Ideas

  • Pivot cone drills
  • Mitt work with angle exits
  • Shadowboxing pivot rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Exit Angle
  • Connect to Ring Position
