Skill 21 — Ring IQ

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Read opponent habits, rhythm, and reactions

B — Make smart tactical adjustments during exchanges

C — Control positioning, pace, and decision-making under pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “See the patterns.”
  • “Think while moving.”
  • “Make adjustments.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Repeating predictable attacks
  • Ignoring opponent reactions
  • Fighting emotionally instead of tactically
  • Standing in bad positions too long

Drill Ideas

  • Pattern-recognition sparring
  • Coach adjustment rounds
  • Tactical movement drills

Progression

  • Connect to Defensive Reaction
  • Connect to Combination Pressure
