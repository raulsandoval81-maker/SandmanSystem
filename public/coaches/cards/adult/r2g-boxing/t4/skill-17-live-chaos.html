Skill 17 — Live Chaos

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain structure and awareness during unpredictable exchanges

B — Recover quickly from mistakes, pressure, and momentum swings

C — Continue making tactical decisions under heavy stress

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay calm in chaos.”
  • “Recover immediately.”
  • “Structure first.”
  • “Think through pressure.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking during heavy exchanges
  • Throwing reckless punches emotionally
  • Freezing after mistakes
  • Losing defensive awareness

Drill Ideas

  • Chaos sparring scenarios
  • Random coach-command drills
  • Pressure recovery rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Strategy Build
  • Connect to Opponent Reading
