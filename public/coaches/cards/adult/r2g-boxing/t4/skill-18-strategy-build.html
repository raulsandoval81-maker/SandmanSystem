Skill 18 — Strategy Build

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Develop tactical plans based on style, range, and reactions

B — Adjust offensive and defensive priorities during rounds

C — Maintain strategic focus under fatigue and pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Build the fight plan.”
  • “Box with intention.”
  • “Adjust strategically.”
  • “Stay disciplined.”

Common Mistakes

  • Fighting emotionally without structure
  • Ignoring opponent patterns
  • Changing tactics too randomly
  • Forgetting long-term round control

Drill Ideas

  • Style-based sparring plans
  • Tactical adjustment rounds
  • Opponent-analysis drills

Progression

  • Connect to Opponent Reading
  • Connect to Finish Instinct
