Skill 15 — Mental Pressure

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply emotional and tactical pressure without reckless aggression

B — Force hesitation through pace, positioning, and composure

C — Maintain psychological control during live exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Break the confidence.”
  • “Stay composed pressuring.”
  • “Make them uncomfortable.”
  • “Control the feeling.”

Common Mistakes

  • Confusing pressure with recklessness
  • Overforcing exchanges emotionally
  • Losing defensive shape during pressure
  • Burning energy chasing finishes

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure-control sparring
  • Forward composure rounds
  • Fatigue pressure scenarios

Progression

  • Connect to Round Stealing
  • Connect to Live Chaos
