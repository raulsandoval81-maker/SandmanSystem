Skill 16 — Round Stealing

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Finish rounds strongly through pressure and activity

B — Control visible momentum during close exchanges

C — Maintain composure while increasing tactical urgency

Coaching Cues

  • “Finish the round hard.”
  • “Own the last exchange.”
  • “Stay technical pushing pace.”
  • “Strong finish matters.”

Common Mistakes

  • Rushing emotionally at round ends
  • Throwing wild combinations
  • Ignoring defense while pressing
  • Burning energy too early in rounds

Drill Ideas

  • Last-30-second sparring rounds
  • Finish-pressure circuits
  • Momentum-control live drills

  • Connect to Live Chaos
