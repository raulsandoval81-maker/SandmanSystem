Skill 29 — Fight Management

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Manage pace, range, pressure, and risk across live rounds

B — Make disciplined tactical decisions under changing conditions

C — Control round flow without losing technical structure

Coaching Cues

  • “Manage the fight.”
  • “Control risk.”
  • “Win the decisions.”
  • “Stay disciplined.”

Common Mistakes

  • Fighting emotionally instead of managing
  • Taking unnecessary risks late
  • Ignoring round flow
  • Losing structure while ahead

Drill Ideas

  • Scenario-based round management
  • Lead-protection sparring
  • Coach-scored tactical rounds

Progression

  • Connect to System Mastery
  • Connect to Adult R2G T5
