Skill 07 — Inside Dominance

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control positioning and posture during close exchanges

B — Blend short offense with defensive responsibility

C — Maintain tactical advantage in pocket situations

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the inside.”
  • “Stay compact.”
  • “Short punches score.”
  • “Protect while working.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing wide punches inside
  • Standing upright during exchanges
  • Ignoring head position
  • Freezing under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Inside sparring rounds
  • Short-hook body drills
  • Compact exchange circuits

Progression

  • Connect to Chain Pressure
  • Connect to Jab System
