Skill 14 — Fatigue Weaponization

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain technical structure while opponents fade physically

B — Increase pressure and tactical pace under fatigue conditions

C — Use conditioning and composure as offensive advantages

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay sharp tired.”
  • “Pressure late.”
  • “Break them with pace.”
  • “Structure wins tired.”

Common Mistakes

  • Getting sloppy under fatigue
  • Throwing reckless punches emotionally
  • Dropping defensive awareness
  • Burning energy too early

Progression
