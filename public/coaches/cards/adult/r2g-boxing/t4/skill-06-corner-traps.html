Skill 06 — Corner Traps

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Guide opponents toward corner positions through footwork and pressure

B — Cut escape lanes while maintaining offensive balance

C — Control exchanges safely inside trapped positions

Coaching Cues

  • “Cut the exits.”
  • “Trap with the feet.”
  • “Pressure under control.”
  • “Don’t rush the finish.”

Common Mistakes

  • Chasing directly into counters
  • Standing square in front of exits
  • Smothering offense inside corners
  • Losing defensive awareness

Drill Ideas

  • Corner trap sparring
  • Pressure angle drills
  • Exit-control mitt rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Inside Dominance
  • Connect to Chain Pressure
