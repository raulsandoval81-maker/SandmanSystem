Skill 24 — Chain Finishing

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build finishing sequences through layered combinations

B — Continue offense after first reaction, block, or escape attempt

C — Finish with composure, defense, and controlled pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Finish in chains.”
  • “Don’t stop after one shot.”
  • “Pressure with control.”
  • “Stay sharp finishing.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing one big punch and stopping
  • Getting reckless during finish attempts
  • Losing balance while chasing
  • Ignoring defense during offense

Drill Ideas

  • Finish chain mitt rounds
  • Pressure follow-up sparring
  • Second and third attack drills

Progression

  • Connect to Corner Dictation
  • Connect to High Pressure Rounds
