Skill 03 — Pressure Control

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply and release pressure intentionally during exchanges

B — Manage pace, distance, and emotional control while advancing

C — Maintain tactical positioning under live resistance

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure with purpose.”
  • “Control the pace.”
  • “Stay balanced forward.”
  • “Don’t chase recklessly.”

Common Mistakes

  • Smothering offense during pressure
  • Walking into counters emotionally
  • Ignoring defensive structure
  • Overforcing exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Controlled pressure sparring
  • Pressure-release movement rounds
  • Forward control mitt drills

Progression

  • Connect to Range Dictation
  • Connect to Counter Pressure
