Skill 05 — Counter Pressure

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply pressure while preparing defensive counters

B — Force reactions and punish predictable responses

C — Maintain balance and tactical awareness during exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure and punish.”
  • “Walk them into counters.”
  • “Stay sharp forward.”
  • “Control the reactions.”

Common Mistakes

  • Pressuring without defensive awareness
  • Overcommitting emotionally
  • Throwing counters too wide
  • Ignoring positioning during pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure-counter sparring
  • Trap-and-counter mitt drills
  • Controlled pressure rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Corner Traps
  • Connect to Inside Dominance
