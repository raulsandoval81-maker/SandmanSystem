Skill 01 — Boxing IQ

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Read opponent patterns, tendencies, and reactions live

B — Make tactical decisions under pressure and fatigue

C — Adjust offense, defense, and positioning throughout rounds

Coaching Cues

  • “Think while boxing.”
  • “Read the reactions.”
  • “Adjust in real time.”
  • “Control the exchange.”

Common Mistakes

  • Fighting emotionally instead of tactically
  • Repeating predictable patterns
  • Ignoring opponent adjustments
  • Overcommitting under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Adaptive sparring rounds
  • Pattern-reading partner drills
  • Coach-adjustment live rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Adaptation
  • Connect to Strategy Build
