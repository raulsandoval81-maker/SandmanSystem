Skill 12 — Body Shot System

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build layered body attack entries through setups and pressure

B — Blend body-head transitions fluidly during exchanges

C — Maintain defensive awareness during close-range attacks

Coaching Cues

  • “Body opens everything.”
  • “Change levels correctly.”
  • “Protect while attacking.”
  • “Dig and move.”

Common Mistakes

  • Reaching from bad range
  • Bending at the waist
  • Staying in place after body attacks
  • Ignoring head protection

Drill Ideas

  • Body-head combination rounds
  • Pressure body shot sparring
  • Close-range body attack drills

Progression

  • Connect to Defense To Offense
  • Connect to Fatigue Weaponization
