Skill 11 — Hook System

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Blend lead and rear hooks into layered offensive systems

B — Create hook openings through angles, setups, and pressure

C — Recover structure immediately after hook exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Short and sharp.”
  • “Turn through the shot.”
  • “Hooks need setups.”
  • “Recover the guard fast.”

Common Mistakes

  • Looping hooks too wide
  • Dropping opposite hand coverage
  • Overrotating during exchanges
  • Standing still after hooking

Drill Ideas

  • Lead-rear hook flow drills
  • Pressure hook sparring
  • Angle-hook mitt rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Body Shot System
  • Connect to Defense To Offense
