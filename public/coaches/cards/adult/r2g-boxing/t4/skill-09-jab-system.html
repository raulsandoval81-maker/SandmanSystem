Skill 09 — Jab System

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Use the jab to manage distance, timing, and reactions

B — Blend jab variations into offensive and defensive layers

C — Control exchanges through rhythm and positioning

Coaching Cues

  • “The jab runs the fight.”
  • “Touch, blind, score.”
  • “Keep it active.”
  • “Build off the jab.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing every jab identically
  • Dropping the rear hand
  • Overreaching and leaning
  • Standing still after jabbing

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-control sparring
  • Rhythm jab mitt rounds
  • Distance-management drills

Progression

  • Connect to Cross System
  • Connect to Hook System
