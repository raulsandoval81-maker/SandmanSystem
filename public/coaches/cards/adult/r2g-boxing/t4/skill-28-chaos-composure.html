Skill 28 — Chaos Composure

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Stay calm during unpredictable exchanges and momentum swings

B — Rebuild stance, guard, and tactics after disruption

C — Continue boxing intelligently when the round gets messy

Coaching Cues

  • “Calm in chaos.”
  • “Recover structure.”
  • “Don’t panic punch.”
  • “Solve the moment.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking after pressure
  • Throwing reckless reactions
  • Freezing after mistakes
  • Forgetting footwork during chaos

Drill Ideas

  • Random chaos rounds
  • Mistake-recovery sparring
  • Pressure reset drills

Progression

  • Connect to Fight Management
  • Connect to System Mastery
