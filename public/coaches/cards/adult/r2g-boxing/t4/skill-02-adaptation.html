Skill 02 — Adaptation

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize changing fight conditions during live exchanges

B — Shift tactics, pace, and positioning as needed

C — Maintain composure while adjusting strategy

Coaching Cues

  • “Adjust fast.”
  • “Don’t stay predictable.”
  • “Solve the problem.”
  • “Stay composed changing.”

Common Mistakes

  • Sticking to failing tactics too long
  • Panicking during adjustments
  • Overcorrecting emotionally
  • Ignoring opponent changes

Drill Ideas

  • Adaptive sparring scenarios
  • Coach-command tactical shifts
  • Style-switch live rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Pressure Control
  • Connect to Opponent Reading
