Skill 10 — Cross System

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build cross entries through timing, setups, and angles

B — Deliver power while maintaining structure and balance

C — Recover immediately into movement, defense, or follow-up offense

Coaching Cues

  • “Straight through the target.”
  • “Set it up first.”
  • “Stay balanced after impact.”
  • “Recover fast.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing naked crosses without setup
  • Overrotating and falling forward
  • Dropping the lead hand
  • Standing still after throwing

Drill Ideas

  • Cross-counter drills
  • Setup-cross mitt rounds
  • Angle-cross sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Hook System
  • Connect to Body Shot System
