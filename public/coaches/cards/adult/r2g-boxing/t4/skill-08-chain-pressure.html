Skill 08 — Chain Pressure

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Sustain layered pressure through multiple exchanges

B — Blend offense, movement, and tactical positioning continuously

C — Force opponents into defensive overload and mistakes

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure keeps building.”
  • “Layer the attack.”
  • “Don’t let them reset.”
  • “Stay balanced forward.”

Common Mistakes

  • Burning energy too early
  • Smothering combinations
  • Ignoring defensive recovery
  • Pressuring emotionally instead of tactically

Drill Ideas

  • Continuous pressure sparring
  • Multi-phase mitt rounds
  • Pressure chain bag circuits

Progression

  • Connect to Jab System
  • Connect to Cross System
