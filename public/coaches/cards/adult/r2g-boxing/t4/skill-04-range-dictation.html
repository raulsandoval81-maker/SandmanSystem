Skill 04 — Range Dictation

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Force exchanges to happen at preferred boxing distance

B — Use footwork, jab systems, and positioning to manage range

C — Punish opponents for entering bad distance

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the range.”
  • “Fight where you’re strongest.”
  • “Control distance first.”
  • “Move before reacting.”

Common Mistakes

  • Allowing uncontrolled entries
  • Backing straight up repeatedly
  • Standing flat during exchanges
  • Ignoring angle exits

Drill Ideas

  • Distance-control sparring
  • Jab-range management drills
  • Movement-and-counter rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Counter Pressure
  • Connect to Corner Traps
