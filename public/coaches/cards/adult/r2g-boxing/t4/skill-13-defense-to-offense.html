Skill 13 — Defense To Offense

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Transition immediately from defensive reactions into scoring offense

B — Use slips, rolls, blocks, and angles to create counters

C — Maintain balance and composure during transitions

Coaching Cues

  • “Defend then answer.”
  • “Make them pay.”
  • “Short reactions.”
  • “Flow into offense.”

Common Mistakes

  • Freezing after defense
  • Countering too emotionally
  • Throwing wide return shots
  • Losing stance during transitions

Drill Ideas

  • Slip-counter circuits
  • Defense-reaction sparring
  • Block-to-offense mitt rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Fatigue Weaponization
  • Connect to Mental Pressure
