Skill 20 — Finish Instinct

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize vulnerable moments during exchanges

B — Apply composed offensive pressure to capitalize safely

C — Maintain defensive awareness while pursuing finishes

Coaching Cues

  • “See the opening.”
  • “Pressure with control.”
  • “Don’t rush emotionally.”
  • “Stay sharp finishing.”

Common Mistakes

  • Wild overcommitted attacks
  • Ignoring defense during finishes
  • Burning energy chasing stoppages
  • Forcing offense without setup

Drill Ideas

  • Finish-pressure sparring
  • Momentum swing drills
  • Controlled finish scenarios

Progression

  • Connect to Fight Breaking
  • Connect to Pressure Layering
