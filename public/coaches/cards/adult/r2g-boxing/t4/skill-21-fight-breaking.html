Skill 21 — Fight Breaking

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Identify when opponent structure, confidence, or pace is weakening

B — Increase pressure with discipline and tactical control

C — Break resistance without losing defense or composure

Coaching Cues

  • “Break them smart.”
  • “Pressure with discipline.”
  • “Don’t get reckless.”
  • “Stay sharp while they fade.”

Common Mistakes

  • Chasing the finish emotionally
  • Throwing wild shots when opponent fades
  • Ignoring defensive recovery
  • Burning energy without control

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure-breaking rounds
  • Fatigue response sparring
  • Composed finish drills

Progression

  • Connect to Pressure Layering
  • Connect to Chain Finishing
