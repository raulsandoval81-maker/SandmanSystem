Skill 26 — Mental Fatigue

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize mental decline during pressure and fatigue

B — Keep decision-making clear under hard rounds

C — Use composure to outlast emotional or tired opponents

Coaching Cues

  • “Think tired.”
  • “Stay calm late.”
  • “Don’t break mentally.”
  • “Make clean choices.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking when tired
  • Making emotional decisions late
  • Forgetting defensive responsibility
  • Accepting bad positions too long

Drill Ideas

  • Fatigue decision rounds
  • Late-round scenario sparring
  • Pressure composure drills

Progression

  • Connect to High Pressure Rounds
  • Connect to Chaos Composure
