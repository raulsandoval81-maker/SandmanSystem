Skill 23 — Weapon Blending

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Blend jab, cross, hooks, body shots, and defensive counters together

B — Choose weapons based on range, angle, and opponent reaction

C — Transition smoothly between tools without losing structure

Coaching Cues

  • “Use the right weapon.”
  • “Blend, don’t force.”
  • “Read the opening.”
  • “Flow between tools.”

Common Mistakes

  • Repeating one favorite punch
  • Ignoring range and angle
  • Forcing power shots without setup
  • Breaking stance during transitions

Drill Ideas

  • Coach-call weapon rounds
  • Range-based punch selection drills
  • Mixed weapon mitt work

Progression

  • Connect to Chain Finishing
  • Connect to Corner Dictation
