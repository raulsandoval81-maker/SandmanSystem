Skill 22 — Pressure Layering

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Stack forward pressure, feints, punches, and angles together

B — Force defensive reactions through repeated tactical layers

C — Keep balance and defensive awareness while advancing

Coaching Cues

  • “Layer the pressure.”
  • “Keep them reacting.”
  • “Pressure in phases.”
  • “Stay balanced forward.”

Common Mistakes

  • Pressuring in one predictable line
  • Smothering punches while advancing
  • Dropping defense during pressure
  • Forcing exchanges without setups

Drill Ideas

  • Layered pressure sparring
  • Feint-to-combo pressure rounds
  • Forward angle control drills

Progression

  • Connect to Weapon Blending
  • Connect to Chain Finishing
