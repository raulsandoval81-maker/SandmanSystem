Skill 19 — Opponent Reading

Journey: Adult R2G — Boxing Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Identify rhythm, habits, and defensive patterns live

B — Recognize emotional and physical changes during rounds

C — Use observations to guide tactical decisions

Coaching Cues

  • “Watch the reactions.”
  • “Read the habits.”
  • “See the openings.”
  • “Think while moving.”

Common Mistakes

  • Ignoring repeated opponent patterns
  • Focusing only on offense
  • Missing defensive habits
  • Fighting emotionally instead of tactically

Drill Ideas

  • Pattern-reading sparring
  • Reaction-analysis rounds
  • Coach-observation drills

Progression

  • Connect to Finish Instinct
  • Connect to Fight Breaking
