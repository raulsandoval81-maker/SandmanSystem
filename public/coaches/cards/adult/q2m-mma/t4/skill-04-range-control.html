Skill 04 — Range Control

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize striking, clinch, and wrestling range

B — Use movement and attacks to control distance

C — Reset range after exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the range.”
  • “Enter on purpose.”
  • “Exit safely.”
  • “Don’t hang in danger.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing in bad range
  • Reaching from too far away
  • Crashing without setup
  • Backing straight up

Drill Ideas

  • Range recognition rounds
  • Entry-exit drill
  • Partner distance control drill

Progression

  • Connect to weapon blending
  • Connect to cage dictation
