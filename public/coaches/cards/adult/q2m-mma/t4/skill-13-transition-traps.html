Skill 13 — Transition Traps

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Force reactions during transitions

B — Trap movement with timing and control

C — Attack once the trap is created

Coaching Cues

  • “Trap the movement.”
  • “Transition with pressure.”
  • “Stay connected.”
  • “Attack the reaction.”

Common Mistakes

  • Moving too fast
  • Losing connection
  • Attacking without setup
  • Leaving escape space

Drill Ideas

  • Transition trap rounds
  • Reaction transition drill
  • Position-flow trap drill

Progression

  • Connect to finish awareness
  • Connect to chaos control
