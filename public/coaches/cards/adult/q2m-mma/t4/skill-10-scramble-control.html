Skill 10 — Scramble Control

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Stay composed during chaotic scrambles

B — Maintain awareness and pressure

C — Recover dominant position quickly

Coaching Cues

  • “Win the scramble.”
  • “Keep moving.”
  • “Stay aware.”
  • “Recover position.”

Common Mistakes

  • Freezing during transitions
  • Losing awareness
  • Panicking under pressure
  • Giving up position

Drill Ideas

  • Live scramble rounds
  • Coach-start scramble drill
  • Reaction recovery circuits

Progression

  • Connect to ground dominance
  • Connect to transition traps
