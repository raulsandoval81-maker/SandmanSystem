Skill 01 — Fight IQ

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Read patterns, reactions, and positioning

B — Make smart tactical choices during exchanges

C — Adjust quickly after success or failure

Coaching Cues

  • “See the fight.”
  • “Read before reacting.”
  • “Adjust fast.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Repeating failed attacks
  • Fighting emotionally
  • Ignoring position
  • Forcing favorite weapons

Drill Ideas

  • Pattern recognition rounds
  • Coach-change reaction drill
  • Live adjustment rounds

Progression

  • Connect to adaptation
  • Connect to opponent reading
