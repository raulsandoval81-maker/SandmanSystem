Skill 20 — Finish Instinct

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize finish windows during live exchanges

B — Commit with control when the finish appears

C — Recover position if the finish is denied

Coaching Cues

  • “See the finish.”
  • “Commit clean.”
  • “Don’t force it.”
  • “Recover if denied.”

Common Mistakes

  • Missing finish opportunities
  • Forcing finishes too early
  • Giving up position to chase
  • Failing to reset after denial

Drill Ideas

  • Finish window rounds
  • Reaction finish drill
  • Control-to-finish situations

Progression

  • Connect to chain finishing
  • Connect to system mastery
