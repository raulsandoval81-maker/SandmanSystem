Skill 24 — Chain Finishing

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Attack with layered finishing systems

B — Chain finishes when the first option is defended

C — Recover control if finish is denied

Coaching Cues

  • “Chain the finish.”
  • “Don’t force one answer.”
  • “Control before release.”
  • “Flow to the next finish.”

Common Mistakes

  • Forcing one finish
  • Losing position
  • Rushing the finish
  • Stopping after first defense

Drill Ideas

  • Chain finishing rounds
  • Finish denial reaction drill
  • Position-to-finish circuits

Progression

  • Connect to fight breaking
  • Connect to system mastery
