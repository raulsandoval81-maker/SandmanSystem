Skill 21 — Fight Breaking

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Identify when opponent structure is weakening

B — Increase pressure with discipline

C — Break position, rhythm, or confidence safely

Coaching Cues

  • “Break the structure.”
  • “Pressure with discipline.”
  • “Stay patient.”
  • “Finish the damage.”

Common Mistakes

  • Rushing the break
  • Getting reckless
  • Ignoring defense
  • Missing the moment to pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure-breaking rounds
  • Late-round structure break drill
  • Partner fatigue pressure drill

Progression

  • Connect to pressure layering
  • Connect to chain finishing
