Skill 11 — Ground Dominance

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Establish dominant top position

B — Maintain pressure through movement

C — Advance position while staying balanced

Coaching Cues

  • “Heavy and controlled.”
  • “Dominate the position.”
  • “Pressure with patience.”
  • “Advance with purpose.”

Common Mistakes

  • Leaving space
  • Rushing transitions
  • Losing hip pressure
  • Forcing submissions too early

Drill Ideas

  • Ground dominance rounds
  • Pressure advancement drill
  • Partner escape-control drill

Progression

  • Connect to chain finishing
  • Connect to transition traps
