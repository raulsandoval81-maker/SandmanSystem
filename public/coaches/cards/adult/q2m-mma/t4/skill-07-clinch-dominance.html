Skill 07 — Clinch Dominance

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Establish dominant clinch position

B — Control posture, pressure, and movement

C — Transition to strikes, takedowns, or exits

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the tie.”
  • “Break posture.”
  • “Heavy pressure.”
  • “Control the exchange.”

Common Mistakes

  • Loose ties
  • Standing upright
  • No head pressure
  • Forcing takedowns blindly

Drill Ideas

  • Clinch control rounds
  • Pummel-to-pressure drill
  • Wall clinch domination drill

Progression

  • Connect to chain pressure
  • Connect to shot chain
