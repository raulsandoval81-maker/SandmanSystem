Skill 22 — Pressure Layering

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply pressure in multiple layers

B — Chain strikes, clinch, wrestling, and ground control

C — Maintain control through opponent reactions

Coaching Cues

  • “Layer the pressure.”
  • “One threat feeds the next.”
  • “Stay connected.”
  • “Never rush the break.”

Common Mistakes

  • Using one type of pressure only
  • Stopping between phases
  • Forcing attacks
  • Losing balance during transitions

Drill Ideas

  • Layered pressure rounds
  • Strike-clinch-shot pressure drill
  • Pressure phase circuits

Progression

  • Connect to weapon blending
  • Connect to cage dictation
