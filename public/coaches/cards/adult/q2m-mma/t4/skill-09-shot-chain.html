Skill 09 — Shot Chain

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Enter shots with setup and timing

B — Chain reattacks when defended

C — Finish or recover position safely

Coaching Cues

  • “Attack in sequence.”
  • “Keep driving.”
  • “Reattack immediately.”
  • “Stay disciplined.”

Common Mistakes

  • One-shot wrestling
  • Head outside position
  • Stopping after defense
  • Standing tall on entries

Drill Ideas

  • Reattack rounds
  • Chain takedown drill
  • Partner defense reaction shots

Progression

  • Connect to scramble control
  • Connect to ground dominance
