Skill 03 — Pressure Control

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply pressure without chasing

B — Control opponent exits and reactions

C — Reset pressure after every exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure with control.”
  • “Cut space down.”
  • “Don’t chase.”
  • “Stay balanced.”

Common Mistakes

  • Running forward
  • Overreaching
  • Letting opponent circle free
  • Breaking stance

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure control rounds
  • Cutoff movement drill
  • Partner escape pressure drill

Progression

  • Connect to counter pressure
  • Connect to pressure layering
