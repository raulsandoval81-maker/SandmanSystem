Skill 25 — Cage Dictation

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Dictate where the fight happens

B — Use pressure, angles, and traps to control space

C — Reset position after every exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “Choose the battlefield.”
  • “Dictate the cage.”
  • “Trap the exits.”
  • “Reset dominant space.”

Common Mistakes

  • Following instead of steering
  • Giving up center
  • Letting opponent reset freely
  • Overchasing near the cage

Drill Ideas

  • Cage dictation rounds
  • Space-control drill
  • Partner escape denial drill

Progression

  • Connect to high pressure rounds
  • Connect to fight management
