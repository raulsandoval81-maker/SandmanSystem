Skill 12 — Chain Finishing

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Attack with layered finishing systems

B — Flow between submissions and control

C — Recover position if finish fails

Coaching Cues

  • “Chain the finish.”
  • “Stay patient.”
  • “Control before attack.”
  • “Flow to the next option.”

Common Mistakes

  • Forcing one finish
  • Losing position
  • Attacking too early
  • Stopping after defense

Drill Ideas

  • Submission chain rounds
  • Position-to-finish drill
  • Reaction finish circuits

Progression

  • Connect to transition traps
  • Connect to finish awareness
