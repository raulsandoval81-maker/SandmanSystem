Skill 29 — Fight Management

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Manage position, pace, pressure, and risk

B — Make decisions based on the whole fight

C — Finish rounds with control and awareness

Coaching Cues

  • “Manage the fight.”
  • “Control the risk.”
  • “Know the moment.”
  • “Finish smart.”

Common Mistakes

  • Chasing unnecessary exchanges
  • Ignoring time and position
  • Poor risk control
  • Letting opponent dictate the round

Drill Ideas

  • Situational fight management rounds
  • Score-clock-position drill
  • Coach scenario live rounds

Progression

  • Connect to system mastery
  • Connect to final testing
