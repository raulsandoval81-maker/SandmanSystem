Skill 23 — Weapon Blending

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Blend striking, clinch, wrestling, and ground threats

B — Use one weapon to open the next

C — Recover flow after failed attacks

Coaching Cues

  • “Blend the weapons.”
  • “One opens the next.”
  • “Don’t fight in boxes.”
  • “Recover and continue.”

Common Mistakes

  • Using isolated attacks
  • Stopping after one phase
  • Forcing bad transitions
  • Ignoring openings created by reactions

Drill Ideas

  • Weapon-blending flow rounds
  • Strike-to-clinch-to-ground circuits
  • Reaction weapon choice drill

Progression

  • Connect to chain finishing
  • Connect to system mastery
