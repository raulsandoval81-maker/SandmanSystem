Skill 06 — Cage Traps

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Force opponent movement toward the cage

B — Remove exits using angle and pressure

C — Attack once the trap is secured

Coaching Cues

  • “Cut the exits.”
  • “Trap before attacking.”
  • “Pressure smart.”
  • “Own the wall.”

Common Mistakes

  • Chasing instead of trapping
  • Overcommitting forward
  • Leaving escape lanes
  • Attacking too early

Drill Ideas

  • Cage trap rounds
  • Partner wall escape drill
  • Pressure-to-trap drill

Progression

  • Connect to cage dictation
  • Connect to fight breaking
