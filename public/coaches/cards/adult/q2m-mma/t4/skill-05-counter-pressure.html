Skill 05 — Counter Pressure

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Absorb or redirect incoming pressure

B — Return pressure immediately with control

C — Reclaim position after the counter

Coaching Cues

  • “Take their pressure.”
  • “Return it clean.”
  • “Don’t retreat forever.”
  • “Win position back.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing straight up
  • Defending without response
  • Panicking under pressure
  • Countering off balance

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure return rounds
  • Defense-to-pressure drill
  • Partner pressure counter drill

Progression

  • Connect to cage traps
  • Connect to fight breaking
