Skill 16 — Round Stealing

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize late-round scoring moments

B — Increase controlled activity without getting reckless

C — Finish the round with pressure and composure

Coaching Cues

  • “Win the last minute.”
  • “Pressure smart.”
  • “Score without panic.”
  • “Finish composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Rushing wild attacks
  • Forgetting defense late
  • Holding back too long
  • Breaking structure to chase points

Drill Ideas

  • Last-minute pressure rounds
  • Score-and-reset drill
  • Coach clock callout rounds

Progression

  • Connect to live chaos
  • Connect to fight management
