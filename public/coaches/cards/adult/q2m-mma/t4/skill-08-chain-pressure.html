Skill 08 — Chain Pressure

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply layered offensive pressure

B — Flow from strike to clinch to takedown

C — Maintain control through reactions

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure never stops.”
  • “Chain the attack.”
  • “Stay connected.”
  • “Flow forward.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after first attack
  • Overforcing offense
  • Losing balance
  • Breaking structure

Drill Ideas

  • Continuous attack rounds
  • Strike-to-shot chain drill
  • Pressure layering circuits

Progression

  • Connect to weapon blending
  • Connect to chain finishing
