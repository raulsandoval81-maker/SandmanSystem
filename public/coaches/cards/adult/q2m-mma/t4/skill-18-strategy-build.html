Skill 18 — Strategy Build

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Identify the athlete’s strongest weapons

B — Build a simple strategy around those weapons

C — Test and adjust the strategy live

Coaching Cues

  • “Build your game.”
  • “Know your weapons.”
  • “Test the plan.”
  • “Adjust live.”

Common Mistakes

  • No clear plan
  • Trying too many weapons
  • Ignoring what works
  • Failing to adjust under resistance

Drill Ideas

  • Game-plan sparring rounds
  • Primary weapon live rounds
  • Coach feedback adjustment drill

Progression

  • Connect to opponent reading
  • Connect to fight management
