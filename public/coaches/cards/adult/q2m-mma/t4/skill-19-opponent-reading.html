Skill 19 — Opponent Reading

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Identify opponent habits, reactions, and openings

B — Adjust attacks based on the read

C — Confirm or change the read during live work

Coaching Cues

  • “Read the pattern.”
  • “Watch the reaction.”
  • “Attack the habit.”
  • “Adjust if wrong.”

Common Mistakes

  • Guessing instead of reading
  • Ignoring repeated reactions
  • Attacking blindly
  • Failing to adjust after bad reads

Drill Ideas

  • Pattern recognition rounds
  • Reaction read drill
  • Opponent habit sparring

Progression

  • Connect to finish instinct
  • Connect to fight breaking
