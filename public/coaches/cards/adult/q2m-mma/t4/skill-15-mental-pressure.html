Skill 15 — Mental Pressure

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply composure and constant pressure

B — Force hesitation and emotional reactions

C — Maintain calm while opponent breaks mentally

Coaching Cues

  • “Break the mindset.”
  • “Stay calm under pressure.”
  • “Make them uncomfortable.”
  • “Pressure without emotion.”

Common Mistakes

  • Fighting emotionally
  • Overforcing exchanges
  • Losing discipline
  • Breaking structure while attacking

Drill Ideas

  • Mental pressure sparring
  • Controlled pressure rounds
  • Composure-under-fire drill

Progression

  • Connect to round stealing
  • Connect to live chaos
