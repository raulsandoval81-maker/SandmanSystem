Skill 03 — Feint

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Show false attacks without overcommitting

B — Read defensive reactions

C — Attack off the reaction

Coaching Cues

  • “Sell the fake.”
  • “Watch the reaction.”
  • “Don’t overreach.”
  • “Attack after movement.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overcommitting feints
  • No follow-up attack
  • Standing still
  • Predictable rhythm

Drill Ideas

  • Feint-and-react drill
  • Partner reaction rounds
  • Strike-feint combinations

Progression

  • Connect to chain entries
  • Connect to counters
