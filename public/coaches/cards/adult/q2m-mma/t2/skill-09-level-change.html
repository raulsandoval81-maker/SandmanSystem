Skill 09 — Level Change

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Lower levels without breaking posture

B — Maintain balance through movement

C — Recover stance after attack attempts

Coaching Cues

  • “Bend the knees.”
  • “Chest up.”
  • “Don’t reach.”
  • “Explode upward.”

Common Mistakes

  • Bending at the waist
  • Standing upright after entry
  • Slow movement
  • Poor balance

Drill Ideas

  • Level-change reps
  • Penetration-step drill
  • Reaction shot drill

Progression

  • Connect to takedowns
  • Connect to chain entries
