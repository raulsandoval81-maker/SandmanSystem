Skill 13 — Ground Transition

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Move safely between positions

B — Maintain pressure during transitions

C — Recover balance after movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Move with pressure.”
  • “Stay connected.”
  • “Control before moving.”
  • “Balance first.”

Common Mistakes

  • Losing pressure
  • Moving too fast
  • Leaving space
  • Poor posture

Drill Ideas

  • Position-flow drill
  • Transition rounds
  • Ground movement circuits

Progression

  • Connect to top pressure
  • Connect to live grappling
