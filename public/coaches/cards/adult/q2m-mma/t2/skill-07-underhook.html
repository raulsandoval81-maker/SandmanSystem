Skill 07 — Underhook

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Establish strong inside underhook position

B — Apply pressure and posture control

C — Transition to offense or recovery

Coaching Cues

  • “Win inside control.”
  • “Head position first.”
  • “Pressure forward.”
  • “Stay connected.”

Common Mistakes

  • Loose elbow position
  • Weak posture
  • No head pressure
  • Standing upright

Drill Ideas

  • Pummel drill
  • Underhook battle rounds
  • Wall underhook control drill

Progression

  • Connect to cage wrestling
  • Connect to takedown chains
