Skill 02 — Timing

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Read movement before committing

B — Attack during openings and reactions

C — Reset after the exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “See the opening.”
  • “Don’t rush.”
  • “Punch on time.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Attacking blindly
  • Forcing exchanges
  • Late reactions
  • Standing still

Drill Ideas

  • Reaction timing drill
  • Coach cue attack drill
  • Partner timing rounds

Progression

  • Connect to counters
  • Connect to live striking
