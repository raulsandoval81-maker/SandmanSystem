Skill 23 — Pressure Control

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply forward pressure without chasing

B — Maintain balance while cutting space

C — Reset pressure after disengagement

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure with control.”
  • “Cut space down.”
  • “Feet under you.”
  • “Don’t chase wildly.”

Common Mistakes

  • Running forward
  • Overreaching
  • Losing stance
  • Letting opponent circle free

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure step rounds
  • Cutoff movement drill
  • Partner escape pressure drill

Progression

  • Connect to cage control
  • Connect to live situations
