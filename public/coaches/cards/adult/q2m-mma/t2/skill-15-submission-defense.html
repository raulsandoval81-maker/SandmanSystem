Skill 15 — Submission Defense

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize submission danger early

B — Protect neck, arms, and posture first

C — Escape to safe position before attacking

Coaching Cues

  • “Protect first.”
  • “Posture saves you.”
  • “Don’t panic.”
  • “Escape before offense.”

Common Mistakes

  • Waiting too long to defend
  • Leaving neck exposed
  • Trying to muscle out
  • Panicking under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Submission danger recognition drill
  • Controlled escape rounds
  • Position reset safety drill

Progression

  • Connect to ground transition
  • Connect to live grappling safety
