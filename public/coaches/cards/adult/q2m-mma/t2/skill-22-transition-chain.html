Skill 22 — Transition Chain

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Move from strike to clinch or shot

B — Continue into secondary attacks or control

C — Recover and continue after failed transitions

Coaching Cues

  • “One phase feeds the next.”
  • “Stay connected.”
  • “Don’t freeze between attacks.”
  • “Recover and continue.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after first attempt
  • Forcing bad transitions
  • Losing posture
  • Not reacting to resistance

Drill Ideas

  • Strike-clinch-shot drill
  • Failed attack recovery rounds
  • Continuous transition flow drill

Progression

  • Connect to live application
  • Connect to finish standard
