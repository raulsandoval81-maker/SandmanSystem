Skill 08 — Hand Fight

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Clear ties and control wrists safely

B — Maintain position while fighting hands

C — Transition into offense or recovery

Coaching Cues

  • “Fight for control.”
  • “Win the hands.”
  • “Stay active.”
  • “Pressure and react.”

Common Mistakes

  • Reaching too far
  • Standing still
  • Weak posture
  • No follow-up attack

Drill Ideas

  • Wrist-control drill
  • Partner hand-fighting rounds
  • Reaction tie-up drill

Progression

  • Connect to entries
  • Connect to clinch offense
