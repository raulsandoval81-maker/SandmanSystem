Skill 16 — Top Pressure

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Establish strong top position

B — Apply pressure without overcommitting

C — Maintain control during movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Heavy but balanced.”
  • “Stay connected.”
  • “Control before strikes.”
  • “Pressure with purpose.”

Common Mistakes

  • Sitting too high
  • Leaving space
  • Overcommitting weight
  • Losing balance during movement

Drill Ideas

  • Top pressure hold drill
  • Partner escape resistance rounds
  • Pressure-to-transition drill

Progression

  • Connect to ground transition
  • Connect to live application
