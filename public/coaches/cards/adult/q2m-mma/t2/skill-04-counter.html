Skill 04 — Counter

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Defend the initial attack

B — Return immediately with controlled offense

C — Recover stance after the counter

Coaching Cues

  • “Defense first.”
  • “Return clean.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Reset quickly.”

Common Mistakes

  • Countering too late
  • Ignoring defense
  • Overthrowing strikes
  • Standing square

Drill Ideas

  • Slip-counter drill
  • Defense reaction rounds
  • Coach call counter drill

Progression

  • Connect to timing
  • Connect to live striking exchanges
