Skill 11 — Combination Flow

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Chain strikes together with balance

B — Maintain movement during combinations

C — Reset after exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Flow strike to strike.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Hands recover.”
  • “Move after contact.”

Common Mistakes

  • Pausing between strikes
  • Overthrowing punches
  • Standing square
  • Watching punches land

Drill Ideas

  • Pad flow rounds
  • Shadow combination drill
  • Partner flow exchanges

Progression

  • Connect to counters
  • Connect to live striking
