Skill 18 — Fatigue Control

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Keep posture, guard, and base while tired

B — Breathe and make simple decisions under fatigue

C — Finish the rep without technical collapse

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t quit structure.”
  • “Breathe and work.”
  • “Hands stay ready.”
  • “Finish clean.”

Common Mistakes

  • Hands dropping
  • Standing tall
  • Sloppy shots or strikes
  • Mentally shutting down

Drill Ideas

  • Technique-under-fatigue rounds
  • Short burst recovery drill
  • Fatigue finish circuit

Progression

  • Connect to round management
  • Connect to finish standard
