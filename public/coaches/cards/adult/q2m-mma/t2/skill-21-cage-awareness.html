Skill 21 — Cage Awareness

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize wall position and danger zones

B — Move before getting trapped

C — Recover center position after exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Know where you are.”
  • “Don’t drift backward.”
  • “Circle before pressure builds.”
  • “Win the center.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing into the wall
  • Ignoring position
  • Freezing on the cage
  • Moving straight backward

Drill Ideas

  • Cage awareness movement drill
  • Wall escape rounds
  • Partner pressure position drill

Progression

  • Connect to cage control
  • Connect to pressure control
