Skill 20 — Live Application

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply assigned T2 skills in live movement

B — Make simple decisions under pressure

C — Reset after success or failure

Coaching Cues

  • “Use what you trained.”
  • “Make decisions.”
  • “Stay calm.”
  • “Reset and go again.”

Common Mistakes

  • Forgetting the assigned skill
  • Freezing under pressure
  • Going wild instead of technical
  • Not resetting after mistakes

Drill Ideas

  • Live skill application rounds
  • Coach-start situation rounds
  • Partner resistance application drill

Progression

  • Connect to live situations
  • Connect to finish standard
