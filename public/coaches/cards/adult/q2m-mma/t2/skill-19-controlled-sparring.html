Skill 19 — Controlled Sparring

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Work live at controlled intensity

B — Apply assigned skills safely with a partner

C — Reset respectfully after each exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “Control the pace.”
  • “Use the skill.”
  • “Protect your partner.”
  • “Stay technical.”

Common Mistakes

  • Going too hard
  • Ignoring assigned skill
  • Emotional reactions
  • Losing safety awareness

Drill Ideas

  • Technical sparring rounds
  • Position-limited sparring
  • Coach-assigned skill sparring

Progression

  • Connect to live application
  • Connect to live situations
