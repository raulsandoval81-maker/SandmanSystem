Skill 25 — Finish Standard

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Complete exchanges with discipline and control

B — Maintain technique under resistance and fatigue

C — Recover stance, position, and composure after every rep

Coaching Cues

  • “Finish clean.”
  • “Don’t stop halfway.”
  • “Control before release.”
  • “Pressure through the end.”

Common Mistakes

  • Quitting exchanges early
  • Sloppy finishes
  • Losing control under fatigue
  • Failing to reset after the rep

Drill Ideas

  • Finish-the-exchange rounds
  • Fatigue finish drill
  • Live finish standard situations

Progression

  • Connect to T3 readiness
  • Connect to full MMA application
