Skill 06 — Cage Control

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control center positioning and forward pressure

B — Cut off exits using angles and footwork

C — Reset pressure after exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the center.”
  • “Cut the cage.”
  • “Pressure with balance.”
  • “Don’t chase.”

Common Mistakes

  • Following instead of cutting off
  • Standing square
  • Overcommitting pressure
  • Backing straight out

Drill Ideas

  • Cage-cutting drill
  • Pressure movement rounds
  • Partner wall-control drill

Progression

  • Connect to cage awareness
  • Connect to pressure control
