Skill 10 — Shot Defense

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize takedown attempts early

B — Defend with hips, pressure, and movement

C — Recover stance or offensive position

Coaching Cues

  • “Heavy hips.”
  • “Head pressure.”
  • “Sprawl and recover.”
  • “Don’t freeze.”

Common Mistakes

  • Slow reactions
  • Standing upright
  • Weak hip pressure
  • Backing straight away

Drill Ideas

  • Reaction sprawl drill
  • Partner shot-defense rounds
  • Front-headlock recovery drill

Progression

  • Connect to counters
  • Connect to cage wrestling
