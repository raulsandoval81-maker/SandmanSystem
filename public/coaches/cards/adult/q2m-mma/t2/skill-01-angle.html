Skill 01 — Angle

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create outside positioning after exchanges

B — Maintain stance while changing angles

C — Reset offense from the new position

Coaching Cues

  • “Move off center.”
  • “Angle after contact.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Reset and attack.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing straight up
  • Crossing feet
  • Standing square
  • Stopping movement

Drill Ideas

  • Angle step drill
  • Partner angle movement rounds
  • Strike-and-angle flow drill

Progression

  • Connect to counters
  • Connect to cage exits
