Skill 12 — Kick Defense

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize kicks early

B — Defend using checks, distance, or movement

C — Recover stance after defense

Coaching Cues

  • “See it early.”
  • “Check and recover.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Move after defense.”

Common Mistakes

  • Late reactions
  • Standing flat-footed
  • Dropping hands
  • No recovery movement

Drill Ideas

  • Kick-check drill
  • Partner kick defense rounds
  • Reaction defense drill

Progression

  • Connect to counters
  • Connect to live striking
