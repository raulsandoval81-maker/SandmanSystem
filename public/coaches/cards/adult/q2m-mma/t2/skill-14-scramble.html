Skill 14 — Scramble

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — React safely during scrambles

B — Recover base and posture quickly

C — Re-establish control after movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Keep moving.”
  • “Find your base.”
  • “Stay composed.”
  • “Recover position.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking
  • Stopping movement
  • Losing posture
  • Giving up position

Drill Ideas

  • Scramble rounds
  • Bad-position recovery drill
  • Partner scramble situations

Progression

  • Connect to live grappling
  • Connect to recovery systems
