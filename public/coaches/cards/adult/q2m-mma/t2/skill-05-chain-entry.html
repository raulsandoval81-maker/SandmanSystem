Skill 05 — Chain Entry

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Enter behind strikes or movement

B — Transition immediately into secondary attacks

C — Continue pressure or reset safely

Coaching Cues

  • “One attack flows to the next.”
  • “Stay connected.”
  • “Don’t pause.”
  • “Pressure forward.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after first attack
  • Overcommitting entries
  • Losing posture
  • Standing upright

Drill Ideas

  • Strike-to-shot drill
  • Chain wrestling rounds
  • Continuous entry flow drill

Progression

  • Connect to transitions
  • Connect to cage pressure
