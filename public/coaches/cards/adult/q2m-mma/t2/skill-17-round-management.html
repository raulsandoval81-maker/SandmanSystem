Skill 17 — Round Management

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Start rounds composed and structured

B — Manage pace, pressure, and recovery during work

C — Finish rounds without losing discipline

Coaching Cues

  • “Start smart.”
  • “Manage the pace.”
  • “Stay composed.”
  • “Finish the round.”

Common Mistakes

  • Starting too fast
  • Holding breath
  • Forcing bad exchanges
  • Breaking structure late

Drill Ideas

  • Paced round drill
  • Coach callout round management
  • Finish-the-round pressure drill

Progression

  • Connect to fatigue control
  • Connect to controlled sparring
