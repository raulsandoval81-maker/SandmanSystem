Skill 27 — Ground Awareness

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize threats and openings on the ground

B — Maintain posture, pressure, and positioning

C — Recover safely after transitions or scrambles

Coaching Cues

  • “Know the position.”
  • “Protect first.”
  • “Pressure with awareness.”
  • “Recover position.”

Common Mistakes

  • Losing awareness during scrambles
  • Ignoring submissions
  • Poor posture
  • Leaving space

Drill Ideas

  • Ground reaction rounds
  • Position recognition drill
  • Transition awareness drill

Progression

  • Connect to combination defense
  • Connect to chaos drills
