Skill 24 — Cage Escapes

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build posture against cage pressure

B — Create angle, underhook, or frame to escape

C — Recover center or offensive position

Coaching Cues

  • “Build posture.”
  • “Win the underhook.”
  • “Circle off.”
  • “Don’t stay stuck.”

Common Mistakes

  • Flattening to the cage
  • No underhook fight
  • Standing upright
  • Escaping straight backward

Drill Ideas

  • Cage escape rounds
  • Wall underhook recovery drill
  • Partner cage pressure escape drill

Progression

  • Connect to counter wrestling
  • Connect to second effort
