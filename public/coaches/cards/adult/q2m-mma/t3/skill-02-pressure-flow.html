Skill 02 — Pressure Flow

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply intelligent forward pressure

B — Maintain flow between attacks and movement

C — Recover position after exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure without rushing.”
  • “Flow attack to attack.”
  • “Cut space.”
  • “Stay balanced.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overchasing
  • Breaking stance
  • Pausing after attacks
  • Forcing exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure-flow rounds
  • Forward pressure movement drill
  • Continuous attack drill

Progression

  • Connect to false pressure
  • Connect to live rounds
