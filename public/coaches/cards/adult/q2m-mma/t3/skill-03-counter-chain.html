Skill 03 — Counter Chain

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Defend the initial attack safely

B — Return immediate chained offense

C — Continue pressure or angle out

Coaching Cues

  • “Defend then return.”
  • “Chain the counter.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Recover after exchange.”

Common Mistakes

  • Countering too late
  • Throwing one strike then freezing
  • Dropping hands
  • Ignoring defense

Drill Ideas

  • Slip-counter-chain drill
  • Reaction counter rounds
  • Partner pressure counter drill

Progression

  • Connect to reaction training
  • Connect to live rounds
