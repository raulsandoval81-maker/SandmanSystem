Skill 17 — Live Rounds

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Enter live rounds with a clear skill focus

B — Apply T3 tools under resistance

C — Reset after success or failure

Coaching Cues

  • “Use the skill.”
  • “Stay composed.”
  • “Make decisions.”
  • “Reset and go again.”

Common Mistakes

  • Going wild instead of technical
  • Forgetting the focus
  • Emotional sparring
  • Not resetting after mistakes

Drill Ideas

  • Goal-based live rounds
  • Coach-start live situations
  • Position-limited sparring

Progression

  • Connect to fatigue performance
  • Connect to fight awareness
