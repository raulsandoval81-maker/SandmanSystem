Skill 26 — Ride Control

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Establish top ride position

B — Maintain pressure and control during movement

C — Recover control after escape attempts

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay heavy.”
  • “Control hips first.”
  • “Pressure without rushing.”
  • “Follow movement.”

Common Mistakes

  • Too much space
  • Riding too high
  • Losing hip pressure
  • Overcommitting weight

Drill Ideas

  • Ride pressure drill
  • Partner escape-control rounds
  • Top-follow movement drill

Progression

  • Connect to ground awareness
  • Connect to chaos drills
