Skill 08 — Dirty Boxing

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Strike safely from clinch positions

B — Maintain pressure and inside control

C — Reset clinch structure after exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Short clean shots.”
  • “Head pressure first.”
  • “Stay balanced in the clinch.”
  • “Strike without losing control.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overthrowing punches
  • Losing clinch position
  • Standing too tall
  • Dropping head position

Drill Ideas

  • Clinch punch drill
  • Wall dirty boxing rounds
  • Pummel-to-strike drill

Progression

  • Connect to cage pressure
  • Connect to live rounds
