Skill 06 — Cage Pressure

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Pressure opponents toward the cage

B — Cut escape angles while maintaining balance

C — Attack or control once position is trapped

Coaching Cues

  • “Cut the cage.”
  • “Pressure with control.”
  • “Don’t chase.”
  • “Trap before attacking.”

Common Mistakes

  • Running forward wildly
  • Following instead of trapping
  • Losing stance
  • Allowing easy escapes

Drill Ideas

  • Cage-cutting rounds
  • Pressure movement drill
  • Wall trap partner drill

Progression

  • Connect to clinch chain
  • Connect to cage escapes
