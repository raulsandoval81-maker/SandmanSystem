Skill 23 — Angle Traps

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Force movement with pressure or feints

B — Cut off escape angles

C — Attack when opponent is trapped

Coaching Cues

  • “Trap the angle.”
  • “Cut them off.”
  • “Don’t follow.”
  • “Attack the exit.”

Common Mistakes

  • Following instead of trapping
  • Letting opponent circle free
  • Overcommitting forward
  • Standing square

Drill Ideas

  • Angle trap rounds
  • Partner escape cutoff drill
  • Pressure-to-angle attack drill

Progression

  • Connect to cage pressure
  • Connect to cage escapes
