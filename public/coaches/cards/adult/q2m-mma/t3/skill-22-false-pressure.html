Skill 22 — False Pressure

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Show pressure without fully committing

B — Draw reactions from the opponent

C — Attack, angle, or reset based on the reaction

Coaching Cues

  • “Make them react.”
  • “Don’t overcommit.”
  • “Pressure to create.”
  • “Attack the reaction.”

Common Mistakes

  • Rushing in for real pressure
  • No follow-up after reaction
  • Predictable movement
  • Losing stance while feinting

Drill Ideas

  • False pressure reaction drill
  • Feint-to-entry rounds
  • Partner reaction read drill

Progression

  • Connect to angle traps
  • Connect to counter wrestling
