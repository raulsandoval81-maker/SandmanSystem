Skill 25 — Counter Wrestling

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize offensive openings from opponent attacks

B — Defend and immediately return offense

C — Recover dominant position after the exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “Defend then score.”
  • “Use their pressure.”
  • “React immediately.”
  • “Recover position.”

Common Mistakes

  • Defending without offense
  • Stopping after the sprawl
  • Weak head pressure
  • Standing upright

Drill Ideas

  • Sprawl-to-score drill
  • Counter wrestling rounds
  • Reaction reattack drill

Progression

  • Connect to rescramble
  • Connect to second effort
