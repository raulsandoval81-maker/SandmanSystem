Skill 15 — Pace Break

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Disrupt opponent rhythm and timing

B — Change tempo during exchanges

C — Recover control after breaking pace

Coaching Cues

  • “Change the rhythm.”
  • “Break their timing.”
  • “Control the tempo.”
  • “Reset and pressure.”

Common Mistakes

  • Staying predictable
  • Rushing the break
  • Breaking structure
  • Forcing exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Rhythm disruption drill
  • Partner timing reactions

Progression
