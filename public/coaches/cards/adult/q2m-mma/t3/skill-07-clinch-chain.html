Skill 07 — Clinch Chain

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Enter clinch safely from strikes or pressure

B — Chain control, strikes, and takedowns together

C — Recover dominant position after exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay connected.”
  • “Flow position to attack.”
  • “Head position wins.”
  • “Pressure and control.”

Common Mistakes

  • Loose clinch control
  • Standing upright
  • Stopping after first attack
  • No head pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Pummel-to-attack drill
  • Clinch flow rounds
  • Wall clinch pressure drill

Progression

  • Connect to dirty boxing
  • Connect to advanced shot entries
