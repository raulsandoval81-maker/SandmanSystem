Skill 16 — Weapon Selection

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Read the available opening

B — Choose strike, clinch, shot, or transition

C — Reset decision-making after the exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “Use the right tool.”
  • “Read before attacking.”
  • “Don’t force your favorite move.”
  • “Choose smart.”

Common Mistakes

  • Forcing attacks
  • Ignoring distance
  • Repeating the same weapon
  • Attacking without reading

Drill Ideas

  • Coach-call weapon drill
  • Distance decision rounds
  • Strike-or-shot reaction drill

Progression

  • Connect to live rounds
  • Connect to fight awareness
