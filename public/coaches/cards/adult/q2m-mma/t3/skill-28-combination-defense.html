Skill 28 — Combination Defense

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Defend multiple attacks in sequence

B — Maintain posture and balance under pressure

C — Recover position after the exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay disciplined.”
  • “Defend the full combination.”
  • “Hands recover quickly.”
  • “Move after defense.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after first defense
  • Dropping hands
  • Standing still
  • Breaking posture

Drill Ideas

  • Multi-strike defense rounds
  • Partner combination drill
  • Reaction-defense flow drill

Progression
