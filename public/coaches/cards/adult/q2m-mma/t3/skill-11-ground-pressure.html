Skill 11 — Ground Pressure

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Establish dominant top pressure

B — Maintain connection during movement

C — Recover control after transitions

Coaching Cues

  • “Heavy but balanced.”
  • “Pressure with purpose.”
  • “Stay connected.”
  • “Control before attack.”

Common Mistakes

  • Leaving space
  • Overcommitting weight
  • Losing hip pressure
  • Moving too fast

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure hold rounds
  • Partner escape-control drill
  • Top pressure movement drill

Progression

  • Connect to submission chain
  • Connect to transition control
