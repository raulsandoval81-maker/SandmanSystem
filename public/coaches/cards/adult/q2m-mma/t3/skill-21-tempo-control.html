Skill 21 — Tempo Control

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control the speed of exchanges

B — Change rhythm to create openings

C — Reset tempo after pressure moments

Coaching Cues

  • “Control the beat.”
  • “Change speed.”
  • “Don’t fight one pace.”
  • “Make them react.”

Common Mistakes

  • One-speed fighting
  • Rushing entries
  • Letting opponent dictate pace
  • Poor reset after exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Tempo-change rounds
  • Fast-slow-fast attack drill
  • Coach tempo callout drill

Progression

  • Connect to false pressure
  • Connect to angle traps
