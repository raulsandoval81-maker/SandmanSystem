Skill 13 — Escape Chain

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Defend dangerous positions safely

B — Chain multiple escape attempts together

C — Recover strong position after escape

Coaching Cues

  • “Keep escaping.”
  • “Don’t stop moving.”
  • “Protect first.”
  • “Recover position.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking
  • Stopping after first defense
  • Leaving arms exposed
  • Trying to muscle out

Drill Ideas

  • Escape reaction rounds
  • Submission-defense circuits
  • Continuous escape drill

Progression

  • Connect to transition control
  • Connect to ground awareness
