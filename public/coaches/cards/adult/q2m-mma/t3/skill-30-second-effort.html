Skill 30 — Second Effort

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Continue working after the first attack fails

B — Recover posture and re-engage immediately

C — Finish exchanges with composure and pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Keep working.”
  • “Second effort wins.”
  • “Recover and go again.”
  • “Pressure through failure.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after failure
  • Standing upright
  • Mentally fading
  • Breaking structure

Drill Ideas

  • Reattack rounds
  • Continuous pressure drill
  • Failed-shot recovery drill

Progression

  • Connect to mastery rounds
  • Connect to T4 readiness
