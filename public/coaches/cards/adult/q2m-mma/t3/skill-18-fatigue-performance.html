Skill 18 — Fatigue Performance

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain structure while tired

B — Make simple decisions under fatigue

C — Finish the round without technical collapse

Coaching Cues

  • “Technique stays alive.”
  • “Breathe and work.”
  • “Don’t quit structure.”
  • “Finish clean.”

Common Mistakes

  • Hands dropping
  • Standing tall
  • Sloppy attacks
  • Mentally shutting down

Drill Ideas

  • Fatigue live rounds
  • Technique-under-fatigue circuit
  • Short recovery repeat rounds

Progression

  • Connect to composure
  • Connect to second effort
