Skill 04 — Reaction Training

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize movement and openings early

B — React immediately with correct response

C — Recover structure after reactions

Coaching Cues

  • “See and react.”
  • “Don’t hesitate.”
  • “Stay composed.”
  • “Recover quickly.”

Common Mistakes

  • Freezing under pressure
  • Late reactions
  • Guessing instead of reading
  • Losing balance

Drill Ideas

  • Coach reaction callouts
  • Partner reaction rounds
  • Visual cue movement drill

Progression

  • Connect to counter chain
  • Connect to chaos drills
