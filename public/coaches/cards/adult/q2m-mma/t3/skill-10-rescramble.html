Skill 10 — Rescramble

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recover from failed positions immediately

B — Continue movement during scrambles

C — Rebuild offense or defense after recovery

Coaching Cues

  • “Keep wrestling.”
  • “Recover instantly.”
  • “Don’t stop moving.”
  • “Second effort wins.”

Common Mistakes

  • Quitting after first failure
  • Stopping movement
  • Standing upright
  • Panicking under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Continuous scramble drill
  • Recovery reaction rounds
  • Coach-start rescramble situations

Progression

  • Connect to second effort
  • Connect to chaos drills
