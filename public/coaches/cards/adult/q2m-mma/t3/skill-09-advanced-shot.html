Skill 09 — Advanced Shot

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Enter behind setups, angles, or counters

B — Adjust shots based on reactions

C — Continue chain attacks if first attempt fails

Coaching Cues

  • “Setup before shooting.”
  • “Adjust mid-shot.”
  • “Drive through position.”
  • “Second effort matters.”

Common Mistakes

  • Shooting from too far away
  • Stopping after first defense
  • Poor posture
  • Telegraphing entries

Drill Ideas

  • Reaction shot drill
  • Chain wrestling rounds
  • Setup-to-shot flow drill

Progression

  • Connect to counter wrestling
  • Connect to rescramble situations
