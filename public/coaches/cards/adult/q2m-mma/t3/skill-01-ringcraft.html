Skill 01 — Ringcraft

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control positioning inside the cage

B — Use angles and movement to control exchanges

C — Reset to dominant space after engagement

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the center.”
  • “Cut angles, don’t chase.”
  • “Control the space.”
  • “Move with purpose.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing straight up
  • Following instead of trapping
  • Standing square
  • Giving away center position

Drill Ideas

  • Cage movement rounds
  • Angle-control drill
  • Partner pressure movement drill

Progression

  • Connect to cage pressure
  • Connect to angle traps
