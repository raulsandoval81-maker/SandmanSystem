Skill 19 — Fight Awareness

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize position, time, pressure, and opponent reactions

B — Adjust tactics during exchanges

C — Recover composure after mistakes

Coaching Cues

  • “Know the fight.”
  • “Read the reaction.”
  • “Adjust quickly.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Ignoring position
  • Repeating failed attacks
  • Fighting emotionally
  • Losing awareness under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Coach-change awareness rounds
  • Pattern recognition sparring
  • Position-time-pressure drill

Progression

  • Connect to composure
  • Connect to tempo control
