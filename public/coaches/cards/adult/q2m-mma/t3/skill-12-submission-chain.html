Skill 12 — Submission Chain

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Attack submissions from controlled positions

B — Chain attacks together through reactions

C — Recover control if the finish fails

Coaching Cues

  • “Attack in sequence.”
  • “Control before submission.”
  • “Flow to the next attack.”
  • “Stay patient.”

Common Mistakes

  • Forcing finishes
  • Losing position
  • Attacking too fast
  • Stopping after first attempt

Drill Ideas

  • Submission flow drill
  • Chain attack rounds
  • Reaction finish drill

Progression

  • Connect to escape chain
  • Connect to transition control
