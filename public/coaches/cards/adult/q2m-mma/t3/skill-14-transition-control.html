Skill 14 — Transition Control

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control movement during transitions

B — Maintain posture and pressure through changes

C — Recover dominant position after movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Control before moving.”
  • “Stay connected.”
  • “Pressure through transitions.”
  • “Recover quickly.”

Common Mistakes

  • Moving too fast
  • Leaving space
  • Losing pressure
  • Poor posture

Drill Ideas

  • Transition flow rounds
  • Pressure movement drill
  • Position-switch circuits

Progression

  • Connect to pace break
  • Connect to live grappling
