Skill 16 — Weapon Adaptation

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize when a weapon is being shut down

B — Adapt to another weapon without losing pressure

C — Recover command after the adjustment

Coaching Cues

  • “Adapt the weapon.”
  • “Don’t force one answer.”
  • “Switch with control.”
  • “Stay dangerous everywhere.”

Common Mistakes

  • Forcing the same attack
  • Switching without setup
  • Losing structure during adjustment
  • Ignoring opponent reactions

Drill Ideas

  • Weapon switch rounds
  • Blocked attack adaptation drill
  • Coach-call weapon change drill

Progression

  • Connect to opponent breaking
  • Connect to fight command
