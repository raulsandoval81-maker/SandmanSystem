Skill 10 — Transition Mastery

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Move between phases with control

B — Maintain pressure, posture, and timing through transitions

C — Settle position before advancing again

Coaching Cues

  • “Master the transition.”
  • “Control before moving.”
  • “No loose space.”
  • “Settle then advance.”

Common Mistakes

  • Rushing transitions
  • Leaving space
  • Losing pressure
  • Advancing without control

Drill Ideas

  • Transition mastery rounds
  • Position-flow pressure drill
  • Phase-change control circuits

Progression

  • Connect to scramble command
  • Connect to submission finishing
