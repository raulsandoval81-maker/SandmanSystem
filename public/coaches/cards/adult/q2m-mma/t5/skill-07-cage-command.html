Skill 07 — Cage Command

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Dictate where the fight happens

B — Use pressure and angles to command cage position

C — Reset dominant space after every exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “Command the cage.”
  • “Cut the exits.”
  • “Own the wall.”
  • “Reset center control.”

Common Mistakes

  • Following instead of steering
  • Giving up center
  • Letting opponent circle free
  • Overchasing near the cage

Drill Ideas

  • Cage command rounds
  • Wall trap movement drill
  • Partner escape denial drill

Progression

  • Connect to clinch mastery
  • Connect to pressure layering
