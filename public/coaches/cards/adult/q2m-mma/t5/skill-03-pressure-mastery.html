Skill 03 — Pressure Mastery

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply pressure without chasing or overreaching

B — Layer threats across striking, clinch, and grappling

C — Maintain pressure through opponent reactions

Coaching Cues

  • “Master the pressure.”
  • “Layer the threats.”
  • “Never chase.”
  • “Break structure slowly.”

Common Mistakes

  • Running forward
  • Using one type of pressure
  • Overcommitting after success
  • Losing balance during layers

Drill Ideas

  • Layered pressure rounds
  • Pressure-to-control drill
  • Escape denial pressure drill

Progression

  • Connect to pressure layering
  • Connect to opponent breaking
