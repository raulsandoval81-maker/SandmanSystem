Skill 21 — Pressure Layering

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply multiple pressure layers without forcing

B — Blend strikes, clinch, wrestling, and ground pressure

C — Maintain control through opponent resistance

Coaching Cues

  • “Layer the pressure.”
  • “One threat opens the next.”
  • “Stay connected.”
  • “Pressure with patience.”

Common Mistakes

  • Using one pressure type only
  • Forcing layers too fast
  • Stopping between phases
  • Losing balance during transitions

Drill Ideas

  • Layered pressure rounds
  • Strike-clinch-ground pressure drill
  • Pressure phase circuits

Progression

  • Connect to reaction control
  • Connect to system application
