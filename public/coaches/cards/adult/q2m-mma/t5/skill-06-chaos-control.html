Skill 06 — Chaos Control

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Stay composed during unpredictable exchanges

B — Control position, breathing, and decision-making

C — Recover structure after chaos

Coaching Cues

  • “Control the chaos.”
  • “Breathe and solve.”
  • “Stay technical.”
  • “Recover structure.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking under pressure
  • Freezing in bad positions
  • Wild reactions
  • Stopping after mistakes

Drill Ideas

  • Coach-start chaos rounds
  • Random position recovery drill
  • Scramble-to-control rounds

Progression

  • Connect to high chaos rounds
  • Connect to legacy standard
