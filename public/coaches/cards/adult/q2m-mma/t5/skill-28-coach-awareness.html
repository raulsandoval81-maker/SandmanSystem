Skill 28 — Coach Awareness

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Hear and process coach direction during live work

B — Apply corrections without losing composure

C — Adjust behavior while staying engaged in the round

Coaching Cues

  • “Hear the corner.”
  • “Adjust immediately.”
  • “Stay coachable live.”
  • “Apply the correction.”

Common Mistakes

  • Tuning out coaching
  • Reacting emotionally to correction
  • Stopping completely to listen
  • Failing to apply feedback

Drill Ideas

  • Coach callout live rounds
  • Correction-response drill
  • Corner instruction sparring

Progression

  • Connect to mastery rounds
  • Connect to legacy standard
