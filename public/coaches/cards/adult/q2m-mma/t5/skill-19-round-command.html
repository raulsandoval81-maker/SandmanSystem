Skill 19 — Round Command

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Start the round with clear control

B — Manage pace, pressure, and risk throughout

C — Finish the round with discipline and authority

Coaching Cues

  • “Command the round.”
  • “Control the pace.”
  • “Manage the risk.”
  • “Finish with authority.”

Common Mistakes

  • Starting without plan
  • Letting opponent dictate pace
  • Taking unnecessary risks
  • Fading late in the round

Drill Ideas

  • Command round sparring
  • Start-middle-finish round drill
  • Coach scenario round management

Progression

  • Connect to fight command
  • Connect to mastery rounds
