Skill 08 — Clinch Mastery

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Establish dominant clinch position

B — Control posture, pressure, strikes, and transitions

C — Exit or advance without losing control

Coaching Cues

  • “Master the tie.”
  • “Head position wins.”
  • “Pressure and control.”
  • “Strike without losing position.”

Common Mistakes

  • Loose grips
  • No head pressure
  • Standing too tall
  • Forcing attacks without control

Drill Ideas

  • Clinch mastery rounds
  • Pummel-to-strike drill
  • Wall clinch control rounds

Progression

  • Connect to chain dominance
  • Connect to transition mastery
