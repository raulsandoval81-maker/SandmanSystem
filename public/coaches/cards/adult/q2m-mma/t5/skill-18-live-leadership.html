Skill 18 — Live Leadership

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Lead live rounds with control and composure

B — Set safe pressure and technical standard

C — Help the room improve through example

Coaching Cues

  • “Lead the room.”
  • “Set the standard.”
  • “Control your pressure.”
  • “Make your partner better.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dominating without control
  • Going too hard on weaker partners
  • Forgetting safety leadership
  • Letting ego lead the round

Drill Ideas

  • Leadership sparring rounds
  • Partner-development rounds
  • Controlled pressure coaching drill

Progression

  • Connect to round command
  • Connect to coach awareness
