Skill 11 — Scramble Command

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Stay composed during broken positions

B — Command movement, hips, and recovery

C — Rebuild dominant position after the scramble

Coaching Cues

  • “Command the scramble.”
  • “Win the next position.”
  • “Keep moving with purpose.”
  • “Recover control.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking in motion
  • Stopping after losing position
  • Reaching instead of rebuilding base
  • Giving up control too early

Drill Ideas

  • Coach-start scramble rounds
  • Bad-position recovery drill
  • Scramble-to-control circuits

Progression

  • Connect to submission finishing
  • Connect to high chaos rounds
