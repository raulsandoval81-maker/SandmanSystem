Skill 04 — Range Manipulation

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Pull opponent into chosen ranges

B — Shift range to create attacks or exits

C — Reset range control after exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Move the range.”
  • “Make them reach.”
  • “Enter on your terms.”
  • “Exit before danger.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing in dead range
  • Letting opponent choose distance
  • Reaching instead of moving
  • Failing to reset after contact

Drill Ideas

  • Range bait drill
  • Entry-exit manipulation rounds
  • Partner distance reaction drill

Progression

  • Connect to fight tempo
  • Connect to weapon adaptation
