Skill 09 — Chain Dominance

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Chain attacks across phases without pause

B — Maintain dominance through reactions and resistance

C — Recover control if the chain fails

Coaching Cues

  • “Dominance through chains.”
  • “One attack feeds the next.”
  • “Stay connected.”
  • “Recover and continue.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after first attack
  • Forcing bad transitions
  • Losing posture
  • Breaking rhythm under resistance

Drill Ideas

  • Strike-clinch-ground chain rounds
  • Failed attack recovery drill
  • Continuous dominance circuits

Progression

  • Connect to transition mastery
  • Connect to system application
