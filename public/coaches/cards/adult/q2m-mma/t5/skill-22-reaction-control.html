Skill 22 — Reaction Control

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Force predictable reactions through pressure and feints

B — Control the response with timing and position

C — Attack or reset based on the reaction

Coaching Cues

  • “Control the reaction.”
  • “Make them answer.”
  • “Read the response.”
  • “Attack the opening.”

Common Mistakes

  • Attacking without reading
  • Overcommitting feints
  • Ignoring reactions
  • Letting opponent reset freely

Drill Ideas

  • Reaction-control rounds
  • Feint-to-read drill
  • Partner reaction trap drill

Progression

  • Connect to pace manipulation
  • Connect to instinct refinement
