Skill 14 — Defensive Composure

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Stay calm while defending pressure

B — Protect structure, position, and breathing

C — Return to offense after defensive recovery

Coaching Cues

  • “Defend calmly.”
  • “Don’t panic.”
  • “Breathe and protect.”
  • “Return with control.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking during defense
  • Freezing under pressure
  • Defending without breathing
  • Failing to return offense

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure defense rounds
  • Composure-under-attack drill
  • Defense-to-reset circuits

Progression

  • Connect to fatigue discipline
  • Connect to fight command
