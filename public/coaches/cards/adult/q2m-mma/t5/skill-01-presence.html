Skill 01 — Presence

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Enter rounds with calm control

B — Maintain posture, pressure, and awareness

C — Set the tone without forcing action

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the room.”
  • “Calm pressure.”
  • “Presence before attack.”
  • “Lead the exchange.”

Common Mistakes

  • Trying to prove too much
  • Rushing early exchanges
  • Breaking posture under pressure
  • Letting opponent set the tone

Drill Ideas

  • Presence sparring rounds
  • Calm pressure entry drill
  • Coach-observed control rounds

Progression

  • Connect to command
  • Connect to fight command
