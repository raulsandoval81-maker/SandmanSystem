Skill 23 — Pace Manipulation

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Change pace to disrupt rhythm

B — Slow, burst, pause, or pressure to create openings

C — Reset tempo after the exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “Manipulate the pace.”
  • “Change the beat.”
  • “Make them uncomfortable.”
  • “Control the rhythm.”

Common Mistakes

  • Fighting one speed
  • Telegraphing tempo changes
  • Rushing the burst
  • Losing structure during pace shifts

Drill Ideas

  • Pace manipulation rounds
  • Slow-fast-slow drill
  • Coach tempo command sparring

Progression

  • Connect to system application
  • Connect to high chaos rounds
