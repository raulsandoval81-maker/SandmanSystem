Skill 02 — Command

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Take control of pace, space, and pressure

B — Direct exchanges with confidence and structure

C — Reset command after resistance or chaos

Coaching Cues

  • “Command the round.”
  • “Dictate the terms.”
  • “Stay composed.”
  • “Lead with control.”

Common Mistakes

  • Waiting too long to take control
  • Forcing command with emotion
  • Losing structure while pressuring
  • Letting opponent dictate pace

Drill Ideas

  • Command rounds
  • Start-fast control drill
  • Coach-call dictate pace drill

Progression

  • Connect to pressure mastery
  • Connect to round command
