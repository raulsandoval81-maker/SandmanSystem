Skill 17 — Opponent Breaking

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Identify when opponent structure is weakening

B — Increase pressure without getting reckless

C — Break rhythm, posture, or confidence safely

Coaching Cues

  • “Break the opponent.”
  • “Pressure with discipline.”
  • “Do not rush the break.”
  • “Stay composed while they fade.”

Common Mistakes

  • Getting reckless after advantage
  • Missing fatigue signs
  • Forcing wild finishes
  • Letting opponent recover freely

Drill Ideas

  • Opponent-break pressure rounds
  • Late-round structure break drill
  • Pressure escalation circuits

Progression

  • Connect to round command
  • Connect to fight command
