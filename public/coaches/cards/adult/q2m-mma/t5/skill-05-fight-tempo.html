Skill 05 — Fight Tempo

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T5

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control rhythm, pace, and timing of exchanges

B — Change tempo to create reactions

C — Reset tempo after pressure or chaos

Coaching Cues

  • “Control the fight tempo.”
  • “Change the beat.”
  • “Make them react.”
  • “Don’t fight one speed.”

Common Mistakes

  • One-speed fighting
  • Rushing exchanges
  • Letting opponent dictate rhythm
  • Poor reset after tempo change

Drill Ideas

  • Tempo-control rounds
  • Fast-slow-fast drill
  • Coach tempo callout sparring

Progression

  • Connect to pace manipulation
  • Connect to fight command
