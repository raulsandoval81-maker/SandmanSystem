Skill 03 — Distance

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize safe striking and grappling range

B — Enter and exit distance with control

C — Reset spacing after exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the space.”
  • “Don’t crowd yourself.”
  • “Move before reaching.”
  • “Reset the distance.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing too close
  • Reaching with strikes
  • Backing straight up
  • Freezing in range

Drill Ideas

  • Distance touch drill
  • Entry and exit rounds
  • Partner range movement drill

Progression

  • Connect to striking entries
  • Connect to takedown setups
