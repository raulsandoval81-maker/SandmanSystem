Skill 09 — Get Up

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build safe base from the ground

B — Protect while standing up

C — Recover stance immediately after standing

Coaching Cues

  • “Build your base.”
  • “Protect while standing.”
  • “Don’t rush up.”
  • “Stand balanced.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing straight up
  • Ignoring defense
  • Poor base
  • Turning away

Drill Ideas

  • Technical stand-up drill
  • Wall get-up rounds
  • Partner pressure stand-up drill

Progression

  • Connect to recovery defense
  • Connect to live scramble situations
