Skill 01 — Stance

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Athletic MMA stance with balanced base

B — Hands ready for striking and defense

C — Reset stance after movement or contact

Coaching Cues

  • “Hands ready.”
  • “Feet under you.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Ready to move.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing too tall
  • Feet too narrow
  • Hands dropping
  • Heavy front foot

Drill Ideas

  • Mirror stance drill
  • Coach movement callouts
  • Shadow movement rounds

Progression

  • Connect to motion
  • Connect to striking entries
