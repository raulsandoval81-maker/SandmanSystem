Skill 13 — Takedown

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Change levels with posture and balance

B — Penetrate safely into the takedown

C — Finish and recover position

Coaching Cues

  • “Level change first.”
  • “Drive through.”
  • “Head up.”
  • “Finish strong.”

Common Mistakes

  • Bending at the waist
  • Shooting too far away
  • Weak penetration step
  • Stopping mid-shot

Drill Ideas

  • Penetration step drill
  • Partner entry reps
  • Controlled takedown finishes

Progression

  • Connect to clinch setups
  • Connect to top control
