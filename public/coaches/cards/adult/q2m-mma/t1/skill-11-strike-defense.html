Skill 11 — Strike Defense

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Protect head and body during exchanges

B — Defend while maintaining balance and vision

C — Recover position after defending

Coaching Cues

  • “Hands home.”
  • “Eyes forward.”
  • “Protect and move.”
  • “Recover your stance.”

Common Mistakes

  • Closing eyes
  • Standing still
  • Dropping hands
  • Panicking under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Partner touch defense drill
  • Block-and-move rounds
  • Controlled defense reactions

Progression

  • Connect to counters
  • Connect to live exchanges
