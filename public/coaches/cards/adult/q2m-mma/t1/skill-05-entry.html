Skill 05 — Entry

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Enter safely behind movement or strikes

B — Maintain balance while closing distance

C — Reset after the entry exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “Enter with purpose.”
  • “Protect yourself entering.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Don’t dive in.”

Common Mistakes

  • Reaching forward
  • Entering square
  • Diving without setup
  • Losing balance

Drill Ideas

  • Jab entry drill
  • Partner touch entry rounds
  • Controlled entry movement drill

Progression

  • Connect to clinch entries
  • Connect to takedown attempts
