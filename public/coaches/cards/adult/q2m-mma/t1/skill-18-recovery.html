Skill 18 — Recovery

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recover breathing after hard exchanges

B — Rebuild stance and posture quickly

C — Return mentally focused for the next rep

Coaching Cues

  • “Recover with purpose.”
  • “Control your breathing.”
  • “Rebuild your posture.”
  • “Ready for the next rep.”

Common Mistakes

  • Hands on knees
  • Poor breathing
  • Losing focus
  • Standing flat-footed

Drill Ideas

  • Breathing reset drill
  • Short recovery rounds
  • Recovery between circuits

Progression

  • Connect to conditioning
  • Connect to round management
