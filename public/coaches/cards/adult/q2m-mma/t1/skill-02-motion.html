Skill 02 — Motion

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Move while maintaining stance integrity

B — Step forward, backward, and laterally

C — Reset balance after movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Move under control.”
  • “Don’t cross feet.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Ready to react.”

Common Mistakes

  • Crossing feet
  • Standing upright
  • Overstepping
  • Losing stance while moving

Drill Ideas

  • Circle movement drill
  • Coach directional callouts
  • Shadow motion rounds

Progression

  • Connect to distance management
  • Connect to entries and exits
