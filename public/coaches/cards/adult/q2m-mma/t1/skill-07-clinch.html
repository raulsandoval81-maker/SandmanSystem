Skill 07 — Clinch

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Establish inside position and posture

B — Apply pressure while maintaining balance

C — Reset control after movement or breaks

Coaching Cues

  • “Inside control.”
  • “Head position matters.”
  • “Pressure forward.”
  • “Stay balanced.”

Common Mistakes

  • Head too high
  • Weak posture
  • Standing square
  • Overreaching arms

Drill Ideas

  • Pummel drill
  • Clinch pressure rounds
  • Head position battles

Progression

  • Connect to takedowns
  • Connect to wall control
