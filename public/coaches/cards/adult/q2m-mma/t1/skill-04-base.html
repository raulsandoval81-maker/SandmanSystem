Skill 04 — Base

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build strong athletic posture

B — Maintain balance under pressure

C — Recover base after contact or movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Strong base.”
  • “Knees bent.”
  • “Hips under you.”
  • “Recover quickly.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing too tall
  • Leaning forward
  • Feet too narrow
  • Weak posture

Drill Ideas

  • Partner pressure drill
  • Balance recovery rounds
  • Base movement circuit

Progression

  • Connect to clinch
  • Connect to takedown defense
