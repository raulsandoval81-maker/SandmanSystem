Skill 16 — Pressure

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply controlled forward pressure

B — Maintain balance while advancing

C — Reset position after exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure with control.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Cut space down.”
  • “Don’t chase wildly.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overcommitting forward
  • Crossing feet
  • Standing square
  • Losing posture

Drill Ideas

  • Forward pressure rounds
  • Partner retreat drill
  • Cage-cutting movement drill

Progression

  • Connect to clinch pressure
  • Connect to live control exchanges
