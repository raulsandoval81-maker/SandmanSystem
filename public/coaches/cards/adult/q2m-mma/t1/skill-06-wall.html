Skill 06 — Wall

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build safe posture against the wall

B — Maintain balance and pressure while pinned

C — Escape and reset position safely

Coaching Cues

  • “Strong posture.”
  • “Don’t flatten out.”
  • “Fight for position.”
  • “Move off the wall.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing upright
  • Leaning backward
  • Stopping movement
  • Giving up under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Wall pressure drill
  • Wall escape rounds
  • Partner pin-and-recover drill

Progression

  • Connect to clinch control
  • Connect to wall escapes
