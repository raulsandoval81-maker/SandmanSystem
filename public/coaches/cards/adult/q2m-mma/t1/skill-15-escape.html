Skill 15 — Escape

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build safe frames and movement

B — Create space before escaping

C — Recover position after escape

Coaching Cues

  • “Frame first.”
  • “Move your hips.”
  • “Create space.”
  • “Recover immediately.”

Common Mistakes

  • Pushing without frames
  • Flat hips
  • Panicking
  • Giving up position

Drill Ideas

  • Hip escape drill
  • Frame-and-recover rounds
  • Partner pressure escapes

Progression

  • Connect to guard recovery
  • Connect to standing escapes
