Skill 08 — Sprawl

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Drop hips backward against takedown pressure

B — Maintain chest pressure and balance

C — Recover stance after defense

Coaching Cues

  • “Heavy hips.”
  • “Chest pressure.”
  • “Legs back fast.”
  • “Recover your stance.”

Common Mistakes

  • Slow hip movement
  • Standing upright
  • Weak chest pressure
  • Not recovering position

Drill Ideas

  • Reaction sprawl drill
  • Partner shot defense drill
  • Sprawl-and-reset rounds

Progression

  • Connect to front head control
  • Connect to takedown defense chains
