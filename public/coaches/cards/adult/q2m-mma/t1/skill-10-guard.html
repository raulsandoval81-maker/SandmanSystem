Skill 10 — Guard

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Establish safe guard position on the ground

B — Maintain posture control and defense

C — Recover guard after movement or pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Protect yourself.”
  • “Control posture.”
  • “Active guard.”
  • “Stay connected.”

Common Mistakes

  • Flat hips
  • Loose legs
  • No posture control
  • Stopping movement

Drill Ideas

  • Guard retention drill
  • Posture control rounds
  • Partner pressure guard drill

Progression

  • Connect to escapes
  • Connect to submissions later
