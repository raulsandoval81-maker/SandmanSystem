Skill 14 — Top Control

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Establish strong top position

B — Apply pressure while maintaining balance

C — Recover control during movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Heavy pressure.”
  • “Stay connected.”
  • “Control before moving.”
  • “Balanced pressure.”

Common Mistakes

  • Sitting too high
  • No pressure
  • Overcommitting forward
  • Losing connection

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure hold drill
  • Partner top-control rounds
  • Balance recovery drill

Progression

  • Connect to ground striking
  • Connect to positional control
