Skill 12 — Basic Combo

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Chain simple strikes together safely

B — Maintain balance during combinations

C — Reset stance after the exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “Flow together.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Hands recover.”
  • “Move after striking.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overthrowing punches
  • Standing square
  • Dropping hands
  • Freezing after combinations

Drill Ideas

  • Pad combination rounds
  • Shadow combo drill
  • Partner combo flow drill

Progression

  • Connect to entries
  • Connect to live striking
