Skill 17 — Conditioning

Journey: Adult Q2M — MMA Based

Tier: Adult T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain movement under fatigue

B — Preserve technique while tired

C — Recover posture and breathing between rounds

Coaching Cues

  • “Breathe and move.”
  • “Technique stays clean.”
  • “Don’t break posture.”
  • “Push with control.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing upright
  • Sloppy technique
  • Holding breath
  • Quitting movement

Drill Ideas

  • Shark-bait conditioning rounds
  • Movement circuits
  • Technique-under-fatigue drill

Progression

  • Connect to live rounds
  • Connect to recovery discipline
