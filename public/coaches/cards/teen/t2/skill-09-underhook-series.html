Skill 9 — Underhook Series

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Teen T2 — Champion

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Secure Underhook

B — Control Position

C — Attack From Underhook

Coaching Cues

  • “Elbow up, chest in”
  • “Control before attack”
  • “Score off position”

Common Mistakes

  • No pressure
  • Not attacking from position

Drill Ideas

  • Underhook entries
  • Underhook to finish drill
  • Live underhook rounds

Progression

  • Chain into throws / singles
