Skill 22 — Leg Ride Intro

Tree: Top

Tier: Teen T2 — Champion

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Insert Leg

B — Control Hips

C — Maintain Balance

Coaching Cues

  • “Hooks in tight”
  • “Control hips first”
  • “Stay balanced on top”

Common Mistakes

  • Loose leg insertion
  • Losing balance to the side
  • Trying to turn before control

Drill Ideas

  • Leg ride entry reps
  • Top balance drills
  • Leg ride control holds

Progression

  • Add leg ride turns
  • Connect to turn chain
