Skill 2 — Angle Control

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Teen T2 — Champion

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create Angle

B — Maintain Angle

C — Attack From Angle

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t stay in front”
  • “Step off line”
  • “Score from the side”

Common Mistakes

  • Attacking straight on
  • Overstepping and losing base
  • Not using angle to finish

Drill Ideas

  • Angle step reps
  • Circle to attack drill
  • Angle to finish rounds

Progression

  • Connect to setups
  • Connect to chain wrestling
