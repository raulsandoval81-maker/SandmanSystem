Skill 16 — Front Headlock to Finish

Tree: Neutral Defense

Tier: Teen T4 — Legend

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Secure Front Headlock

B — Apply Heavy Pressure

C — Finish the Score

Coaching Cues

  • “Head down, hips heavy”
  • “Control first”
  • “Finish from the front”

Common Mistakes

  • Loose front headlock
  • No pressure through the position
  • Letting the opponent recover underneath

Drill Ideas

  • Front headlock control reps
  • Spin-behind finish drills
  • Live front headlock rounds

Progression

  • Connect to shot shutdown
