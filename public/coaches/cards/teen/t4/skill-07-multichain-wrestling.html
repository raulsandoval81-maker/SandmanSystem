Skill 7 — Multi-Chain Wrestling

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Teen T4 — Legend

Structure (A/B/C)

A — First Attack

B — Chain Reactions

C — Continuous Scoring

Coaching Cues

  • “Never stop attacking”
  • “Flow through positions”
  • “Score in sequence”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after 1–2 attacks
  • Forcing instead of flowing
  • Breaking rhythm

Drill Ideas

  • 3–5 chain attack drills
  • Continuous chain rounds
  • Live flow wrestling

Progression

  • Full match integration
