Skill 17 — Whizzer System

Tree: Neutral Defense

Tier: Teen T4 — Legend

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Insert Whizzer

B — Apply Pressure

C — Recover or Counter

Coaching Cues

  • “Heavy overhook”
  • “Pressure down and in”
  • “Recover with control”

Common Mistakes

  • Loose whizzer
  • No hip pressure
  • Standing tall on defense

Drill Ideas

  • Whizzer reps
  • Single-leg defense situations
  • Whizzer to counter drills

Progression

  • Connect to counter domination
