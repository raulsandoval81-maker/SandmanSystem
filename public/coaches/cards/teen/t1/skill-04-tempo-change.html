Skill 04 — Tempo Change

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Teen T1 — Warrior

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Slow Control

B — Sudden Speed

C — Explode to Attack

Coaching Cues

  • “Slow… then go”
  • “Change speeds”
  • “Explode on cue”

Common Mistakes

  • Same speed always
  • No explosion
  • Telegraphing attack

Drill Ideas

  • Tempo drills
  • Slow-fast shot entries
  • Fake slow → real go

Progression

  • Add to motion attacks
