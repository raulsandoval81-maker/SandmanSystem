Skill 08 — Snap Down to Score

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Teen T1 — Warrior

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control Head

B — Snap Down

C — Go Behind

Coaching Cues

  • “Snap hard”
  • “Move the head”
  • “Circle behind”

Common Mistakes

  • Weak snap
  • No movement after snap
  • Standing up

Drill Ideas

  • Snap reps
  • Snap to go-behind drill
  • Front headlock entries

Progression

  • Add front headlock control
