Skill — Breakdown

Tree: Top

Tier: Teen T0 — Apprentice

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Position

B — Pressure

C — Flatten

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay behind hips”
  • “Pressure through the chest”
  • “Break him down before you ride fancy”

Common Mistakes

  • Too high on the back
  • No forward pressure
  • Trying to turn before flattening

Drill Ideas

  • Top position breakdown reps
  • Breakdown to ride drill
  • Partner flatten drill

Progression

  • Add spiral ride
  • Add turn setup after flatten
