Skill — Defense to Offense

Tree: Neutral Defense

Tier: Teen T0 — Apprentice

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Defend

B — Control

C — Re-attack

Coaching Cues

  • “Stop the first shot first”
  • “Find control before scoring”
  • “Attack back fast”

Common Mistakes

  • Trying to re-attack before securing defense
  • Stopping the shot but not controlling opponent
  • Resetting too slowly

Drill Ideas

  • Defend and re-shot drill
  • Shot-stop-score drill
  • Partner reaction rounds

Progression

  • Add chain re-attacks
  • Add live short goes
