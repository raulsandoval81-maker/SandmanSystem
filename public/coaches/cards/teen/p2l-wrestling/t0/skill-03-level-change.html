Skill 3 — Level Change

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Teen T0 — Apprentice

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Drop hips

B — Keep chest up

C — Recover stance

Coaching Cues

  • “Bend knees, not waist”
  • “Head up”
  • “Quick down, quick up”

Common Mistakes

  • Bending at waist
  • Dropping head
  • Slow recovery

Drill Ideas

  • Level change reps
  • Shot fake drill
  • Coach clap reaction

Progression

  • Add penetration step
  • Connect to shots
