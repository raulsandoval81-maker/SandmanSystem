Skill 14 — Ride Control

Tree: Top

Tier: Teen T0 — Apprentice

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Position Control

B — Hand Control

C — Maintain Ride

Coaching Cues

  • “Heavy hips, no space”
  • “Stay behind”
  • “Control first”

Common Mistakes

  • Too high on the back
  • Reaching and losing control
  • Losing hip pressure

Drill Ideas

  • 10–20 sec ride hold
  • Wrist control + follow
  • Base vs ride control

Progression

  • Add breakdown
  • Add return
