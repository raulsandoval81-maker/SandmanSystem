Skill — Sprawl

Tree: Neutral Defense

Tier: Teen T0 — Apprentice

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Hips down

B — Legs back

C — Recover

Coaching Cues

  • “Heavy hips, fast feet”
  • “Chest pressure on shoulders”
  • “Recover to stance, don’t stay dead”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing legs back with no hip pressure
  • Staying extended too long
  • Not recovering after the stop

Drill Ideas

  • Shadow sprawl reps
  • Partner shot to sprawl drill
  • Sprawl and recover drill

Progression

  • Add spin behind
  • Add front headlock control
