Skill 1 — Stance Integrity

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Teen T0 — Apprentice

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Proper posture

B — Balanced base

C — Ready to move

Coaching Cues

  • “Chest up, eyes forward”
  • “Weight on balls of feet”
  • “Hands ready, not reaching”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing too tall
  • Leaning forward
  • Flat feet

Drill Ideas

  • Mirror stance drill
  • Stance hold (10–20 sec)
  • Coach pressure checks

Progression

  • Add motion while holding stance
  • Add reaction drills
