Skill — Distance Awareness

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Teen T0 — Apprentice

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Feel range

B — Adjust feet

C — Enter safely

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t reach from too far”
  • “Close with your feet first”
  • “Know when you are in scoring range”

Common Mistakes

  • Reaching with hands before feet move
  • Standing too close and getting tied up badly
  • Shooting from too far away

Drill Ideas

  • Partner range check drill
  • Shadow entries with stop points
  • Coach call: too far / too close / right distance

Progression

  • Add motion into range
  • Add attack from correct distance
