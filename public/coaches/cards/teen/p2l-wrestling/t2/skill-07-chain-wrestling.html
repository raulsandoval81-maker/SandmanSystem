Skill 7 — Chain Wrestling

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Teen T2 — Champion

Structure (A/B/C)

A — First Attack

B — Reaction Read

C — Second / Third Attack

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t stop at one”
  • “Flow to the next attack”
  • “Score off reactions”

Common Mistakes

  • Single shot only
  • Pausing between attacks
  • Forcing instead of flowing

Drill Ideas

  • 2–3 attack chains
  • Shot → finish → re-attack
  • Live chain-only rounds

Progression

  • Connect to all offensive systems
