Skill 6 — Motion to Re-Attack

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Teen T2 — Champion

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create Movement

B — Draw Reaction

C — Re-Attack Opening

Coaching Cues

  • “Move him to get your shot”
  • “First attack creates the second”
  • “Don’t stop after the first attempt”

Common Mistakes

  • One-shot mindset
  • No setup before re-attack
  • Stopping after contact

Drill Ideas

  • Shot → re-shot drill
  • Fake → attack → re-attack
  • Continuous motion rounds

Progression

  • Connect to chain wrestling
