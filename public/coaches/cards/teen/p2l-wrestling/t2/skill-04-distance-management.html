Skill 4 — Distance Management

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Teen T2 — Champion

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control Distance

B — Close or Create Space

C — Attack at Correct Range

Coaching Cues

  • “Know your distance”
  • “Too far, don’t shoot”
  • “Close before attack”

Common Mistakes

  • Shooting from space
  • Standing too close without control
  • Poor spacing in ties

Drill Ideas

  • Distance reaction drills
  • In/out movement drills
  • Distance to shot entries

Progression

  • Connect to setups
  • Connect to tempo shift
