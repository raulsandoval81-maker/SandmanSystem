Skill 20 — Turn Flow

Tree: Top

Tier: Teen T4 — Legend

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create First Turn Threat

B — Read Reaction

C — Flow to Next Score

Coaching Cues

  • “One turn leads to the next”
  • “Flow, don’t pause”
  • “Keep pressure through the chain”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after first attempt
  • Losing pressure between turns
  • Forcing instead of flowing

Drill Ideas

  • Turn chain rounds
  • Reaction-based turn drills
  • Live top scoring rounds

Progression

  • Connect to chain turns
