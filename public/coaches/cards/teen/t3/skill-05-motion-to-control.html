Skill 5 — Motion to Control

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Teen T3 — Veteran

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create Movement

B — Direct Opponent

C — Establish Control

Coaching Cues

  • “Move him where you want”
  • “Control through motion”
  • “Set the position”

Common Mistakes

  • Moving without purpose
  • No control after motion
  • Chasing instead of directing

Drill Ideas

  • Motion control drills
  • Push-pull direction drills
  • Live control rounds

Progression

  • Connect to tie systems
