Skill 10 — 2-on-1 Series

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Teen T3 — Veteran

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Secure Control

B — Break Position

C — Attack From Control

Coaching Cues

  • “Control the arm tight”
  • “Keep it close to your body”
  • “Move to score”

Common Mistakes

  • Loose grip
  • No movement after control
  • Standing still

Drill Ideas

  • 2-on-1 control reps
  • 2-on-1 to finish drill
  • Live tie rounds

Progression

  • Connect to chain wrestling
