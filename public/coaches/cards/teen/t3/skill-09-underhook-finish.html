Skill 9 — Underhook to Finish

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Teen T3 — Veteran

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Secure Underhook

B — Control & Elevate

C — Finish Attack

Coaching Cues

  • “Elbow up, chest in”
  • “Control the position”
  • “Finish clean”

Common Mistakes

  • Loose underhook
  • No elevation or pressure
  • Not finishing the attack

Drill Ideas

  • Underhook to finish reps
  • Underhook live rounds
  • Position to score drill

Progression

  • Connect to upper body system
