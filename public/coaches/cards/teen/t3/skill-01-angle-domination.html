Skill 1 — Angle Domination

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Teen T3 — Veteran

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create Angle

B — Maintain Angle

C — Control From Angle

Coaching Cues

  • “Never stay square”
  • “Own the angle”
  • “Control from the side”

Common Mistakes

  • Returning to neutral position
  • Overstepping and losing base
  • Not using angle to control opponent

Drill Ideas

  • Angle control rounds
  • Circle + control drills
  • Angle to finish reps

Progression

  • Connect to tie domination
