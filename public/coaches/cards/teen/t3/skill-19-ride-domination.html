Skill 19 — Ride Domination

Tree: Top

Tier: Teen T3 — Veteran

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Establish Control

B — Apply Pressure

C — Break Opponent Down

Coaching Cues

  • “No space anywhere”
  • “Heavy hips, constant pressure”
  • “Break him before you turn him”

Common Mistakes

  • Riding too high
  • Losing pressure in transitions
  • Trying to turn too early

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure ride rounds
  • Breakdown to control drill
  • Continuous top pressure drill

Progression

  • Connect to turn system
