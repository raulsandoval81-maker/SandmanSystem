Skill 7 — Chain Wrestling (3–4 Attacks)

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Teen T3 — Veteran

Structure (A/B/C)

A — First Attack

B — Read Reaction

C — 2nd–4th Attack Chain

Coaching Cues

  • “3 moves minimum”
  • “Flow, don’t force”
  • “Score on the chain, not the first”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after 1–2 attempts
  • Forcing the same attack repeatedly
  • Pausing between movements

Drill Ideas

  • 3-attack chain drill
  • Continuous chain rounds
  • Reaction-based chain reps

Progression

  • Connect to full match flow
