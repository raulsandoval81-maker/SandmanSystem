Skill 10 — Range Control

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Understand when you are outside, inside, or in danger range

B — Use feet, jab, and guard to manage distance

C — Reset range after every exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the distance.”
  • “Jab controls space.”
  • “Move before you’re stuck.”
  • “Reset the range.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing in danger range too long
  • Backing straight up
  • Reaching instead of stepping
  • Letting the opponent set distance

Drill Ideas

  • Range touch partner drill
  • Jab distance control rounds
  • In-out movement sparring games

Progression

  • Connect to Ring Position
  • Connect to Distance Traps
