Skill 16 — Ring Position

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Understand center, edge, corner, and escape lanes

B — Use footwork to improve position during rounds

C — Avoid giving up space without purpose

Coaching Cues

  • “Know where you are.”
  • “Win position.”
  • “Don’t drift to the ropes.”
  • “Move with purpose.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing into bad space
  • Ignoring corners and boundaries
  • Following instead of steering
  • Not resetting to center

Drill Ideas

  • Center-control sparring games
  • Corner escape drills
  • Ring-position awareness rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Range Control
  • Connect to Ring Awareness
