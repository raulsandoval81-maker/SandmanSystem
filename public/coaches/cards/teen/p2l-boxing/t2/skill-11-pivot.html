Skill 11 — Pivot

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Pivot without crossing stance

B — Create a new angle after contact or pressure

C — Finish balanced and ready to punch or defend

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn off the lead foot.”
  • “Keep your stance.”
  • “Angle after contact.”
  • “Don’t hop.”

Common Mistakes

  • Jumping instead of pivoting
  • Crossing feet
  • Dropping hands while turning
  • Finishing square

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-pivot drills
  • Partner pressure pivot exits
  • Bag pivot angle rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Exit Angle
  • Connect to Ring Position
