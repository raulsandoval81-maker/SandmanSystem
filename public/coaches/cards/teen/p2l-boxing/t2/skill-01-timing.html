Skill 01 — Timing

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Read the opponent’s rhythm before attacking

B — Strike during openings, not randomly

C — Reset position after the timed action

Coaching Cues

  • “See the beat.”
  • “Punch on time.”
  • “Don’t force it.”
  • “Reset after contact.”

Common Mistakes

  • Guessing instead of reading
  • Throwing late
  • Rushing the opening
  • Staying in range after punching

Drill Ideas

  • Partner rhythm-read drill
  • Timed jab entry rounds
  • Call-and-counter timing rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Rhythm
  • Connect to Counter
