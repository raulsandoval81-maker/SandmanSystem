Skill 12 — Exit Angle

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Exit after attacking or defending

B — Move off the center line instead of straight back

C — Reset stance at a safer angle

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t leave straight back.”
  • “Angle out.”
  • “Move after contact.”
  • “Reset safe.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing straight up
  • Admiring punches after landing
  • Crossing feet on exit
  • Exiting with hands low

Drill Ideas

  • Combo-exit angle drill
  • Defense-to-angle rounds
  • Partner pressure exit games

Progression

  • Connect to Pivot
  • Connect to Ring Position
