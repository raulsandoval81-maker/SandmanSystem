Skill 04 — Counter

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize offensive openings during exchanges

B — Counter immediately from defensive position

C — Exit or reset after the counter lands

Coaching Cues

  • “Defend then answer.”
  • “Counter on time.”
  • “Stay sharp.”
  • “Recover after the shot.”

Common Mistakes

  • Countering too late
  • Ignoring defense first
  • Throwing wild responses
  • Standing in range afterward

Drill Ideas

  • Slip-counter partner drill
  • Defense-to-counter rounds
  • Coach call counter reactions

Progression

  • Connect to Cross Counter
  • Connect to Hook Counter
