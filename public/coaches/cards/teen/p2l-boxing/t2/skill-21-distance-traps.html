Skill 21 — Distance Traps

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create reactions by controlling range and pressure

B — Bait predictable movement patterns safely

C — Punish openings while staying defensively sound

Coaching Cues

  • “Make them reach.”
  • “Control the space.”
  • “Set the trap first.”
  • “Counter clean.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing too close to bait safely
  • Reaching during counters
  • Backing straight up
  • Ignoring defensive recovery

Drill Ideas

  • Step-back counter drills
  • Range bait partner rounds
  • Distance trap sparring games

Progression

  • Connect to Range Control
  • Connect to Pressure Sparring
