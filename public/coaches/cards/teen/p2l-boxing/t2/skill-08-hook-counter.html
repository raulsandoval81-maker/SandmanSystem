Skill 08 — Hook Counter

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Defend or slip into safe hook range

B — Return the hook counter with tight mechanics

C — Reset defense before continuing pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Tight hook.”
  • “Don’t swing wide.”
  • “Counter from your base.”
  • “Defense comes back.”

Common Mistakes

  • Looping the hook wide
  • Throwing off balance
  • Dropping the opposite hand
  • Standing square after the counter

Drill Ideas

  • Block-hook counter drill
  • Slip-hook counter rounds
  • Partner counter flow drill

Progression

  • Connect to Counter
  • Connect to Hook Counter
