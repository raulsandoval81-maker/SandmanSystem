Skill 17 — Controlled Sparring

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Live

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply learned skills with control and respect

B — Maintain safety, composure, and technical habits

C — Learn from live reactions without escalating intensity

Coaching Cues

  • “Controlled means controlled.”
  • “Use your skills.”
  • “Take care of your partner.”
  • “Learn, don’t fight.”

Common Mistakes

  • Turning controlled sparring into fighting
  • Abandoning defense under pressure
  • Trying to win instead of improve
  • Ignoring coach pace

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-only controlled sparring
  • Defense-first sparring rounds
  • Specific-task sparring games

Progression

  • Connect to Live Application
  • Connect to Pressure Sparring
