Skill 15 — Combination Flow

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Link punches together with balance and rhythm

B — Flow between offense, defense, and movement

C — Finish combinations with position and control

Coaching Cues

  • “Flow, don’t force.”
  • “Hands and feet together.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Finish in stance.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing too fast and sloppy
  • Stopping after the last punch
  • Standing square during combinations
  • Dropping hands between punches

Drill Ideas

  • Combination flow bag rounds
  • Partner call-and-flow drills
  • Shadowboxing flow rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Combination Entries
  • Connect to Live Application
