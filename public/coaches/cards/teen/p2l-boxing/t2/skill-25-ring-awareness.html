Skill 25 — Ring Awareness

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Understand positioning relative to ropes, corners, and center

B — Make smart movement decisions during live exchanges

C — Control space while maintaining composure and defense

Coaching Cues

  • “Know where you are.”
  • “Protect center space.”
  • “Don’t drift into corners.”
  • “Move with awareness.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing into ropes carelessly
  • Ignoring corner danger
  • Following instead of steering
  • Losing awareness during exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Center-control sparring games
  • Corner escape rounds
  • Ring-awareness movement drills

Progression

  • Connect to Pressure Sparring
  • Connect to T3 Boxing Readiness
