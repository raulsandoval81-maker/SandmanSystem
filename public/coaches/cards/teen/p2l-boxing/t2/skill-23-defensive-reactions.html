Skill 23 — Defensive Reactions

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize pressure and incoming attacks early

B — Respond with proper defensive movement

C — Recover stance and awareness immediately after defense

Coaching Cues

  • “See it early.”
  • “Defend with purpose.”
  • “Recover fast.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking under pressure
  • Closing eyes during exchanges
  • Freezing after defense
  • Dropping guard while reacting

Drill Ideas

  • Coach call reaction rounds
  • Defense-only sparring drills
  • Partner pressure reaction games

Progression

  • Connect to Defense Reset
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
