Skill 06 — Jab Entry

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Enter range safely behind the jab

B — Maintain balance while closing distance

C — Continue offense or angle out after entry

Coaching Cues

  • “Enter behind the jab.”
  • “Feet with hands.”
  • “Protect the chin.”
  • “Flow after entry.”

Common Mistakes

  • Jumping into range
  • Entering square
  • Reaching with punches
  • Stopping after entry

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-step entry rounds
  • Entry-to-angle drills
  • Partner pressure entry rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Combination Entries
  • Connect to Pressure Step
