Skill 13 — Pressure Step

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply pressure with stance and control

B — Cut space without chasing recklessly

C — Stay defensively ready while advancing

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure smart.”
  • “Cut space.”
  • “Small steps.”
  • “Don’t chase.”

Common Mistakes

  • Running forward wild
  • Crossing feet under pressure
  • Dropping hands while advancing
  • Following instead of cutting off

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure step partner drill
  • Ring-cutting rounds
  • Jab-pressure entry drill

Progression

  • Connect to Ring Position
  • Connect to Pressure Sparring
