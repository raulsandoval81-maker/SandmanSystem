Skill 02 — Rhythm

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain offensive and defensive rhythm

B — Flow between movement and striking smoothly

C — Avoid predictable patterns while staying composed

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay flowing.”
  • “Move with rhythm.”
  • “Don’t freeze between actions.”
  • “Stay relaxed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after combinations
  • Moving stiff and robotic
  • Becoming predictable
  • Rushing exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Continuous flow shadowboxing
  • Rhythm partner rounds
  • Bag rhythm combinations

Progression

  • Connect to Feint
  • Connect to Combination Flow
