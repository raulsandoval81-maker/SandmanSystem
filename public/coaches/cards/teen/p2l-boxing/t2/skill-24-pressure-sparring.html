Skill 24 — Pressure Sparring

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Live

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply forward pressure while staying technical

B — Maintain defense and ring awareness during exchanges

C — Control pace, composure, and partner safety

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure smart.”
  • “Cut space, don’t chase.”
  • “Stay defensively responsible.”
  • “Controlled pace.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overcommitting forward
  • Ignoring defense while pressuring
  • Escalating intensity too high
  • Getting square during exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure-only sparring rounds
  • Ring-cutting sparring games
  • Corner pressure drills

Progression

  • Connect to Ring Awareness
  • Connect to Live Application
