Skill 18 — Fatigue Control

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Conditioning

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain stance and defense while tired

B — Control breathing and pace through fatigue

C — Continue technical decisions late in rounds

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay sharp tired.”
  • “Breathe and reset.”
  • “Don’t fall apart.”
  • “Technique under fatigue.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping hands when tired
  • Standing flat-footed late
  • Holding breath under pressure
  • Throwing sloppy punches to survive

Drill Ideas

  • Fatigue shadowboxing rounds
  • Bag sprint to defense reset
  • Late-round technical rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Round Management
  • Connect to Live Application
