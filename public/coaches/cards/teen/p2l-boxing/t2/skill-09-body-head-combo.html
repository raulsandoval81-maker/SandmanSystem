Skill 09 — Body Head Combo

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Change levels safely to attack the body

B — Return upstairs with posture and balance

C — Exit or reset after the combination

Coaching Cues

  • “Body then head.”
  • “Bend your knees.”
  • “Don’t lean.”
  • “Finish protected.”

Common Mistakes

  • Reaching down to the body
  • Standing tall between punches
  • Dropping both hands
  • Staying in front after the combo

Drill Ideas

  • Body-head bag rounds
  • Partner body-head flow drill
  • Level-change combo shadowboxing

Progression

  • Connect to Combination Flow
  • Connect to Range Control
