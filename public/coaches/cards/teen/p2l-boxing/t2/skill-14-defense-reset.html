Skill 14 — Defense Reset

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Defend without breaking stance

B — Recover guard immediately after pressure

C — Reset feet, eyes, and posture before next action

Coaching Cues

  • “Defense then reset.”
  • “Hands come back.”
  • “Feet under you.”
  • “Eyes stay up.”

Common Mistakes

  • Defending then freezing
  • Dropping guard after defense
  • Backing straight up
  • Standing tall after contact

Drill Ideas

  • Defense-reset partner drill
  • Block-slip-reset rounds
  • Coach call defense reactions

Progression

  • Connect to Defensive Reactions
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
