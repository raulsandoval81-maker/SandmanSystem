Skill 19 — Round Management

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Understand pace, time, pressure, and reset moments

B — Make smart decisions throughout the round

C — Finish rounds with discipline and awareness

Coaching Cues

  • “Manage the round.”
  • “Know the moment.”
  • “Don’t waste energy.”
  • “Finish smart.”

Common Mistakes

  • Starting too fast and fading
  • Ignoring time and pace
  • Fighting every second the same way
  • Losing discipline late

Drill Ideas

  • Fast-start slow-finish rounds
  • Last-30-second control drills
  • Coach-paced round management

Progression

  • Connect to Fatigue Control
  • Connect to Live Application
