Skill 20 — Live Application

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Live

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply T2 skills in controlled live situations

B — Make decisions using timing, range, and defense

C — Stay composed while solving real reactions

Coaching Cues

  • “Use the work.”
  • “Make smart reads.”
  • “Stay composed.”
  • “Control your pace.”

Common Mistakes

  • Forgetting the skill focus once live begins
  • Trying to win instead of apply
  • Losing defense under pressure
  • Escalating beyond the drill

Drill Ideas

  • Specific-skill live rounds
  • Timing and range sparring games
  • Controlled live application rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
  • Connect to Pressure Sparring
