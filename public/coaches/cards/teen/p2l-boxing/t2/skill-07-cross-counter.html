Skill 07 — Cross Counter

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Read the jab, step, or opening

B — Fire the cross counter from balance

C — Recover guard and exit after the counter

Coaching Cues

  • “See it early.”
  • “Cross on time.”
  • “Turn the hip.”
  • “Get out after.”

Common Mistakes

  • Countering late
  • Reaching with the cross
  • Dropping the lead hand
  • Staying in the pocket too long

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-cross counter partner drill
  • Slip-cross counter rounds
  • Counter and exit shadowboxing

Progression

  • Connect to Counter
  • Connect to Exit Angle
