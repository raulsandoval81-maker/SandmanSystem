Skill 03 — Feint

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create believable reactions with controlled feints

B — Stay balanced while drawing defensive movement

C — Attack immediately off the reaction window

Coaching Cues

  • “Sell the fake.”
  • “Watch the reaction.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Feint with purpose.”

Common Mistakes

  • Over-feinting without attacking
  • Leaning too far forward
  • Breaking stance during feints
  • Pausing after the fake

Drill Ideas

  • Feint-to-counter rounds
  • Reaction bait partner drills
  • Feint entry shadowboxing

Progression

  • Connect to Counter
  • Connect to Distance Traps
