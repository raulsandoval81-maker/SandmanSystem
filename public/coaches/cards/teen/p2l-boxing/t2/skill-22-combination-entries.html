Skill 22 — Combination Entries

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Enter safely behind combinations and movement

B — Create attack lanes with pressure and angles

C — Exit responsibly after engagement

Coaching Cues

  • “Enter behind punches.”
  • “Feet move with hands.”
  • “Flow into range.”
  • “Exit after contact.”

Common Mistakes

  • Jumping into range reckless
  • Crossing feet during entries
  • Overcommitting pressure
  • Standing square after attacking

Drill Ideas

  • Combination-entry rounds
  • Pressure-entry partner drills
  • Angle-entry shadowboxing

Progression

  • Connect to Combination Flow
  • Connect to Pressure Sparring
