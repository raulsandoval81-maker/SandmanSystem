Skill 13 — Defense Chain

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Combine multiple defensive reactions together

B — Stay balanced through each defensive layer

C — Recover ready to counter or move

Coaching Cues

  • “Defend in sequence.”
  • “Stay compact.”
  • “Recover after every action.”
  • “Defense flows.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after one defense
  • Standing tall during reactions
  • Opening guard between movements
  • Panicking under combinations

Drill Ideas

  • Slip-roll-block drills
  • Partner combination defense rounds
  • Defense flow shadowboxing

Progression

  • Connect to Hook Defense
  • Connect to Body Defense
