Skill 11 — Feint

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Use believable movement to create reactions

B — Stay balanced while feinting

C — Attack immediately off the reaction

Coaching Cues

  • “Sell the fake.”
  • “Stay relaxed.”
  • “Watch their reaction.”
  • “Feint then fire.”

Common Mistakes

  • Over-exaggerating movement
  • Leaning too far forward
  • Feinting without purpose
  • Freezing after the feint

Drill Ideas

  • Feint-to-jab drills
  • Reaction feint rounds
  • Mirror movement partner drill

Progression

  • Connect to Timing
  • Connect to Distance Trap
