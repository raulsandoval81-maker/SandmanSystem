Skill 08 — Angle Attack

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create an angle with footwork before attacking

B — Punch from a safe outside position

C — Exit before returning to the center line

Coaching Cues

  • “Step off the line.”
  • “Punch from the angle.”
  • “Don’t stand in front.”
  • “Angle then reset.”

Common Mistakes

  • Punching before the feet move
  • Crossing feet while angling
  • Staying square after the attack
  • Backing straight out

Drill Ideas

  • Step-angle punch drills
  • Partner angle entry rounds
  • Bag angle attack rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Pivot
  • Connect to Ring Movement
