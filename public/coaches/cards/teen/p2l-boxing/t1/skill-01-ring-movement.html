Skill 01 — Ring Movement

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Move around the ring with balance and control

B — Cut angles without crossing feet

C — Stay ready to punch or defend while moving

Coaching Cues

  • “Move with purpose.”
  • “Don’t cross your feet.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Hands ready while moving.”

Common Mistakes

  • Crossing feet while circling
  • Standing tall while moving
  • Dropping hands during footwork
  • Running instead of boxing

Drill Ideas

  • Ring movement shadowboxing
  • Circle-and-reset drills
  • Partner pressure movement rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Angle Attack
  • Connect to Pressure Step
