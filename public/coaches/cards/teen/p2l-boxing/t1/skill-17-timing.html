Skill 17 — Timing

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Read rhythm before attacking

B — Punch when the opening appears

C — Reset before forcing another exchange

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t rush the moment.”
  • “See the opening.”
  • “Punch on time.”
  • “Reset after contact.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing without reading
  • Waiting too long
  • Guessing instead of reacting
  • Overcommitting after one opening

Drill Ideas

  • Call-and-punch timing rounds
  • Partner rhythm read drill
  • Jab timing shadowboxing

Progression

  • Connect to Counter Jab
  • Connect to Distance Trap
