Skill 04 — Slip Counter

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Slip outside the incoming punch with eyes up

B — Return a clean counter from position

C — Reset defense immediately after the counter

Coaching Cues

  • “Small slip.”
  • “Eyes on target.”
  • “Counter right away.”
  • “Don’t fall off balance.”

Common Mistakes

  • Over-slipping too far
  • Looking down during defense
  • Countering late
  • Leaving the opposite hand low

Drill Ideas

  • Slip-jab counter drill
  • Slip-cross counter drill
  • Partner line defense rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Defense Chain
  • Connect to Counter Jab
