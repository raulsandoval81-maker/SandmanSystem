Skill 10 — Pressure Step

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply forward pressure without losing stance

B — Cut space while staying defensively responsible

C — Force reactions without rushing recklessly

Coaching Cues

  • “Walk them down smart.”
  • “Small steps.”
  • “Pressure with discipline.”
  • “Hands ready while advancing.”

Common Mistakes

  • Charging forward wild
  • Crossing feet under pressure
  • Dropping hands while advancing
  • Overreaching punches

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure walk partner drill
  • Controlled ring-cutting rounds
  • Pressure jab shadowboxing

Progression

  • Connect to Ring Movement
  • Connect to Distance Trap
