Skill 12 — Reaction Drill

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Drill

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize visual or verbal reaction cues quickly

B — Respond with correct movement or strike

C — Recover to stance immediately after reaction

Coaching Cues

  • “React fast, stay clean.”
  • “Eyes up.”
  • “Reset after every action.”
  • “Don’t guess.”

Common Mistakes

  • Reacting before the cue
  • Panicking under speed
  • Dropping stance during movement
  • Watching the floor

Drill Ideas

  • Coach call reaction rounds
  • Color or number cue drills
  • Partner touch-reaction games

Progression

  • Connect to Timing
  • Connect to Light Sparring
