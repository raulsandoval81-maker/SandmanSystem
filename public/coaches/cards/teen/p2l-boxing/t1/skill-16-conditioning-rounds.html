Skill 16 — Conditioning Rounds

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Conditioning

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain pace and posture under fatigue

B — Continue clean technique while tired

C — Finish every round with discipline

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay sharp tired.”
  • “Technique under fatigue.”
  • “Breathe and move.”
  • “Finish the round.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing flat-footed tired
  • Dropping hands under fatigue
  • Quitting mentally late
  • Losing breathing rhythm

Drill Ideas

  • Bag sprint rounds
  • Continuous movement rounds
  • Conditioning shadowboxing

Progression

  • Connect to Finish Standard
  • Connect to Light Sparring
