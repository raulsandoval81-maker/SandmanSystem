Skill 14 — Three Punch Flow

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Throw three punches with rhythm and balance

B — Maintain defensive posture during combinations

C — Exit clean after the final strike

Coaching Cues

  • “Flow, don’t rush.”
  • “Punches connect together.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Finish in stance.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing too hard too early
  • Losing balance during combinations
  • Standing square after punching
  • Dropping hands during flow

Drill Ideas

  • Three-punch bag rounds
  • Partner flow combinations
  • Shadowboxing flow rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Partner Flow
  • Connect to Light Sparring
