Skill 02 — Double Jab

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Fire the first jab to measure and disrupt

B — Fire the second jab with balance and purpose

C — Reset stance after the second jab

Coaching Cues

  • “Touch then stick.”
  • “Step with the jab.”
  • “Bring it back clean.”
  • “Don’t reach.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing both jabs lazy
  • Reaching instead of stepping
  • Dropping the rear hand
  • Standing tall after punching

Drill Ideas

  • Double jab shadowboxing
  • Double jab to reset rounds
  • Partner touch-jab drills

Progression

  • Connect to Counter Jab
  • Connect to Ring Movement
