Skill 15 — Partner Flow

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Drill

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Work controlled offensive and defensive exchanges

B — Maintain rhythm and composure with a partner

C — Show control, respect, and awareness

Coaching Cues

  • “Flow, don’t fight.”
  • “Controlled speed.”
  • “Take care of your partner.”
  • “Stay technical.”

Common Mistakes

  • Going too hard
  • Ignoring defensive responsibility
  • Losing rhythm under pressure
  • Turning drills into sparring

Drill Ideas

  • Touch-and-flow rounds
  • Call-and-response combinations
  • Mirror movement partner drills

Progression

  • Connect to Light Sparring
  • Connect to Three Punch Flow
