Skill 18 — Distance Trap

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Use range to invite a predictable reaction

B — Stay balanced while baiting the attack

C — Counter or exit once the trap is triggered

Coaching Cues

  • “Make them reach.”
  • “Control the space.”
  • “Bait, don’t run.”
  • “Trap then answer.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing straight up
  • Standing too close to trap
  • Waiting flat-footed
  • Missing the counter window

Drill Ideas

  • Step-back trap drill
  • Range bait partner rounds
  • Jab trap to counter drill

Progression

  • Connect to Timing
  • Connect to Light Sparring
