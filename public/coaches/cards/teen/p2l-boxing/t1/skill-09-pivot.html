Skill 09 — Pivot

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Pivot off the lead foot without crossing stance

B — Turn the body into a new angle

C — Finish balanced and ready to punch or defend

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn, don’t hop.”
  • “Keep your stance.”
  • “Eyes stay up.”
  • “Balance after the turn.”

Common Mistakes

  • Jumping instead of pivoting
  • Crossing the rear foot
  • Dropping hands during the turn
  • Finishing square

Drill Ideas

  • Lead-foot pivot drill
  • Pivot after jab rounds
  • Partner pressure pivot exits

Progression

  • Connect to Angle Attack
  • Connect to Ring Movement
