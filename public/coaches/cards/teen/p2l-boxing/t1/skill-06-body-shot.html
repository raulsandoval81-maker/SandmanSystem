Skill 06 — Body Shot

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Change level without dropping posture

B — Throw the body shot with balance and protection

C — Return to guard and exit after contact

Coaching Cues

  • “Bend your knees.”
  • “Don’t reach low.”
  • “Head protected.”
  • “Hit and get out.”

Common Mistakes

  • Leaning forward to reach the body
  • Dropping both hands
  • Standing in place after punching
  • Throwing without setup

Drill Ideas

  • Body shot level-change drill
  • Jab-to-body entry rounds
  • Body shot to exit angle drill

Progression

  • Connect to Body Defense
  • Connect to Angle Attack
