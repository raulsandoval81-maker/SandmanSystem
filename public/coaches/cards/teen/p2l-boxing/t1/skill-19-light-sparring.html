Skill 19 — Light Sparring

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Live

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply boxing skills with control and respect

B — Maintain safety, composure, and clean defense

C — Learn from live reactions without turning reckless

Coaching Cues

  • “Light means light.”
  • “Control first.”
  • “Use what you learned.”
  • “Protect your partner.”

Common Mistakes

  • Turning light sparring into fighting
  • Abandoning technique under pressure
  • Ignoring coach control
  • Trying to win instead of learn

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-only light sparring
  • Body-only controlled rounds
  • Defense-first light rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Partner Flow
  • Connect to Finish Standard
