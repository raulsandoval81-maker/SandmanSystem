Skill 20 — Finish Standard

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Finish rounds with discipline and composure

B — Keep boxing structure under fatigue

C — Show effort, respect, and control through the end

Coaching Cues

  • “Finish strong.”
  • “Stay disciplined tired.”
  • “Last reps matter.”
  • “Don’t fade mentally.”

Common Mistakes

  • Quitting mentally near the end
  • Dropping stance under fatigue
  • Losing defense late
  • Coasting before the round ends

Drill Ideas

  • End-of-round push drills
  • Fatigue shadowboxing rounds
  • Finish-hard bag rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Conditioning Rounds
  • Connect to T2 Boxing Readiness
