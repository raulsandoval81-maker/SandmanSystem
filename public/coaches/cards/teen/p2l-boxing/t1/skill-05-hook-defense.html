Skill 05 — Hook Defense

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize the hook line early

B — Protect with tight guard and stable posture

C — Reset or counter after the defense

Coaching Cues

  • “Elbow tight.”
  • “Chin tucked.”
  • “Don’t turn away.”
  • “Defense then answer.”

Common Mistakes

  • Opening the guard wide
  • Turning the head away
  • Standing tall on contact
  • Freezing after defense

Drill Ideas

  • Hook block partner drill
  • Hook defense to jab reset
  • Defense chain rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Defense Chain
  • Connect to Body Defense
