Skill 03 — Counter Jab

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Read the opponent’s jab or forward step

B — Answer with a sharp jab from position

C — Recover guard and angle out after the counter

Coaching Cues

  • “See it, answer it.”
  • “Beat them to the line.”
  • “Counter from balance.”
  • “Punch and reset.”

Common Mistakes

  • Waiting too long to answer
  • Leaning back instead of staying balanced
  • Throwing from poor stance
  • Not resetting after the counter

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-for-jab partner drill
  • Call-and-counter rounds
  • Counter jab shadowboxing

Progression

  • Connect to Slip Counter
  • Connect to Timing
