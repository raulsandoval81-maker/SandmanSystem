Skill 07 — Body Defense

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize body attacks without panicking

B — Protect ribs and centerline with tight elbows

C — Reset stance and eyes after defending

Coaching Cues

  • “Elbows home.”
  • “Stay compact.”
  • “Don’t fold.”
  • “Defend and recover.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping the head too low
  • Opening the elbows
  • Backing straight up
  • Freezing after body contact

Drill Ideas

  • Body defense partner rounds
  • Elbow shell reaction drill
  • Body defense to jab reset

Progression

  • Connect to Hook Defense
  • Connect to Defense Chain
