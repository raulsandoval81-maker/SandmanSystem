Skill 23 — Weapon Blending

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Blend jab, cross, hook, body shots, and movement together

B — Choose tools based on range, angle, and reaction

C — Maintain flow while changing weapons

Coaching Cues

  • “Blend your tools.”
  • “Right weapon, right moment.”
  • “Don’t force one attack.”
  • “Flow between weapons.”

Common Mistakes

  • Repeating the same weapon
  • Forcing power shots
  • Ignoring range before attacking
  • Losing rhythm during transitions

Drill Ideas

  • Weapon-call rounds
  • Mixed-weapon combination drills
  • Range-based attack games

Progression

  • Connect to Chain Finishing
  • Connect to System Mastery
