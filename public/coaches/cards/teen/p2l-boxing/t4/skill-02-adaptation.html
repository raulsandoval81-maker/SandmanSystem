Skill 02 — Adaptation

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize changing fight conditions quickly

B — Adjust tactics, pace, and tools in real time

C — Maintain composure while solving new problems

Coaching Cues

  • “Adjust immediately.”
  • “Don’t stay predictable.”
  • “Solve the problem.”
  • “Think under pressure.”

Common Mistakes

  • Repeating failed tactics
  • Panicking during changes
  • Ignoring opponent adjustments
  • Fighting emotionally

Drill Ideas

  • Situational adjustment rounds
  • Adaptive sparring games
  • Coach-change tactical drills

Progression

  • Connect to Pressure Control
  • Connect to Opponent Reading
