Skill 06 — Corner Traps

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Trap opponents against ropes and corners intelligently

B — Remove escape lanes through pressure and angles

C — Maintain defensive structure while attacking

Coaching Cues

  • “Cut every exit.”
  • “Pressure smart.”
  • “Don’t smother the work.”
  • “Own the corner.”

Common Mistakes

  • Chasing instead of trapping
  • Overcommitting attacks
  • Standing square under pressure
  • Ignoring counters while trapping

Drill Ideas

  • Corner-trap sparring games
  • Ring-cutting drills
  • Corner pressure rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Inside Dominance
  • Connect to Chain Pressure
