Skill 15 — Mental Pressure

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply pressure that forces rushed decisions

B — Stay calm while making the opponent uncomfortable

C — Break composure without losing your own discipline

Coaching Cues

  • “Make them uncomfortable.”
  • “Stay calm while they rush.”
  • “Pressure their mind.”
  • “Discipline breaks chaos.”

Common Mistakes

  • Turning pressure into anger
  • Chasing instead of controlling
  • Losing composure while pressuring
  • Forcing exchanges recklessly

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure decision rounds
  • Composure-under-pressure sparring
  • Controlled intimidation pace drills

Progression

  • Connect to Round Stealing
  • Connect to Fight Breaking
