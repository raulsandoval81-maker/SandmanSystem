Skill 29 — Fight Management

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Manage pace, momentum, and exchanges strategically

B — Make intelligent adjustments across the full fight

C — Stay composed while controlling difficult moments

Coaching Cues

  • “Manage the fight.”
  • “Control momentum.”
  • “Don’t waste exchanges.”
  • “Stay disciplined.”

Common Mistakes

  • Fighting emotionally
  • Ignoring momentum shifts
  • Wasting energy unnecessarily
  • Failing to adapt strategically

Drill Ideas

  • Strategic sparring rounds
  • Momentum-control drills
  • Fight-situation simulations

Progression

  • Connect to System Mastery
  • Connect to Championship Readiness
