Skill 01 — Boxing IQ

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Understand patterns, reactions, and strategic openings

B — Make intelligent adjustments during live exchanges

C — Stay composed while solving complex fight situations

Coaching Cues

  • “Think while fighting.”
  • “Read the patterns.”
  • “Adjust in real time.”
  • “Smart pressure wins.”

Common Mistakes

  • Fighting emotionally instead of intelligently
  • Ignoring opponent habits
  • Repeating predictable attacks
  • Failing to adapt under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Pattern-recognition sparring
  • Tactical adjustment rounds
  • Coach-call strategic games

Progression

  • Connect to Adaptation
  • Connect to Strategy Build
