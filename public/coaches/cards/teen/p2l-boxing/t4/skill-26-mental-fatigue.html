Skill 26 — Mental Fatigue

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Conditioning

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain decision-making while mentally exhausted

B — Preserve discipline and structure under stress

C — Continue solving problems late in hard rounds

Coaching Cues

  • “Think tired.”
  • “Stay disciplined exhausted.”
  • “Don’t mentally break.”
  • “Solve problems late.”

Common Mistakes

  • Mentally shutting down under fatigue
  • Abandoning strategy tired
  • Panicking during pressure
  • Losing composure late

Drill Ideas

  • Fatigue-decision sparring
  • Late-round tactical drills
  • Conditioning-to-problem-solving rounds

Progression

  • Connect to High Pressure Rounds
  • Connect to Chaos Composure
