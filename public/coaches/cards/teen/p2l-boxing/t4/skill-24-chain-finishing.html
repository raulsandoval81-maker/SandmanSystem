Skill 24 — Chain Finishing

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Finish through multiple linked attacks instead of one shot

B — Keep pressure alive after defensive reactions

C — Finish with discipline, accuracy, and control

Coaching Cues

  • “Finish in chains.”
  • “Don’t stop at one.”
  • “Stay accurate.”
  • “Control the finish.”

Common Mistakes

  • Swinging wild while finishing
  • Stopping after the first clean shot
  • Losing defense during finish attempts
  • Overcommitting without position

Drill Ideas

  • Finish-chain bag rounds
  • Pressure finish sparring
  • Multi-phase finishing drills

Progression

  • Connect to System Mastery
  • Connect to Fight Breaking
