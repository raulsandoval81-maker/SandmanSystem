Skill 12 — Body Shot System

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Attack the body through layered entries and setups

B — Blend body-head transitions fluidly under pressure

C — Maintain defensive awareness during close exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Break the body.”
  • “Change levels safely.”
  • “Body opens the head.”
  • “Protect while attacking.”

Common Mistakes

  • Reaching into body shots
  • Dropping both hands
  • Standing in range after attacks
  • Ignoring angle exits

Drill Ideas

  • Body-head combination rounds
  • Pressure body-shot drills
  • Level-change sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Combination Systems
  • Connect to Defensive Reactions
