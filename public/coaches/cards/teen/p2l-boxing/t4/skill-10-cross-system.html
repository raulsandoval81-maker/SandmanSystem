Skill 10 — Cross System

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Deliver layered rear-hand attacks from multiple setups

B — Blend timing, pressure, and angles into the cross

C — Recover structure after high-commitment offense

Coaching Cues

  • “Drive from the floor.”
  • “Cross through openings.”
  • “Recover after impact.”
  • “Don’t overreach.”

Common Mistakes

  • Forcing power shots
  • Dropping the lead hand
  • Standing square after throwing
  • Ignoring setup timing

Drill Ideas

  • Cross-counter rounds
  • Pressure-cross combinations
  • Rear-hand setup drills

Progression

  • Connect to Hook System
  • Connect to Body Shot System
