Skill 11 — Hook System

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build layered offense around lead and rear hooks

B — Blend hooks into pressure, counters, and angles

C — Recover defensive structure after rotational attacks

Coaching Cues

  • “Short and sharp.”
  • “Turn through the shot.”
  • “Hooks create openings.”
  • “Protect after impact.”

Common Mistakes

  • Looping hooks wide
  • Standing square during rotation
  • Dropping opposite hand
  • Overcommitting body weight

Drill Ideas

  • Lead-rear hook chains
  • Angle hook sparring
  • Hook-counter drills

Progression

  • Connect to Body Shot System
  • Connect to Combination Systems
