Skill 07 — Inside Dominance

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control close-range exchanges through position and pressure

B — Blend offense and defense inside the pocket

C — Stay composed and balanced under contact pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the inside.”
  • “Stay compact.”
  • “Short punches win.”
  • “Protect while working.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing wide punches inside
  • Standing tall under pressure
  • Panicking in close exchanges
  • Losing defensive awareness

Drill Ideas

  • Inside-control sparring
  • Short-shot pressure drills
  • Close-range reaction games

Progression

  • Connect to Chain Pressure
  • Connect to Jab System
