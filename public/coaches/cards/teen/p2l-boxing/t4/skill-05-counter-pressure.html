Skill 05 — Counter Pressure

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Draw pressure and punish predictable attacks

B — Blend defensive reads with immediate counters

C — Recover control after the exchange finishes

Coaching Cues

  • “Make them pay.”
  • “Pressure creates openings.”
  • “Counter under control.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Countering too late
  • Panicking during pressure
  • Overcommitting counters
  • Ignoring defensive recovery

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure-counter rounds
  • Reaction counter sparring
  • Defensive bait drills

Progression

  • Connect to Corner Traps
  • Connect to Inside Dominance
