Skill 19 — Opponent Reading

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Identify opponent patterns, habits, and reactions

B — Use those reads to choose better tactics

C — Adjust pressure, range, and weapons based on live reads

Coaching Cues

  • “Read before reacting.”
  • “Find the pattern.”
  • “Use what they give you.”
  • “Adjust fast.”

Common Mistakes

  • Only focusing on your own offense
  • Missing repeated reactions
  • Failing to adjust after reads
  • Guessing instead of observing

Drill Ideas

  • Pattern-read sparring
  • Opponent habit callouts
  • Reaction-based strategy rounds
