Skill 17 — Live Chaos

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Live

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Stay composed in unpredictable live exchanges

B — Solve pressure, fatigue, and mistakes in real time

C — Recover structure immediately after disruption

Coaching Cues

  • “Calm in chaos.”
  • “Solve the problem.”
  • “Recover fast.”
  • “Keep your system.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking when exchanges get messy
  • Abandoning fundamentals
  • Fighting emotionally
  • Freezing after mistakes

Drill Ideas

  • Random-start live rounds
  • Chaos recovery drills
  • Unpredictable pressure sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Chaos Composure
  • Connect to System Mastery
