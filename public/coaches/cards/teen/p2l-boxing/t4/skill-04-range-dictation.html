Skill 04 — Range Dictation

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Force exchanges to happen at your preferred range

B — Control distance through footwork, jabs, and angles

C — Deny opponent comfort and rhythm

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the range.”
  • “Force your distance.”
  • “Don’t fight their fight.”
  • “Control the exchange.”

Common Mistakes

  • Allowing opponents to set distance
  • Standing still during exchanges
  • Ignoring angle exits
  • Fighting at the wrong range

Drill Ideas

  • Distance-control sparring
  • Jab-and-angle rounds
  • Range denial partner drills

Progression

  • Connect to Counter Pressure
  • Connect to Corner Traps
