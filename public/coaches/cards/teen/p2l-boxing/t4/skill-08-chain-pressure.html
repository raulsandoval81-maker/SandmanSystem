Skill 08 — Chain Pressure

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Sustain pressure across multiple exchanges and positions

B — Blend movement, offense, and defensive recovery together

C — Break opponents mentally through relentless control

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure never stops.”
  • “Flow between layers.”
  • “Stay disciplined.”
  • “Break the pace.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overextending combinations
  • Burning energy too fast
  • Losing defensive structure
  • Pausing after exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Continuous pressure rounds
  • Multi-layer sparring drills
  • Chain attack partner games

Progression

  • Connect to Jab System
  • Connect to Cross System
