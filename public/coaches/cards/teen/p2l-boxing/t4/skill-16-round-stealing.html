Skill 16 — Round Stealing

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize late-round scoring moments

B — Increase clean output without losing structure

C — Finish rounds with control, urgency, and discipline

Coaching Cues

  • “Win the last exchange.”
  • “Finish smart.”
  • “Urgency, not panic.”
  • “Clean work late.”

Common Mistakes

  • Flurrying sloppy at the end
  • Forcing reckless exchanges
  • Losing defense late
  • Waiting too long to surge

Drill Ideas

  • Last-30-second rounds
  • Late-round scoring drills
  • Controlled finish bursts
