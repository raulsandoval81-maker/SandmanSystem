Skill 14 — Fatigue Weaponization

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Conditioning

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Use conditioning and pace as pressure tools

B — Stay technical while making opponents work

C — Break rhythm late without losing discipline

Coaching Cues

  • “Make pace a weapon.”
  • “Stay sharp tired.”
  • “Break them late.”
  • “Pressure with conditioning.”

Common Mistakes

  • Getting sloppy while tired
  • Trying to outwork without structure
  • Dropping defense under fatigue
  • Burning energy too early

Drill Ideas

  • Late-round pressure rounds
  • Conditioning-to-skill transitions
  • Fatigue pressure sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Mental Pressure
  • Connect to Mental Fatigue
