Skill 13 — Defense To Offense

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Turn defensive reactions into immediate offense

B — Counter without losing balance or structure

C — Recover position after the offensive response

Coaching Cues

  • “Defend then answer.”
  • “Make defense create offense.”
  • “Counter with control.”
  • “Recover after the shot.”

Common Mistakes

  • Defending then freezing
  • Countering off balance
  • Throwing wild after defense
  • Ignoring reset after offense

Drill Ideas

  • Block-counter rounds
  • Slip-to-combo drills
  • Defense-to-offense sparring games

Progression

  • Connect to Counter Pressure
  • Connect to Finish Instinct
