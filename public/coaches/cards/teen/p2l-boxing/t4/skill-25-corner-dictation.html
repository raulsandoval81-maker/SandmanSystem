Skill 25 — Corner Dictation

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Force opponents into corners through controlled positioning

B — Dictate exits, reactions, and exchange choices

C — Maintain discipline while controlling trapped positions

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the corner.”
  • “Dictate the exits.”
  • “Trap, don’t chase.”
  • “Control the space.”

Common Mistakes

  • Letting opponents escape freely
  • Rushing attacks in the corner
  • Getting countered while overcommitting
  • Standing square in trapped positions

Drill Ideas

  • Corner control sparring
  • Exit denial drills
  • Corner dictation pressure rounds

Progression

  • Connect to High Pressure Rounds
  • Connect to System Mastery
