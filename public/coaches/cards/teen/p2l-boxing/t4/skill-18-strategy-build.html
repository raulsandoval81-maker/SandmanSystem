Skill 18 — Strategy Build

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build a clear plan based on opponent reactions

B — Connect tools, pace, range, and pressure into strategy

C — Adjust the plan when live conditions change

Coaching Cues

  • “Have a plan.”
  • “Build the fight.”
  • “Adjust when needed.”
  • “Use your tools together.”

Common Mistakes

  • Fighting without a plan
  • Repeating failed tactics
  • Ignoring opponent habits
  • Changing strategy emotionally

Drill Ideas

  • Plan-based sparring rounds
  • Opponent-style simulation drills
  • Coach-assigned strategy games

Progression

  • Connect to Opponent Reading
  • Connect to Fight Management
