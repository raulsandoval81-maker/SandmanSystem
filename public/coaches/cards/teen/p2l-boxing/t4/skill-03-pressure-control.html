Skill 03 — Pressure Control

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control pressure without wasting movement or energy

B — Blend pace, distance, and positioning together

C — Force reactions while staying defensively disciplined

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure with control.”
  • “Own the space.”
  • “Break them mentally.”
  • “Stay balanced.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overpressuring recklessly
  • Ignoring defense during pursuit
  • Burning energy chasing
  • Standing square under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Controlled pressure rounds
  • Ring-cutting sparring drills
  • Pressure-and-reset games

Progression

  • Connect to Range Dictation
  • Connect to Counter Pressure
