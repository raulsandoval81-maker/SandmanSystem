Skill 28 — Fight Awareness

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

B — Make tactical decisions during active exchanges

C — Stay composed while solving problems in real time

Coaching Cues

  • “Think under pressure.”

  • Ignoring positioning
  • Missing momentum shifts
