Skill 26 — Live Rounds

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Live

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply advanced boxing concepts during live exchanges

B — Maintain composure, awareness, and defensive structure

C — Make smart adjustments during real-time rounds

Coaching Cues

  • “Use the work.”
  • “Think during exchanges.”
  • “Stay defensively responsible.”
  • “Control the pace.”

Common Mistakes

  • Fighting emotionally
  • Ignoring setups and reads
  • Overcommitting exchanges
