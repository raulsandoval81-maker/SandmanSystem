Skill 18 — Angle Traps

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create traps using angles, exits, and positioning

B — Force predictable reactions through footwork

C — Counter safely while controlling range

Coaching Cues

  • “Trap with angles.”
  • “Move before they move.”
  • “Create predictable reactions.”
  • “Counter clean.”

Common Mistakes

  • Moving without purpose
  • Crossing feet during angles
  • Standing too close during traps
  • Overcommitting counters

Drill Ideas

  • Angle trap sparring games
  • Step-off counter drills
  • Partner reaction angle rounds
