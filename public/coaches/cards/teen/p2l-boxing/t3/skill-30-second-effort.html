Skill 30 — Second Effort

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Conditioning

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Respond immediately after mistakes or failed exchanges

B — Continue fighting intelligently under fatigue

C — Show resilience, composure, and discipline late

Coaching Cues

  • “Respond immediately.”
  • “No quitting mentally.”
  • “Recover and continue.”
  • “Second effort matters.”

Common Mistakes

  • Mentally shutting down after mistakes
  • Standing still after exchanges
  • Panicking while tired
  • Losing technical discipline late

Drill Ideas

  • Recovery-response rounds
  • Fatigue reaction sparring
  • Continuous effort drills

Progression

  • Connect to T4 Boxing Readiness
  • Connect to Live Rounds
