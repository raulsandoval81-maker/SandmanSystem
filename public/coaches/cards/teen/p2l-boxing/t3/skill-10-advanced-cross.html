Skill 10 — Advanced Cross

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Deliver the cross from multiple setups and angles

B — Transfer weight and power without losing structure

C — Recover defensively after high-commitment strikes

Coaching Cues

  • “Drive from the floor.”
  • “Turn the hip.”
  • “Don’t overreach.”
  • “Recover after impact.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overcommitting body weight
  • Dropping the lead hand
  • Standing square after punching
  • Forcing the shot without setup

Drill Ideas

  • Cross-counter rounds
  • Jab-cross angle drills
  • Power-cross bag work

Progression

  • Connect to Counter Punching
  • Connect to Offensive Layers
