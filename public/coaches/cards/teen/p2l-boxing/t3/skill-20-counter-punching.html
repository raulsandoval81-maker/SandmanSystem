Skill 20 — Counter Punching

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Read offensive mistakes during live exchanges

B — Deliver accurate counters from defensive structure

C — Recover position immediately after engagement

Coaching Cues

  • “Make them miss.”
  • “Counter on time.”
  • “Punish mistakes.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Countering too late
  • Forcing counters without reads
  • Dropping defense after punching
  • Standing in danger range afterward

Drill Ideas

  • Slip-counter sparring drills
  • Counter timing rounds
  • Reaction-counter games

Progression

  • Connect to Defensive Layers
  • Connect to Offensive Layers
