Skill 24 — Pace Break

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Interrupt opponent rhythm through pace changes

B — Shift intensity without losing composure

C — Regain control after explosive exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Break the rhythm.”
  • “Change the pace.”
  • “Explode then recover.”
  • “Control the exchange.”

Common Mistakes

  • Fighting emotionally
  • Burning energy too early
  • Telegraphing pace changes
  • Losing structure during bursts

Drill Ideas

  • Pace-shift sparring rounds
  • Explosive burst drills
  • Rhythm interruption games

Progression

  • Connect to Weapon Selection
  • Connect to Fight Awareness
