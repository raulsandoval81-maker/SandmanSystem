Skill 21 — Pocket Awareness

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Understand danger range and exchange positioning

B — Make smart decisions while inside the pocket

C — Exit safely without panic or wasted movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Know the danger zone.”
  • “Stay composed inside.”
  • “See the openings.”
  • “Exit with discipline.”

Common Mistakes

  • Freezing inside exchanges
  • Backing straight up
  • Throwing blind punches
  • Losing defensive awareness

Drill Ideas

  • Pocket sparring rounds
  • Inside reaction drills
  • Close-range awareness games

Progression

  • Connect to Defensive Layers
  • Connect to Offensive Layers
