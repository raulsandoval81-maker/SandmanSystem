Skill 11 — Lead Hook System

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build offense around the lead hook from multiple positions

B — Blend hooks with footwork, angles, and setups

C — Recover balance and defense after engagement

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn the hip.”
  • “Short and sharp.”
  • “Hook off angles.”
  • “Recover after impact.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing wide looping hooks
  • Standing square during the punch
  • Dropping the rear hand
  • Overcommitting body weight

Drill Ideas

  • Lead-hook angle drills
  • Hook-entry combinations
  • Partner hook reaction rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Offensive Layers
  • Connect to Pocket Awareness
