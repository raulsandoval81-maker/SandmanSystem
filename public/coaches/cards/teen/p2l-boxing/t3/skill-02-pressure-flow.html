Skill 02 — Pressure Flow

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply constant pressure without overcommitting

B — Blend offense, defense, and footwork together

C — Force reactions while staying balanced and composed

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure without rushing.”
  • “Flow through positions.”
  • “Stay defensively responsible.”
  • “Break them mentally.”

Common Mistakes

  • Charging forward reckless
  • Stopping after exchanges
  • Ignoring defense while pressuring
  • Crossing feet during pursuit

Drill Ideas

  • Continuous pressure rounds
  • Pressure-flow sparring
  • Ring-cutting movement drills

Progression

  • Connect to Corner Pressure
  • Connect to Tempo Control
