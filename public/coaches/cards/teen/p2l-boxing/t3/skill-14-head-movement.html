Skill 14 — Head Movement

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Move the head without losing stance or balance

B — Blend slips, rolls, and defensive angles together

C — Return offense immediately after movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Move with purpose.”
  • “Small movement.”
  • “Eyes stay up.”
  • “Defend and answer.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overmoving the head
  • Breaking stance while slipping
  • Looking down during defense
  • Forgetting offensive recovery

Drill Ideas

  • Slip-roll-counter drills
  • Head movement reaction rounds
  • Defense flow sparring games

Progression

  • Connect to Combination Defense
  • Connect to Defensive Layers
