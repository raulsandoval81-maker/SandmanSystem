Skill 03 — Counter Chain

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Link multiple counters together from defensive reads

B — Transition smoothly between defensive reactions

C — Maintain structure throughout chained exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Defend and flow.”
  • “Counter in sequence.”
  • “Stay sharp.”
  • “Recover after every action.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after one counter
  • Throwing wild follow-ups
  • Ignoring defense after landing
  • Overextending combinations

Drill Ideas

  • Counter-chain partner drills
  • Slip-roll-counter sequences
  • Reaction chain sparring rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Counter Punching
  • Connect to Defensive Layers
