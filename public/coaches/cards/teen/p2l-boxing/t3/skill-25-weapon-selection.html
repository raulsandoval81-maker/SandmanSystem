Skill 25 — Weapon Selection

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Choose the correct strike for the opening presented

B — Match tools to distance, angle, and timing

C — Stay composed while making live decisions

Coaching Cues

  • “Use the right tool.”
  • “Read before attacking.”
  • “Don’t force punches.”
  • “Think while fighting.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing the same attacks repeatedly
  • Ignoring distance and positioning
  • Forcing power shots
  • Panicking during exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Open-reaction sparring games
  • Weapon-choice partner drills
  • Situational boxing rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Live Rounds
  • Connect to Fight Awareness
