Skill 15 — Combination Defense

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Defend layered offensive combinations under pressure

B — Blend blocks, slips, rolls, and movement together

C — Recover balance and vision during extended exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Defense flows together.”
  • “Don’t freeze.”
  • “Recover after every layer.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after one defensive action
  • Panicking under combinations
  • Standing square during exchanges
  • Dropping guard during movement

Drill Ideas

  • Multi-layer defense drills
  • Combination reaction rounds
  • Partner defensive flow sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Defensive Layers
  • Connect to Counter Punching
