Skill 12 — Rear Hook System

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Deliver rear hooks from balanced offensive positions

B — Use rotation, timing, and setup for clean impact

C — Recover structure after rotational attacks

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn through the shot.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Don’t swing wild.”
  • “Protect after throwing.”

Common Mistakes

  • Over-rotating body weight
  • Dropping the lead hand
  • Throwing from too far away
  • Losing stance after impact

Drill Ideas

  • Rear-hook setup rounds
  • Cross-hook combination drills
  • Power rotation bag work

Progression

  • Connect to Counter Punching
  • Connect to Offensive Layers
