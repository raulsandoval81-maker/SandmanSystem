Skill 06 — Corner Pressure

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Cut escape lanes and pressure opponents into corners

B — Maintain defensive awareness while attacking

C — Control positioning without overcommitting recklessly

Coaching Cues

  • “Cut the exits.”
  • “Pressure smart.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Don’t smother your work.”

Common Mistakes

  • Chasing instead of cutting off
  • Overextending combinations
  • Standing square in close range
  • Ignoring counters while pressuring

Drill Ideas

  • Corner trap sparring games
  • Ring-cutting pressure rounds
  • Corner exit denial drills
