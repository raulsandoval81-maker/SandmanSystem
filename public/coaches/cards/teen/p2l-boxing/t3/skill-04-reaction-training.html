Skill 04 — Reaction Training

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Drill

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize live cues under pressure

B — React with correct offensive or defensive choices

C — Recover composure and positioning after reactions

Coaching Cues

  • “See it fast.”
  • “React under control.”
  • “Eyes up.”
  • “Recover immediately.”

Common Mistakes

  • Guessing before the cue
  • Panicking under speed
  • Breaking stance during reactions
  • Freezing after mistakes

Drill Ideas

  • Coach-call reaction rounds
  • Live visual cue drills
  • Partner reaction sparring games
