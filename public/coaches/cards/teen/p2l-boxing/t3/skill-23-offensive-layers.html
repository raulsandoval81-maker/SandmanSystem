Skill 23 — Offensive Layers

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Stack setups, feints, and combinations together

B — Build attacks through reactions and positioning

C — Maintain defensive responsibility during offense

Coaching Cues

  • “Layer the attack.”
  • “Build reactions.”
  • “Flow through openings.”
  • “Stay defensively aware.”

Common Mistakes

  • Attacking without setup
  • Repeating predictable entries
  • Ignoring defense during offense
  • Forcing exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Layered combination rounds
  • Feint-to-attack sparring games
  • Pressure setup drills

Progression

  • Connect to Pace Break
  • Connect to Weapon Selection
