Skill 07 — Inside Boxing

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Stay balanced and protected in close range exchanges

B — Create short punches and defensive reactions inside

C — Maintain posture and awareness under pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Short and tight.”
  • “Protect your position.”
  • “Stay compact.”
  • “Work without reaching.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing wide punches inside
  • Standing too tall in close range
  • Ignoring defense while attacking
  • Panicking under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Inside punching rounds
  • Short-hook partner drills
  • Close-range defense games

Progression

  • Connect to Dirty Boxing
  • Connect to Pocket Awareness
