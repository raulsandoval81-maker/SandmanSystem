Skill 08 — Dirty Boxing

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control position during close-contact exchanges

B — Create short strikes and inside pressure safely

C — Maintain awareness and discipline during chaos

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay tight inside.”
  • “Short punches matter.”
  • “Control position first.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Getting wild in close range
  • Ignoring posture and balance
  • Throwing looping punches
  • Losing defensive structure

Drill Ideas

  • Clinch-position boxing drills
  • Short-shot sparring rounds
  • Inside pressure partner games

Progression

  • Connect to Inside Boxing
  • Connect to Pocket Awareness
