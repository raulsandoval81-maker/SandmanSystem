Skill 17 — False Pressure

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create the appearance of pressure without overcommitting

B — Force reactions using movement, posture, and positioning

C — Stay defensively responsible while manipulating pace

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure without rushing.”
  • “Make them react.”
  • “Sell the pressure.”
  • “Stay balanced.”

Common Mistakes

  • Charging forward recklessly
  • Pressuring without setup
  • Standing square during pressure
  • Ignoring defensive recovery

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure-feint rounds
  • False-entry sparring games
  • Controlled ring-cutting drills

Progression

  • Connect to Angle Traps
  • Connect to Pocket Awareness
