Skill 01 — Ringcraft

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control positioning, pace, and direction inside the ring

B — Manipulate movement using pressure, angles, and distance

C — Stay composed while solving live movement problems

Coaching Cues

  • “Control the room.”
  • “Own the center.”
  • “Move them where you want.”
  • “Think while moving.”

Common Mistakes

  • Following instead of steering
  • Backing into bad positions
  • Ignoring ring geography
  • Pressuring without purpose

Drill Ideas

  • Center-control sparring
  • Ring steering partner rounds
  • Pressure-and-angle movement drills

Progression

  • Connect to Pressure Flow
  • Connect to Corner Pressure
