Skill 19 — Rope Escapes

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Escape ropes and corners without panic

B — Use pivots, angles, and defense to recover space

C — Reestablish center control after escaping

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t stay trapped.”
  • “Angle out.”
  • “Protect while escaping.”
  • “Recover center.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing straight into corners
  • Panicking under pressure
  • Escaping with hands low
  • Standing still after escape

Drill Ideas

  • Corner escape rounds
  • Rope-pressure sparring drills
  • Pivot-and-escape games

Progression

  • Connect to Ringcraft
  • Connect to Pocket Awareness
