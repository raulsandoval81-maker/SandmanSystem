Skill 27 — Fatigue Performance

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Conditioning

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain decision-making while exhausted

B — Preserve technique and defense under fatigue

C — Finish rounds with discipline and composure

Coaching Cues

  • “Think tired.”
  • “Stay technical exhausted.”
  • “Protect your structure.”
  • “Finish strong.”

Common Mistakes

  • Quitting mentally under fatigue
  • Throwing sloppy punches tired
  • Standing flat-footed late
  • Losing defensive awareness

Drill Ideas

  • Fatigue sparring rounds
  • Conditioning-to-live transitions
  • Late-round reaction drills

Progression

  • Connect to Fight Awareness
  • Connect to Second Effort
