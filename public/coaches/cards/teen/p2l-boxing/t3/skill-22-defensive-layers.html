Skill 22 — Defensive Layers

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Blend multiple defensive reactions together smoothly

B — Transition between slips, rolls, blocks, and movement

C — Stay balanced and ready to counter after defense

Coaching Cues

  • “Defense stacks together.”
  • “Flow through reactions.”
  • “Recover after every layer.”
  • “Stay calm under pressure.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after one defense
  • Crossing feet during reactions
  • Dropping hands between layers
  • Panicking under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Multi-layer defense drills
  • Slip-roll-block sequences
  • Defensive flow sparring rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Counter Punching
  • Connect to Chaos Drills
