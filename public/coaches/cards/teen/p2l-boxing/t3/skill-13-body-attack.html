Skill 13 — Body Attack

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create layered attacks to the body from safe positions

B — Blend head and body transitions together smoothly

C — Maintain defensive awareness during body exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Attack levels.”
  • “Don’t reach low.”
  • “Protect while changing levels.”
  • “Body opens the head.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping both hands
  • Leaning into body shots
  • Standing in range after attacking
  • Ignoring defensive recovery

Drill Ideas

  • Body-head combination drills
  • Body pressure rounds
  • Level-change sparring games

Progression

  • Connect to Offensive Layers
  • Connect to Pocket Awareness
