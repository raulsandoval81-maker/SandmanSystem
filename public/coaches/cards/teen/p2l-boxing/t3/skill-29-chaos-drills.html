Skill 29 — Chaos Drills

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Drill

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Solve unpredictable situations under pressure

B — Maintain composure during fast-changing exchanges

C — Recover structure after mistakes or disruptions

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay calm in chaos.”
  • “Solve the problem.”
  • “Recover fast.”
  • “Keep thinking.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking during unpredictability
  • Abandoning fundamentals
  • Freezing after mistakes
  • Losing defensive awareness

Drill Ideas

  • Random reaction sparring
  • Coach-call chaos drills
  • Disruption recovery rounds
