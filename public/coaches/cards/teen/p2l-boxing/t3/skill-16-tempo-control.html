Skill 16 — Tempo Control

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control pace changes during exchanges and rounds

B — Blend explosive bursts with calm resets

C — Break opponent rhythm while staying composed

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the pace.”
  • “Slow-fast-slow.”
  • “Break their rhythm.”
  • “Stay relaxed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Fighting one speed the whole round
  • Rushing exchanges emotionally
  • Burning energy too early
  • Becoming predictable

Drill Ideas

  • Tempo-change sparring rounds
  • Slow-fast combination drills
  • Rhythm disruption games

Progression

  • Connect to False Pressure
  • Connect to Pace Break
