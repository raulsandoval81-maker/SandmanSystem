Skill 06 — Hook

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Hook travels short and tight from guard

B — Rotate hips and shoulders through the punch

C — Return hand to guard after the hook

Coaching Cues

  • “Short hook.”
  • “Turn the hip.”
  • “Opposite hand stays home.”
  • “Don’t swing wide.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing the hook too wide
  • Dropping the opposite hand
  • Overrotating past balance
  • Standing flat-footed

Drill Ideas

  • Hook mitt repetitions
  • Hook-only bag rounds
  • Jab-cross-hook drills

Progression

  • Connect to Uppercut
  • Connect to Combination 2
