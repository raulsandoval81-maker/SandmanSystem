Skill 13 — Angle Step

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Step off the center line after punching or defending

B — Keep stance and balance while changing direction

C — Reset into a safer attack or exit lane

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t stay in front.”
  • “Step and turn.”
  • “Move after punching.”
  • “Create a new lane.”

Common Mistakes

  • Crossing feet
  • Turning the back
  • Standing still after combinations
  • Losing guard while moving

Drill Ideas

  • Angle-step shadowboxing
  • Combo-and-angle mitt work
  • Partner angle exit drills

Progression

  • Connect to Combination 1
  • Connect to Combination 2
