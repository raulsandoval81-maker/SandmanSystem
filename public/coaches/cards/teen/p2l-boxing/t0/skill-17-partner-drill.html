Skill 17 — Partner Drill

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Work controlled offense and defense with a partner

B — Maintain spacing, safety, and communication

C — Reset cleanly between repetitions

Coaching Cues

  • “Controlled work.”
  • “Protect your partner.”
  • “Clean reps.”
  • “Technical pace.”

Common Mistakes

  • Going too hard too early
  • Ignoring distance
  • Rushing repetitions
  • Dropping defense during drills

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-for-jab drills
  • Slip and return drills
  • Controlled combo partner rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Conditioning
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
