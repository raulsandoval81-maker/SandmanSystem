Skill 09 — Slip

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Move head slightly outside the punch line

B — Keep eyes forward and hands ready while slipping

C — Return balanced and ready to answer

Coaching Cues

  • “Small slip.”
  • “Eyes up.”
  • “Don’t lean too far.”
  • “Slip and reset.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overleaning out of stance
  • Dropping hands during the slip
  • Closing eyes during defense
  • Standing tall while slipping

Drill Ideas

  • Slip line drill
  • Partner jab slip drill
  • Shadowboxing slip rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Roll
  • Connect to Parry
