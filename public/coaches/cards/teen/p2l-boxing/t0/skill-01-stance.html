Skill 01 — Stance

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Athletic boxing stance with knees bent and chin protected

B — Hands ready, feet under the body, weight balanced

C — Reset stance after movement, punches, or defense

Coaching Cues

  • “Feet under you.”
  • “Hands home.”
  • “Chin down, eyes up.”
  • “Ready to move.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing too tall
  • Feet too close together
  • Hands dropping after movement
  • Leaning too far forward

Drill Ideas

  • Stance check rounds
  • Coach movement calls
  • Shadowboxing with stance resets

Progression

  • Connect to Guard
  • Connect to Footwork
