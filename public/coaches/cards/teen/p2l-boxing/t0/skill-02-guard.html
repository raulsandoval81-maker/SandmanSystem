Skill 02 — Guard

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Hands high with chin protected

B — Elbows protect the body while eyes stay forward

C — Return hands to guard after every punch or movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Hands home.”
  • “Protect your chin.”
  • “Elbows tight.”
  • “See through the guard.”

Common Mistakes

  • Hands dropping after punches
  • Elbows flaring wide
  • Looking down while defending
  • Reaching instead of staying protected

Drill Ideas

  • Guard hold rounds
  • Touch defense drills
  • Shadowboxing with guard recovery

Progression

  • Connect to Jab
  • Connect to Defense Shell
