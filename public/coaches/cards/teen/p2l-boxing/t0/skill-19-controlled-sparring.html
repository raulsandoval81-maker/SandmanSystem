Skill 19 — Controlled Sparring

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply beginner boxing skills in live controlled rounds

B — Focus on safety, composure, and clean technique

C — Maintain control regardless of pace or pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Technical work.”
  • “Stay controlled.”
  • “Protect your partner.”
  • “Learn, don’t brawl.”

Common Mistakes

  • Going too hard emotionally
  • Closing eyes during exchanges
  • Throwing wild punches
  • Ignoring defense under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-only sparring
  • Controlled touch sparring
  • Movement-only live rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Finish Standard
  • Connect to Teen P2L Boxing T1
