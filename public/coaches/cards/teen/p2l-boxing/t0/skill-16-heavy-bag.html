Skill 16 — Heavy Bag

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Strike the bag with clean boxing mechanics

B — Maintain stance, guard, and balance while punching

C — Reset after combinations without admiring punches

Coaching Cues

  • “Punch and recover.”
  • “Don’t chase the bag.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Technique before power.”

Common Mistakes

  • Pushing punches
  • Standing too close
  • Dropping hands after combinations
  • Watching punches land

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-only bag rounds
  • Combination bag rounds
  • Movement and punch rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Partner Drill
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
