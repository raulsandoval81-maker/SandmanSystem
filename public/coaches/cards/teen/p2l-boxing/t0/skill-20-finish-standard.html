Skill 20 — Finish Standard

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Finish rounds with effort, discipline, and composure

B — Maintain boxing structure while tired

C — Show respect, focus, and clean habits through the end

Coaching Cues

  • “Finish strong.”
  • “Stay disciplined tired.”
  • “Protect your structure.”
  • “Last reps matter.”

Common Mistakes

  • Quitting mentally late
  • Dropping stance under fatigue
  • Ignoring defense while tired
  • Losing focus near the end

Drill Ideas

  • End-of-round sprint drills
  • Fatigue shadowboxing rounds
  • Finish-hard bag rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Teen P2L Boxing T1
  • Connect to Testing Readiness
