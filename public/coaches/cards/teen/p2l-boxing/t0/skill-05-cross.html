Skill 05 — Cross

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Rear hand drives straight from guard

B — Hip and shoulder rotate through the punch

C — Return to stance and guard after the cross

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn the hip.”
  • “Straight down the middle.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Recover fast.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing the cross wide
  • Dropping the lead hand
  • Overreaching forward
  • Standing square after the punch

Drill Ideas

  • Cross mechanics shadowboxing
  • Jab-cross mitt rounds
  • Heavy bag cross rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Hook
  • Connect to Combination 1
