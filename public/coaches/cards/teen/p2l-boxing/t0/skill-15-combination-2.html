Skill 15 — Combination 2

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Link jab, cross, and hook together smoothly

B — Rotate hips and shoulders through the sequence

C — Finish balanced and ready to defend

Coaching Cues

  • “Flow the punches.”
  • “Turn the hips.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Hands recover.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing punches separately
  • Overrotating hooks
  • Dropping hands during combinations
  • Standing flat-footed

Drill Ideas

  • Three-punch mitt rounds
  • Heavy bag combo rounds
  • Shadowboxing flow drills

Progression

  • Connect to Heavy Bag
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
