Skill 03 — Footwork

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Move without crossing the feet

B — Keep stance while moving forward, backward, and side to side

C — Stay balanced before and after punches

Coaching Cues

  • “Step, don’t cross.”
  • “Feet under you.”
  • “Stay ready to punch.”
  • “Move smooth.”

Common Mistakes

  • Crossing feet
  • Taking steps too big
  • Standing upright while moving
  • Losing stance after movement

Drill Ideas

  • Line footwork drills
  • Circle movement rounds
  • Shadowboxing movement-only rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Distance
  • Connect to Angle Step
