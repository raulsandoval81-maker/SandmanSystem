Skill 12 — Distance

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Understand safe punching and defensive range

B — Use footwork to enter and exit cleanly

C — Stay balanced while changing distance

Coaching Cues

  • “Own your range.”
  • “In and out.”
  • “Don’t reach.”
  • “Feet before punches.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing too close without defense
  • Reaching from too far away
  • Backing straight up
  • Crossing feet while moving

Drill Ideas

  • Range touch drills
  • Jab distance rounds
  • Entry-exit shadowboxing

Progression

  • Connect to Angle Step
  • Connect to Combination 1
