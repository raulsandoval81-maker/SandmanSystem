Skill 07 — Uppercut

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Load slightly with knees while keeping eyes up

B — Drive upward with a short punch path

C — Recover immediately to guard and stance

Coaching Cues

  • “Short path up.”
  • “Use the legs.”
  • “Stay tight.”
  • “Eyes up.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dipping too low
  • Swinging the punch wide
  • Dropping the opposite hand
  • Losing balance upward

Drill Ideas

  • Uppercut mitt rounds
  • Close-range bag rounds
  • Hook-uppercut drills

Progression

  • Connect to Combination 2
  • Connect to Heavy Bag
