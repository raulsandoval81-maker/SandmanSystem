Skill 08 — Defense Shell

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Cover head and body with compact guard

B — Keep elbows tight and eyes forward under pressure

C — Reset or answer after defending

Coaching Cues

  • “Shell up, don’t panic.”
  • “Elbows tight.”
  • “Eyes open.”
  • “Defend then move.”

Common Mistakes

  • Opening the guard under pressure
  • Looking down while defending
  • Freezing after covering
  • Standing too tall in the shell

Drill Ideas

  • High guard defense rounds
  • Coach touch reaction drills
  • Defense shell to reset drills

Progression

  • Connect to Slip
  • Connect to Roll
