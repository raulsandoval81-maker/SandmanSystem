Skill 18 — Conditioning

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build boxing endurance and movement capacity

B — Maintain stance and breathing while tired

C — Finish rounds with effort and discipline

Coaching Cues

  • “Move while tired.”
  • “Breathe and work.”
  • “Strong stance late.”
  • “Finish the round.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing upright when tired
  • Holding breath during work
  • Dropping hands under fatigue
  • Quitting movement late

Drill Ideas

  • Jump rope rounds
  • Shadowboxing conditioning circuits
  • Heavy bag sprint rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
  • Connect to Finish Standard
