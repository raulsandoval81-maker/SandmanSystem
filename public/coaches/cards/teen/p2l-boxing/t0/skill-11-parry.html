Skill 11 — Parry

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Redirect the incoming punch with a small hand motion

B — Keep the opposite hand protecting the chin

C — Return immediately to guard or counter position

Coaching Cues

  • “Small touch.”
  • “Don’t swat.”
  • “Other hand home.”
  • “Parry and reset.”

Common Mistakes

  • Reaching too far
  • Dropping the opposite hand
  • Swatting wide
  • Standing still after defense

Drill Ideas

  • Jab parry partner drill
  • Parry-reset rounds
  • Parry-counter mitt work

Progression

  • Connect to Distance
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
