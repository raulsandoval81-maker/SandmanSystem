Skill 10 — Roll

Journey: Teen P2L — Boxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Bend knees and lower level under the punch

B — Roll under while keeping balance and vision

C — Rise back into stance ready to defend or answer

Coaching Cues

  • “Bend the legs.”
  • “Stay compact.”
  • “Eyes stay up.”
  • “Come up ready.”

Common Mistakes

  • Bending only at the waist
  • Looking down during the roll
  • Rolling too wide
  • Losing stance after the roll

Drill Ideas

  • Slip rope rolling drill
  • Hook defense partner drill
  • Roll and reset rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Parry
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
