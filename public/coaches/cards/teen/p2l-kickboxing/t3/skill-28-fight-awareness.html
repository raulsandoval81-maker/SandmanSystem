Skill 28 — Fight Awareness

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain awareness of pace, positioning, pressure, and reactions

B — Blend tactical thinking into live exchanges naturally

C — Stay composed and adaptive under chaos and fatigue

Coaching Cues

  • “See the whole fight.”
  • “Stay aware under pressure.”
  • “Think while moving.”
  • “Adapt live.”

Common Mistakes

  • Tunnel vision during exchanges
  • Ignoring ring position
  • Fighting emotionally
  • Losing tactical awareness when tired

Drill Ideas

  • Tactical sparring rounds
  • Adaptive pressure drills
  • Situational fight scenarios

Progression

  • Connect to Chaos Drills
  • Connect to Second Effort
