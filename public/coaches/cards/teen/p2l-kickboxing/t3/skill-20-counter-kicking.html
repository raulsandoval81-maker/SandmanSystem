Skill 20 — Counter Kicking

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Counter attacks through kick timing and reactions

B — Blend checks, movement, and counter kicks fluidly

C — Maintain balance and composure during exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Counter the opening.”
  • “Stay balanced on return.”
  • “Defend then attack.”
  • “Flow through the reaction.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing kicks too late
  • Dropping hands during counters
  • Overcommitting balance
  • Forcing reactions emotionally

Drill Ideas

  • Check-counter kick rounds
  • Reaction kicking drills
  • Counter sparring exchanges

Progression

  • Connect to Pocket Awareness
  • Connect to Defensive Layers
