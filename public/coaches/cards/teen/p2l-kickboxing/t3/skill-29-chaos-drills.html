Skill 29 — Chaos Drills

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Perform technically during unpredictable pressure situations

B — Blend reactions, movement, and composure under chaos

C — Maintain structure and decision-making while stressed

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay composed in chaos.”
  • “Trust your training.”
  • “Keep structure under pressure.”
  • “React intelligently.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking under unpredictable pressure
  • Breaking posture emotionally
  • Throwing wild combinations
  • Forgetting tactical movement

Drill Ideas

  • Chaos sparring rounds
  • Random reaction drills
  • Pressure adaptation scenarios

Progression

  • Connect to Second Effort
