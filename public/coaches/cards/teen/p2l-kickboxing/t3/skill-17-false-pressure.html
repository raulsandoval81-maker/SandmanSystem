Skill 17 — False Pressure

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create reactions using fake pressure and movement

B — Blend feints, entries, and exits into tactical setups

C — Stay balanced and composed during pressure changes

Coaching Cues

  • “Sell the pressure.”
  • “Make them react.”
  • “Don’t rush the trap.”
  • “Flow in and out.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overcommitting fake entries
  • Standing still after feints
  • Forcing reactions emotionally
  • Losing defensive posture

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure-feint sparring
  • Fake-entry reaction drills
  • Trap-building partner work

Progression

  • Connect to Angle Traps
  • Connect to Rope Escapes
