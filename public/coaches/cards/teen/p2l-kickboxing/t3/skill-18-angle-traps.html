Skill 18 — Angle Traps

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create offensive openings through angles and positioning

B — Blend movement, timing, and pressure into trap systems

C — Maintain balance and awareness during exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Create the opening.”
  • “Trap with movement.”
  • “Control the angle.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Forcing angles too aggressively
  • Crossing feet during movement
  • Standing square after traps
  • Ignoring defensive structure

Drill Ideas

  • Angle trap sparring
  • Movement-and-counter drills
  • Pressure-angle rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Rope Escapes
  • Connect to Counter Kicking
