Skill 30 — Second Effort

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Continue performing after the first effort fails

B — Maintain composure, pressure, and tactical awareness while tired

C — Recover mentally and physically during hard exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Go again.”
  • “Stay composed under fatigue.”
  • “Second effort wins fights.”
  • “Keep moving forward.”

Common Mistakes

  • Mentally quitting after resistance
  • Breaking posture under fatigue
  • Stopping movement after failed attacks
  • Losing discipline during hard exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Second-effort sparring
  • Fatigue pressure drills
  • Recovery-and-return rounds

Progression

  • Connect to T4 Kickboxing Readiness
  • Connect to Championship Development
