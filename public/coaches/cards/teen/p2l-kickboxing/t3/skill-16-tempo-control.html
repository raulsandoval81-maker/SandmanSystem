Skill 16 — Tempo Control

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control pace and rhythm during live exchanges

B — Blend slow-fast timing changes into pressure and counters

C — Maintain composure while dictating fight tempo

Coaching Cues

  • “Control the pace.”
  • “Break the rhythm.”
  • “Stay relaxed.”
  • “Fight at your speed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Fighting at one speed constantly
  • Rushing exchanges emotionally
  • Burning energy too early
  • Losing composure under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Tempo sparring rounds
  • Slow-fast reaction drills
  • Pace-change pad work

Progression

  • Connect to False Pressure
  • Connect to Angle Traps
