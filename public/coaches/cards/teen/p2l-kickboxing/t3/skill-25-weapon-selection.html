Skill 25 — Weapon Selection

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Select the correct offensive tools during live exchanges

B — Blend tactical awareness into reactions and setups

C — Maintain composure while adapting under pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Choose the right weapon.”
  • “Don’t force attacks.”
  • “Read the opening.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing predictable attacks
  • Forcing favorite techniques
  • Ignoring openings
  • Losing tactical awareness

Drill Ideas

  • Situational sparring
  • Reaction weapon drills
  • Tactical exchange rounds
