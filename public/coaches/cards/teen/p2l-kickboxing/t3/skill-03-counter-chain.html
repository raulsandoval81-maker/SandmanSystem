Skill 03 — Counter Chain

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Blend multiple counters together under live pressure

B — Flow naturally between defense and offense

C — Maintain balance and composure during counter exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Counter the reaction.”
  • “Stay fluid.”
  • “Flow into the next opening.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after the first counter
  • Throwing emotional combinations
  • Dropping defense during counters
  • Overcommitting balance

Drill Ideas

  • Counter-chain sparring
  • Reaction-counter drills
  • Multi-counter pad rounds
