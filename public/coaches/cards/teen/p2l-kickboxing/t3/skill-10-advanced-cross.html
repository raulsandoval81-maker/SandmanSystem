Skill 10 — Advanced Cross

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Deliver advanced rear-hand offense with timing and setups

B — Blend movement, counters, and pressure into cross attacks

C — Maintain balance and defensive structure during exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Build the shot.”
  • “Drive through clean.”
  • “Recover immediately.”
  • “Stay balanced.”

Common Mistakes

  • Forcing the rear hand
  • Overcommitting weight forward
  • Dropping the lead hand
  • Standing still after impact

Drill Ideas

  • Cross-counter sparring
  • Setup-to-cross pad drills
  • Pressure-cross rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Lead Hook System
  • Connect to Rear Kick System
