Skill 14 — Head Movement

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Move the head safely during live striking exchanges

B — Blend slips, rolls, and angles into defensive reactions

C — Recover into balanced offensive or defensive position

Coaching Cues

  • “Move with purpose.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Defend without panic.”
  • “Slip and return.”

Common Mistakes

  • Moving too large
  • Dropping eyes during movement
  • Breaking stance while slipping
  • Forgetting counters after defense

Drill Ideas

  • Slip-counter drills
  • Reaction head movement rounds
  • Defensive sparring exchanges

Progression

  • Connect to Combination Defense
  • Connect to Tempo Control
