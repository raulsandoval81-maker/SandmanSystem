Skill 09 — Advanced Jab

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Use the jab to control pace, reactions, and positioning

B — Blend feints, movement, and pressure through jab systems

C — Maintain balance and defensive structure during exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “The jab controls everything.”
  • “Use it to build reactions.”
  • “Stay behind the jab.”
  • “Pressure intelligently.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing lazy jabs
  • Standing still after extension
  • Overreaching range
  • Failing to build setups

Drill Ideas

  • Advanced jab sparring
  • Feint-jab pressure drills
  • Double/triple jab rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Advanced Cross
  • Connect to Lead Hook System
