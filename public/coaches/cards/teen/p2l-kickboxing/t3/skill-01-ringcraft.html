Skill 01 — Ringcraft

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control space, angles, and positioning during live exchanges

B — Blend movement, pressure, and awareness together

C — Maintain composure and tactical control under pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the space.”
  • “Control where the fight happens.”
  • “Move with purpose.”
  • “Stay aware.”

Common Mistakes

  • Giving up center position
  • Backing straight up repeatedly
  • Standing still under pressure
  • Losing awareness during exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Ring-control sparring
  • Pressure-and-angle rounds
  • Space management drills

Progression

  • Connect to Pressure Flow
  • Connect to Corner Pressure
