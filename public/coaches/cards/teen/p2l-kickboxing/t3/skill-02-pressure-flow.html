Skill 02 — Pressure Flow

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply forward pressure without losing structure

B — Blend offense, movement, and defensive awareness fluidly

C — Maintain balance and composure while advancing

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure with control.”
  • “Flow forward.”
  • “Cut space intelligently.”
  • “Don’t rush.”

Common Mistakes

  • Charging straight into range
  • Dropping guard while pressuring
  • Forcing exchanges emotionally
  • Losing balance during attacks

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure sparring rounds
  • Forward-flow pad drills
  • Controlled pressure partner work

Progression

  • Connect to Counter Chain
  • Connect to Corner Pressure
