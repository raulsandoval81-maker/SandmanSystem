Skill 22 — Defensive Layers

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Blend multiple defensive systems together under pressure

B — Use guard, movement, checks, slips, and counters fluidly

C — Maintain composure and recovery during live exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Defend in layers.”
  • “Never stop moving.”
  • “Recover immediately.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Relying on one defensive reaction
  • Freezing during pressure
  • Dropping posture after defense
  • Panicking under combinations

Drill Ideas

  • Layered defense sparring
  • Reaction-defense drills
  • Pressure-defense rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Offensive Layers
  • Connect to Pace Break
