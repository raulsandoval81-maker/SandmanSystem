Skill 04 — Reaction Training

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Improve reactions under live speed and pressure

B — Blend defense, movement, and counters instinctively

C — Maintain composure during unpredictable exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “React naturally.”
  • “Stay calm under speed.”
  • “See before reacting.”
  • “Trust your training.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking during fast exchanges
  • Freezing under pressure
  • Overreacting to feints
  • Breaking stance during reactions

Drill Ideas

  • Reaction sparring rounds
  • Coach-call reaction drills
  • Fast-exchange partner work

Progression

  • Connect to Setup System
  • Connect to Advanced Jab
