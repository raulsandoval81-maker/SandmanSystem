Skill 12 — Rear Kick System

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build setups, counters, and pressure around rear-leg kicking

B — Blend kicks naturally into combinations and reactions

C — Maintain balance and recovery after impact

Coaching Cues

  • “Set up the kick.”
  • “Kick with balance.”
  • “Recover immediately.”
  • “Flow through the exchange.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing naked kicks
  • Dropping hands during kicks
  • Losing balance after impact
  • Standing square after recovery

Drill Ideas

  • Kick setup rounds
  • Rear-kick counter drills
  • Pressure kick sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Body Attack
  • Connect to Head Movement
