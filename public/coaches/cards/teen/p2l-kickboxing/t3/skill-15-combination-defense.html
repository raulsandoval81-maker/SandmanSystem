Skill 15 — Combination Defense

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Defend layered combinations under pressure

B — Blend movement, guard, checks, and counters together

C — Maintain composure and posture during exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay composed under fire.”
  • “Defend in layers.”
  • “Recover immediately.”
  • “Don’t freeze.”

Common Mistakes

  • Blocking only the first strike
  • Panicking during pressure
  • Standing square while defending
  • Dropping posture after contact

Drill Ideas

  • Combination defense rounds
  • Pressure-defense partner work
  • Defend-and-return drills

Progression

  • Connect to Tempo Control
  • Connect to False Pressure
