Skill 11 — Lead Hook System

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build offensive and defensive systems around the lead hook

B — Blend hooks into pressure, counters, and angle changes

C — Maintain posture and balance through rotational offense

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn with balance.”
  • “Hook with structure.”
  • “Flow into angles.”
  • “Recover fast.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing wide hooks
  • Over-rotating balance
  • Dropping the rear hand
  • Standing still after hooks

Drill Ideas

  • Lead-hook counter rounds
  • Hook-angle pad drills
  • Pressure hook sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Rear Kick System
  • Connect to Body Attack
