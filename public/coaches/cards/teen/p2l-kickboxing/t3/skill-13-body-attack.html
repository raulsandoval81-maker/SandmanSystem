Skill 13 — Body Attack

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Attack the body strategically through punches and kicks

B — Blend level changes into pressure and combinations

C — Maintain defensive awareness during offensive exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Change levels.”
  • “Break rhythm through the body.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Flow back to defense.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping the head too low
  • Overcommitting body shots
  • Ignoring counters
  • Standing still after attacks

Drill Ideas

  • Body-shot sparring rounds
  • Level-change pad drills
  • Body-to-head combination work

Progression

  • Connect to Head Movement
  • Connect to Combination Defense
