Skill 07 — Inside Kickboxing

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Fight effectively at close range under pressure

B — Blend short strikes, defense, and movement together

C — Maintain composure and posture during inside exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay compact.”
  • “Protect while attacking.”
  • “Control the inside.”
  • “Short and sharp.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing wide punches inside
  • Standing too tall in exchanges
  • Ignoring defensive positioning
  • Panicking under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Inside fighting rounds
  • Short-combo pad drills
  • Clinch-break exchanges

Progression

  • Connect to Clinch Entry
  • Connect to Advanced Jab
