Skill 08 — Clinch Entry

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Enter clinch positions safely and under control

B — Blend strikes, posture, and pressure into entries

C — Maintain balance and defensive awareness during transitions

Coaching Cues

  • “Enter with structure.”
  • “Protect your posture.”
  • “Control the tie.”
  • “Stay balanced.”

Common Mistakes

  • Diving into the clinch recklessly
  • Dropping posture during entries
  • Ignoring defensive positioning
  • Standing square after contact

Drill Ideas

  • Clinch-entry partner drills
  • Strike-to-clinch rounds
  • Controlled clinch sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Advanced Jab
  • Connect to Advanced Cross
