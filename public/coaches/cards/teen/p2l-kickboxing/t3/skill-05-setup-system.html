Skill 05 — Setup System

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build offensive openings through feints, timing, and pressure

B — Blend setups naturally into combinations and counters

C — Maintain composure and tactical patience during exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Create the opening.”
  • “Set traps intelligently.”
  • “Don’t force offense.”
  • “Build the reaction.”

Common Mistakes

  • Attacking without setup
  • Over-feinting unnecessarily
  • Telegraphing intentions
  • Rushing combinations emotionally

Drill Ideas

  • Feint-to-counter drills
  • Setup sparring rounds
  • Reaction-building partner work

Progression

  • Connect to Corner Pressure
  • Connect to Inside Kickboxing
