Skill 23 — Offensive Layers

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build layered offensive systems through setups and reactions

B — Blend combinations, counters, pressure, and angles together

C — Maintain balance and awareness during aggressive exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Build the attack.”
  • “Layer the reactions.”
  • “Pressure intelligently.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing random combinations
  • Attacking without setup
  • Overcommitting emotionally
  • Losing defensive awareness

Drill Ideas

  • Layered offense rounds
  • Setup-counter drills
  • Pressure combination sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Pace Break
  • Connect to Weapon Selection
