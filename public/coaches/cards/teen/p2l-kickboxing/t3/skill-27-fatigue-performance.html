Skill 27 — Fatigue Performance

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Perform technically while exhausted and pressured

B — Maintain composure, breathing, and structure under fatigue

C — Finish rounds with discipline and awareness intact

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay technical while tired.”
  • “Breathe and move.”
  • “Don’t break structure.”
  • “Finish strong.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping hands under fatigue
  • Throwing emotional attacks
  • Standing flat-footed
  • Losing composure when tired

Drill Ideas

  • Late-round sparring
  • Conditioning exchanges
  • Fatigue pressure drills

Progression

  • Connect to Fight Awareness
  • Connect to Chaos Drills
