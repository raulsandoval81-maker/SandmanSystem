Skill 24 — Pace Break

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Break opponent rhythm through timing and pressure changes

B — Blend slow-fast tempo transitions into live exchanges

C — Maintain composure while controlling pace

Coaching Cues

  • “Break the rhythm.”
  • “Control the pace.”
  • “Stay relaxed.”
  • “Change speeds intelligently.”

Common Mistakes

  • Moving at one speed constantly
  • Rushing attacks emotionally
  • Burning energy too early
  • Ignoring tactical timing

Drill Ideas

  • Pace-change sparring
  • Tempo disruption drills
  • Slow-fast combination rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Weapon Selection
  • Connect to Live Rounds
