Skill 19 — Rope Escapes

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Escape pressure safely from ropes or corners

B — Blend movement, defense, and angles under pressure

C — Recover position and composure after escapes

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t stay trapped.”
  • “Angle out.”
  • “Defend while moving.”
  • “Recover immediately.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing straight into pressure
  • Freezing near boundaries
  • Escaping without defense
  • Standing square after exits

Drill Ideas

  • Corner escape rounds
  • Pressure-exit sparring
  • Defensive movement drills

Progression

  • Connect to Counter Kicking
  • Connect to Pocket Awareness
