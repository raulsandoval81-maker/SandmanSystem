Skill 21 — Pocket Awareness

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Stay composed during close-range exchanges

B — Blend offense, defense, and reactions inside the pocket

C — Maintain posture and awareness under pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay calm inside.”
  • “See the openings.”
  • “Protect while attacking.”
  • “Don’t panic in exchanges.”

Common Mistakes

  • Freezing under pressure
  • Throwing wild combinations
  • Ignoring defensive structure
  • Standing square in exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Pocket sparring rounds
  • Short-range reaction drills
  • Inside combination exchanges

Progression

  • Connect to Defensive Layers
  • Connect to Offensive Layers
