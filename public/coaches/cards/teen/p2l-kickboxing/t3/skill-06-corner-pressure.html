Skill 06 — Corner Pressure

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T3

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply intelligent pressure near ropes or corners

B — Cut exits while maintaining balance and defensive structure

C — Control pace and positioning without rushing emotionally

Coaching Cues

  • “Cut the exit.”
  • “Pressure with control.”
  • “Don’t chase.”
  • “Own the space.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overcommitting while pressuring
  • Running straight into counters
  • Standing square near corners
  • Throwing emotional combinations

Drill Ideas

  • Corner pressure sparring
  • Ring-cutting drills
  • Pressure-and-trap rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Inside Kickboxing
  • Connect to Clinch Entry
