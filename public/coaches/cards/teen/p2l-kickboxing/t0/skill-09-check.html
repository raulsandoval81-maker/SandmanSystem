Skill 09 — Check

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Lift the leg to defend incoming kicks safely

B — Maintain balance and guard during the defense

C — Recover immediately back to stance and readiness

Coaching Cues

  • “Knee up.”
  • “Stay tall.”
  • “Hands protect.”
  • “Reset fast.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping hands during the check
  • Turning sideways too far
  • Losing balance
  • Checking too low

Drill Ideas

  • Partner check drills
  • Kick-and-check rounds
  • Defensive movement rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
  • Connect to Distance
