Skill 12 — Basic Clinch

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Establish safe head and hand position in the clinch

B — Maintain posture and balance while controlling contact

C — Exit the clinch under control and reset stance

Coaching Cues

  • “Head up.”
  • “Strong posture.”
  • “Control inside space.”
  • “Don’t hang.”

Common Mistakes

  • Bending at the waist
  • Loose hand control
  • Standing square
  • Losing balance during contact

Drill Ideas

  • Clinch position rounds
  • Pummel and posture drills
  • Clinch-to-knee entries

Progression

  • Connect to Knee
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
