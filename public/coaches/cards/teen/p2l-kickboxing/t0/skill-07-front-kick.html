Skill 07 — Front Kick

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Lift knee first and extend the kick straight forward

B — Strike with balance and controlled posture

C — Recover the leg quickly back to stance

Coaching Cues

  • “Knee first.”
  • “Kick through the target.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Bring it back fast.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping hands during the kick
  • Leaning backward too far
  • Pushing without chambering
  • Falling out of stance

Drill Ideas

  • Front kick air reps
  • Pad front kick rounds
  • Kick-reset movement drills

Progression

  • Connect to Teep
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
