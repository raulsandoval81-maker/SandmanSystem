Skill 13 — Distance

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Understand safe striking and defensive range

B — Enter and exit distance with control

C — Stay balanced while managing space

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the space.”
  • “Don’t crowd yourself.”
  • “See range before striking.”
  • “Exit clean.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing too close
  • Reaching while attacking
  • Backing straight up
  • Losing stance while moving

Drill Ideas

  • Partner range drills
  • Touch-and-exit rounds
  • Movement spacing games

Progression

  • Connect to Angle Step
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
