Skill 04 — Jab

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Fire the lead hand straight from stance

B — Use the jab to manage distance and start offense

C — Return immediately to guard and stance

Coaching Cues

  • “Straight out, straight back.”
  • “Touch the target.”
  • “Hand comes home.”
  • “Use it to measure.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping the rear hand
  • Reaching with the chin
  • Slow return to guard
  • Throwing without stance

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-only shadow rounds
  • Jab to pad touch
  • Jab and exit drills

Progression

  • Connect to Cross
  • Connect to Basic Combination
