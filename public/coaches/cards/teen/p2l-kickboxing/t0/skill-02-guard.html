Skill 02 — Guard

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Protect chin, head, and body with disciplined hand position

B — Return hands after every strike

C — Keep guard active while moving, kicking, or defending

Coaching Cues

  • “Hands come home.”
  • “Protect first.”
  • “Don’t admire your strike.”
  • “Guard travels with you.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping hands after punches
  • Opening the elbows too wide
  • Lifting chin
  • Guard disappearing during kicks

Drill Ideas

  • Mirror guard checks
  • Punch-return drills
  • Move with guard rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Jab
  • Connect to Check
