Skill 17 — Heavy Bag

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Strike the bag with balance, control, and clean mechanics

B — Maintain stance, guard, and breathing during work rounds

C — Finish each exchange with recovery and reset

Coaching Cues

  • “Hit clean.”
  • “Don’t chase the bag.”
  • “Hands come home.”
  • “Reset after every combo.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing too close to the bag
  • Pushing punches instead of striking
  • Dropping hands during combinations
  • Losing balance after kicks

Drill Ideas

  • Timed heavy bag rounds
  • One-combo reset rounds
  • Punch-kick bag flow drills

Progression

  • Connect to Conditioning
  • Connect to Finish Standard
