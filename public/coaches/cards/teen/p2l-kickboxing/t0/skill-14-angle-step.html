Skill 14 — Angle Step

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Move off the center line with controlled footwork

B — Maintain stance and vision while creating angles

C — Reset into offensive or defensive position after movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Move off line.”
  • “Don’t cross your feet.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Create better position.”

Common Mistakes

  • Crossing feet during movement
  • Turning the back to the opponent
  • Moving without balance
  • Standing still after angling

Drill Ideas

  • Angle step shadow rounds
  • Partner angle drills
  • Strike-and-angle movement rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Basic Combination
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
