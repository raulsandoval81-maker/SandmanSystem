Skill 16 — Pad Work

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Strike pads with control, balance, and proper mechanics

B — Listen and respond to coaching commands during rounds

C — Maintain pace, posture, and composure through work periods

Coaching Cues

  • “Sharp and clean.”
  • “Hit with purpose.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Work with discipline.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing wild strikes
  • Losing stance after combinations
  • Ignoring defensive recovery
  • Working too fast without control

Drill Ideas

  • Timed pad rounds
  • Command-based striking drills
  • Pad flow combinations

Progression

  • Connect to Heavy Bag
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
