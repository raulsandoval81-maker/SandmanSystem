Skill 19 — Conditioning

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build work capacity through kickboxing-specific effort

B — Maintain stance, guard, and breathing while tired

C — Finish rounds with discipline instead of sloppy movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay sharp when tired.”
  • “Breathe and work.”
  • “Finish clean.”
  • “Effort with form.”

Common Mistakes

  • Quitting posture under fatigue
  • Dropping hands when tired
  • Rushing instead of working clean
  • Letting effort erase technique

Drill Ideas

  • Kickboxing conditioning rounds
  • Bag sprint intervals
  • Technique-under-fatigue drills

Progression

  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
  • Connect to Finish Standard
