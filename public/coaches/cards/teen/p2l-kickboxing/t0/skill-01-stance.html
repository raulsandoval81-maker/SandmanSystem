Skill 01 — Stance

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build an athletic kickboxing stance with balance and posture

B — Keep hands ready, chin protected, and feet under control

C — Reset stance after movement, strikes, or contact

Coaching Cues

  • “Hands up.”
  • “Chin tucked.”
  • “Feet under you.”
  • “Ready to punch, kick, or move.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing too tall
  • Feet too narrow or too wide
  • Hands dropping
  • Crossing feet while moving

Drill Ideas

  • Stance hold rounds
  • Move and freeze stance checks
  • Reset after command drills

Progression

  • Connect to Guard
  • Connect to Footwork
