Skill 10 — Knee

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Drive the knee upward with posture and balance

B — Use hips and core to generate controlled power

C — Return quickly to stance after the strike

Coaching Cues

  • “Drive the hip.”
  • “Stay tall.”
  • “Hands active.”
  • “Reset after contact.”

Common Mistakes

  • Leaning backward excessively
  • Dropping hands
  • Weak hip engagement
  • Falling forward after the strike

Drill Ideas

  • Knee strike repetitions
  • Pad knee rounds
  • Clinch-to-knee drills

Progression

  • Connect to Basic Clinch
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
