Skill 15 — Basic Combination

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Combine basic punches and kicks with flow

B — Maintain stance, balance, and guard during combinations

C — Finish combinations under control and reset

Coaching Cues

  • “Everything connects.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Hands return home.”
  • “Flow, don’t rush.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing too fast without control
  • Dropping hands during combinations
  • Losing stance between strikes
  • Stopping movement after attacks

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-cross-hook rounds
  • Punch-to-kick combinations
  • Pad combination drills

Progression

  • Connect to Pad Work
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
