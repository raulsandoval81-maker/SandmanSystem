Skill 08 — Round Kick

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Rotate hips and shoulders through the kick

B — Strike with proper shin and body alignment

C — Recover balance and return to stance after impact

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn the hip over.”
  • “Whip the kick.”
  • “Hands stay active.”
  • “Land balanced.”

Common Mistakes

  • Kicking with the foot instead of shin
  • Not pivoting the base foot
  • Dropping hands
  • Falling after the kick

Drill Ideas

  • Round kick shadow rounds
  • Pad kick repetition
  • Kick-return movement drills

Progression

  • Connect to Check
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
