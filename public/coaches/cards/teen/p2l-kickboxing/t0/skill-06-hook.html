Skill 06 — Hook

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Deliver a tight hook with balance and rotation

B — Keep elbow aligned and strike from proper range

C — Return to guard immediately after impact

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn the hip.”
  • “Short and tight.”
  • “Don’t swing wide.”
  • “Hands come home.”

Common Mistakes

  • Looping the punch too wide
  • Dropping the opposite hand
  • Over-rotating balance
  • Standing too close or too far

Drill Ideas

  • Lead hook shadow rounds
  • Hook on pads
  • Hook-return-reset drills

Progression

  • Connect to Basic Combination
  • Connect to Pad Work
