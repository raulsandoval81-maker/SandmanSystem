Skill 03 — Footwork

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Move forward, backward, and side-to-side without crossing feet

B — Keep stance while entering and exiting range

C — Reset balance before striking or defending

Coaching Cues

  • “Step, don’t reach.”
  • “Move your base.”
  • “Balance before power.”
  • “Exit clean.”

Common Mistakes

  • Crossing feet
  • Bouncing without control
  • Leaning instead of stepping
  • Losing stance after movement

Drill Ideas

  • Line movement drills
  • Step-and-reset rounds
  • Partner range movement

Progression

  • Connect to Distance
  • Connect to Angle Step
