Skill 05 — Cross

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Drive the rear hand straight through the target

B — Rotate hip, shoulder, and rear foot together

C — Recover back to stance and guard after impact

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn the hip.”
  • “Straight line.”
  • “Rear hand comes home.”
  • “Don’t fall in.”

Common Mistakes

  • Arm punching
  • Overreaching
  • Rear hand returning low
  • Losing balance after the punch

Drill Ideas

  • Cross mechanics on air
  • Jab-cross pad rounds
  • Cross and reset drills

Progression

  • Connect to Hook
  • Connect to Basic Combination
