Skill 20 — Controlled Sparring

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply basic kickboxing skills with control and safety

B — Maintain distance, guard, stance, and composure under pressure

C — Respect partner, rules, and coach direction during live work

Coaching Cues

  • “Control first.”
  • “Use what you know.”
  • “Stay composed.”
  • “Protect your partner.”

Common Mistakes

  • Turning controlled work into a fight
  • Forgetting defense under pressure
  • Ignoring distance
  • Getting emotional during contact

Drill Ideas

  • Touch-contact sparring rounds
  • Jab-only controlled rounds
  • Kickboxing rule-based sparring games

Progression

  • Connect to Finish Standard
  • Connect to T1 Kickboxing Readiness
