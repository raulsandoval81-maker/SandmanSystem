Skill 11 — Teep

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Use the teep to manage distance and disrupt pressure

B — Drive from the hips while maintaining balance

C — Recover quickly back to stance after extension

Coaching Cues

  • “Push through the target.”
  • “Balance first.”
  • “Hands stay active.”
  • “Reset fast.”

Common Mistakes

  • Leaning backward excessively
  • Dropping hands during the kick
  • Pushing without hip engagement
  • Falling out of stance after contact

Drill Ideas

  • Teep distance drills
  • Partner push-kick rounds
  • Teep and reset movement drills

Progression

  • Connect to Distance
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
