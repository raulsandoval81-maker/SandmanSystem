Skill 18 — Partner Drill

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T0

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Work safely with a partner using control and awareness

B — Follow drill rules, spacing, and coach commands

C — Maintain discipline, respect, and composure through contact

Coaching Cues

  • “Protect your partner.”
  • “Control the pace.”
  • “Follow the drill.”
  • “Respect the work.”

Common Mistakes

  • Turning drills into sparring
  • Ignoring spacing
  • Overpowering the partner
  • Losing focus between reps

Drill Ideas

  • Call-and-response partner rounds
  • Defense-only partner drills
  • Controlled entry and exit drills

Progression

  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
  • Connect to Finish Standard
