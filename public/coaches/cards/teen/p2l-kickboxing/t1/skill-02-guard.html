Skill 02 — Guard

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain an active guard during offense and defense

B — Protect head and body while moving through exchanges

C — Recover guard immediately after every strike

Coaching Cues

  • “Hands come home.”
  • “Protect while attacking.”
  • “Guard stays disciplined.”
  • “See through your defense.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping hands during combinations
  • Opening elbows too wide
  • Pulling chin upward
  • Forgetting defense after kicks

Drill Ideas

  • Guard recovery rounds
  • Defense-only movement drills
  • Partner touch-defense rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Block
  • Connect to Controlled Exchanges
