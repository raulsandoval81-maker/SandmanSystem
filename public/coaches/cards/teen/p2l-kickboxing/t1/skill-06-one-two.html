Skill 06 — One Two

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Flow jab to cross with balance and rhythm

B — Maintain guard and stance during the combination

C — Finish the exchange under control and reset

Coaching Cues

  • “Sharp one-two.”
  • “Everything connected.”
  • “Hands recover fast.”
  • “Don’t stand still after.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing punches separately instead of flowing
  • Dropping hands after the cross
  • Losing stance during extension
  • Standing square after combinations

Drill Ideas

  • One-two shadow rounds
  • Pad one-two repetitions
  • One-two and exit drills

Progression

  • Connect to Lead Hook
  • Connect to Basic Combo
