Skill 09 — Uppercut

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Drive the uppercut upward with hips and posture

B — Maintain balance and defensive awareness during close exchanges

C — Recover quickly into stance after contact

Coaching Cues

  • “Drive from the legs.”
  • “Short and sharp.”
  • “Protect your chin.”
  • “Stay compact.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping hands before throwing
  • Leaning too far forward
  • Making the punch too wide
  • Losing posture after impact

Drill Ideas

  • Uppercut shadow rounds
  • Uppercut pad drills
  • Close-range punch combinations

Progression

  • Connect to Body Shot
  • Connect to Basic Combo
