Skill 16 — Distance

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Manage striking range with awareness and control

B — Enter and exit exchanges safely and efficiently

C — Maintain balance and defensive readiness during movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the range.”
  • “Don’t crowd yourself.”
  • “Enter clean, exit clean.”
  • “See the space.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing too close while striking
  • Backing straight up under pressure
  • Reaching instead of stepping
  • Losing stance during exits

Drill Ideas

  • Partner range drills
  • Distance touch rounds
  • Entry-and-exit movement drills

Progression

  • Connect to Angle
  • Connect to Basic Combo
