Skill 13 — Teep

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Use the teep to manage pressure and distance

B — Maintain balance and defensive posture during extension

C — Reset immediately after contact

Coaching Cues

  • “Push through the target.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Use it to control space.”
  • “Recover fast.”

Common Mistakes

  • Leaning backward excessively
  • Dropping hands during extension
  • Failing to reset stance
  • Pushing without hip engagement

Drill Ideas

  • Teep distance rounds
  • Partner pressure teep drills
  • Teep and angle exits

Progression

  • Connect to Distance
  • Connect to Controlled Exchanges
