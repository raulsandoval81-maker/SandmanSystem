Skill 01 — Stance

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain a balanced kickboxing stance during movement and striking

B — Stay prepared for punches, kicks, checks, and defense

C — Reset stance immediately after exchanges or pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Balanced and ready.”
  • “Hands protect first.”
  • “Stay athletic.”
  • “Recover your base.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing too square
  • Losing stance during combinations
  • Crossing feet under pressure
  • Standing too tall

Drill Ideas

  • Movement and reset rounds
  • Stance recovery drills
  • Partner pressure movement drills

Progression

  • Connect to Footwork
  • Connect to Distance
