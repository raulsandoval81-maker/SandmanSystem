Skill 18 — Basic Combo

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Blend punches, kicks, and movement into controlled combinations

B — Maintain stance, guard, and balance during exchanges

C — Recover and reset cleanly after combinations

Coaching Cues

  • “Everything flows.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Hands recover fast.”
  • “Move after combinations.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing combinations too wild
  • Dropping hands after strikes
  • Losing stance during exchanges
  • Standing still after combos

Drill Ideas

  • Punch-kick combination rounds
  • Pad flow drills
  • Angle-and-combo movement rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Partner Drill
  • Connect to Kickboxing Standard
