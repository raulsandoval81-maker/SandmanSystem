Skill 08 — Rear Hook

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Rotate hips and shoulders to generate rear hook power

B — Keep stance and guard disciplined during rotation

C — Recover balance immediately after the strike

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn through the shot.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Protect while attacking.”
  • “Don’t spin out.”

Common Mistakes

  • Over-rotating the body
  • Dropping lead hand
  • Standing too square
  • Losing stance after impact

Drill Ideas

  • Rear hook mechanics drills
  • Hook pad combinations
  • Rear hook angle exits

Progression
