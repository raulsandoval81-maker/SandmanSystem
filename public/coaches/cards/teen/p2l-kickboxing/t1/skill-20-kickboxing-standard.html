Skill 20 — Kickboxing Standard

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply core T1 kickboxing skills with control and structure

B — Blend offense, defense, movement, and discipline together

C — Maintain composure and technical standards under pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Everything connects.”
  • “Stay composed.”
  • “Trust your structure.”
  • “Perform with discipline.”

Common Mistakes

  • Breaking stance during exchanges
  • Getting emotional under pressure
  • Forgetting defense while attacking
  • Losing composure when tired

Drill Ideas

  • Controlled kickboxing rounds
  • Pressure-and-reset drills
  • Technical performance rounds

Progression

  • Connect to T2 Kickboxing Readiness
  • Connect to Advanced Combination Work
