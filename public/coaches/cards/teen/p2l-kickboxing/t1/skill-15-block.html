Skill 15 — Block

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Defend punches and strikes with controlled blocks

B — Maintain stance and vision during defensive exchanges

C — Recover immediately into movement or counters

Coaching Cues

  • “See the strike.”
  • “Block without panic.”
  • “Stay disciplined.”
  • “Defend and move.”

Common Mistakes

  • Closing eyes during defense
  • Overreacting to strikes
  • Losing posture after blocks
  • Standing still after defense

Drill Ideas

  • Partner blocking drills
  • Defense-only reaction rounds
  • Block-and-counter movement drills

Progression

  • Connect to Distance
  • Connect to Partner Drill
