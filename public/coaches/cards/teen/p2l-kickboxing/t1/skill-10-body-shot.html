Skill 10 — Body Shot

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Attack the body with balance and proper level change

B — Maintain defensive awareness while entering range

C — Recover immediately into stance after striking

Coaching Cues

  • “Level change, don’t bend.”
  • “Protect while entering.”
  • “Hit and exit.”
  • “Stay compact.”

Common Mistakes

  • Diving forward at the target
  • Dropping hands while attacking
  • Standing square inside range
  • Not exiting after the shot

Drill Ideas

  • Body shot pad rounds
  • Level change entry drills
  • Head-body combination drills

Progression

  • Connect to Basic Combo
  • Connect to Partner Drill
