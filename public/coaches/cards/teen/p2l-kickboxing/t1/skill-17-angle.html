Skill 17 — Angle

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create better attacking and defensive positions through angles

B — Move off the center line while maintaining stance and balance

C — Recover into offense or defense immediately after movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Move off line.”
  • “Create position.”
  • “Stay balanced while moving.”
  • “Don’t stand in front.”

Common Mistakes

  • Crossing feet during movement
  • Moving without purpose
  • Losing stance after angles
  • Failing to reset after movement

Drill Ideas

  • Angle-and-strike drills
  • Partner angle movement rounds
  • Strike-and-exit pad rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Basic Combo
  • Connect to Partner Drill
