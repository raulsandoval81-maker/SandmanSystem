Skill 07 — Lead Hook

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Deliver a compact lead hook with balance and rotation

B — Maintain defensive structure during the strike

C — Recover immediately into stance after contact

Coaching Cues

  • “Short and tight.”
  • “Turn the hip.”
  • “Don’t swing wide.”
  • “Hands recover.”

Common Mistakes

  • Looping the punch too wide
  • Dropping rear hand
  • Over-rotating balance
  • Standing too close to throw correctly

Drill Ideas

  • Lead hook pad rounds
  • Hook and angle drills
  • One-two-hook combinations

Progression

  • Connect to Rear Hook
  • Connect to Basic Combo
