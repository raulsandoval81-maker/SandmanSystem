Skill 11 — Lead Kick

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Deliver a controlled lead kick with speed and balance

B — Maintain defensive posture during the strike

C — Recover quickly into stance after contact

Coaching Cues

  • “Fast and balanced.”
  • “Hands stay active.”
  • “Kick and recover.”
  • “Don’t fall in.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping hands during the kick
  • Leaning backward excessively
  • Failing to reset stance
  • Standing square after impact

Drill Ideas

  • Lead kick repetition rounds
  • Lead kick touch drills
  • Lead kick and exit movement rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Rear Kick
  • Connect to Basic Combo
