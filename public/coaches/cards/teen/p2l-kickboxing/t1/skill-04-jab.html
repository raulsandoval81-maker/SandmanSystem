Skill 04 — Jab

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Use the jab to manage distance and create offense

B — Maintain balance and defense during jab exchanges

C — Recover quickly into stance and guard after extension

Coaching Cues

  • “Fast out, fast back.”
  • “Own the distance.”
  • “Touch and move.”
  • “Jab sets everything up.”

Common Mistakes

  • Reaching with the chin
  • Dropping rear hand
  • Standing still after jabbing
  • Pushing the punch

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-only spar rounds
  • Jab and angle drills
  • Pad jab timing rounds

Progression

  • Connect to One Two
  • Connect to Distance
