Skill 14 — Check

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Defend kicks with balanced leg checks

B — Maintain guard and posture during defensive reactions

C — Reset quickly into offense or movement after defense

Coaching Cues

  • “Knee up fast.”
  • “Hands stay disciplined.”
  • “Defend and recover.”
  • “Stay balanced.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping hands during checks
  • Turning sideways too far
  • Losing balance after defense
  • Checking too low

Drill Ideas

  • Kick-and-check rounds
  • Partner reaction drills
  • Defend-and-return combinations

Progression

  • Connect to Block
  • Connect to Controlled Exchanges
