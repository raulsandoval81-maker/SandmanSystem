Skill 12 — Rear Kick

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Generate controlled power through rear kick rotation

B — Maintain posture and balance through the strike

C — Recover stance immediately after impact

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn the hip through.”
  • “Hands stay disciplined.”
  • “Kick with balance.”
  • “Recover fast.”

Common Mistakes

  • Over-rotating balance
  • Dropping guard during kicks
  • Failing to pivot the base foot
  • Standing tall after impact

Drill Ideas

  • Rear kick pad rounds
  • Rear kick power drills
  • Kick-reset movement rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Teep
  • Connect to Basic Combo
