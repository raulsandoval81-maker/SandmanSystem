Skill 05 — Cross

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Deliver a balanced rear straight with hip rotation

B — Maintain posture and defensive awareness during extension

C — Recover immediately into stance and movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn the hip.”
  • “Straight line power.”
  • “Hands recover.”
  • “Don’t fall forward.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overreaching the punch
  • Dropping lead hand
  • Losing balance after impact
  • Arm punching without rotation

Drill Ideas

  • Cross mechanics rounds
  • Jab-cross pad drills
  • Cross and angle exits

Progression

  • Connect to One Two
  • Connect to Basic Combo
