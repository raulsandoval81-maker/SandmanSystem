Skill 19 — Partner Drill

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Work with control, timing, and discipline during partner exchanges

B — Apply offense and defense safely under movement and pressure

C — Maintain respect, focus, and composure through live drilling

Coaching Cues

  • “Protect your partner.”
  • “Control the pace.”
  • “Use technique, not emotion.”
  • “Stay disciplined.”

Common Mistakes

  • Escalating intensity unnecessarily
  • Ignoring spacing and control
  • Dropping defense during exchanges
  • Losing composure under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Controlled partner flow rounds
  • Touch-contact combo drills
  • Defense-and-return partner rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Kickboxing Standard
  • Connect to T2 Readiness
