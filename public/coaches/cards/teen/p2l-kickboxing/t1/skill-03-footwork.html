Skill 03 — Footwork

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T1

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Move efficiently while maintaining stance and balance

B — Enter and exit range under control

C — Support offense and defense through movement discipline

Coaching Cues

  • “Move your base.”
  • “Don’t cross your feet.”
  • “Balance before strikes.”
  • “Exit clean.”

Common Mistakes

  • Crossing feet during pressure
  • Standing flat-footed
  • Overreaching into range
  • Moving without guard

Drill Ideas

  • Movement ladder rounds
  • Angle-and-reset drills
  • Range management partner rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Angle
  • Connect to Distance
