Skill 04 — Counter

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize openings after defensive reactions

B — Return offense immediately with balance and control

C — Recover stance and defense after counter exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Defend then return.”
  • “Take the opening.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Counter with purpose.”

Common Mistakes

  • Countering too late
  • Overcommitting after defense
  • Dropping guard during counters
  • Standing still after exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Defense-counter partner rounds
  • Slip-return drills
  • Kick-check-counter rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Double Jab
  • Connect to Hook Kick Counter
