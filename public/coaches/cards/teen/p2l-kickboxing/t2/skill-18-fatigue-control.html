Skill 18 — Fatigue Control

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain technique and composure while tired

B — Control breathing, movement, and posture under fatigue

C — Finish rounds with discipline and structure intact

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay technical when tired.”
  • “Breathe and move.”
  • “Don’t break structure.”
  • “Finish composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping hands under fatigue
  • Throwing sloppy combinations
  • Standing flat-footed
  • Panicking during exhaustion

Drill Ideas

  • Conditioning sparring rounds
  • Late-round pressure drills
  • Technique-under-fatigue work

Progression

  • Connect to Round Management
  • Connect to Live Application
