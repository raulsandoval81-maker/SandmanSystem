Skill 24 — Pressure Sparring

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Perform under sustained pressure and pace

B — Maintain structure, defense, and composure during live exchanges

C — Apply tactical awareness while tired and pressured

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay composed under fire.”
  • “Trust your structure.”
  • “Think while moving.”
  • “Pressure without panic.”

Common Mistakes

  • Breaking stance during pressure
  • Throwing emotional combinations
  • Forgetting defense when tired
  • Backing straight up repeatedly

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure sparring rounds
  • Corner-pressure drills
  • High-pace reaction exchanges

Progression

  • Connect to Ring Awareness
  • Connect to T3 Readiness
