Skill 17 — Controlled Sparring

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply T2 kickboxing skills under live pressure

B — Maintain composure, structure, and awareness during exchanges

C — Respect safety, control, and partner discipline

Coaching Cues

  • “Think while fighting.”
  • “Control before power.”
  • “Stay composed.”
  • “Use your skills.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking during exchanges
  • Throwing wild combinations
  • Forgetting defense under pressure
  • Losing posture when tired
