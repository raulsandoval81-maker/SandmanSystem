Skill 01 — Timing

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize openings and strike at the correct moment

B — Blend movement, rhythm, and reactions together

C — Maintain balance and composure during exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t rush the opening.”
  • “See before you strike.”
  • “Timing beats speed.”
  • “Stay patient.”

Common Mistakes

  • Forcing attacks too early
  • Throwing without reading movement
  • Overcommitting balance
  • Panicking under exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Reaction sparring rounds
  • Coach-call timing drills
  • Touch-counter exchanges

Progression

  • Connect to Rhythm
  • Connect to Counter
