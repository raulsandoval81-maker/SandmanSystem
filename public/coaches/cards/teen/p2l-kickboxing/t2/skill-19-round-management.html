Skill 19 — Round Management

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control pace, energy, and positioning throughout rounds

B — Make tactical adjustments during live exchanges

C — Maintain composure under pressure and fatigue

Coaching Cues

  • “Control the round.”
  • “Don’t waste energy.”
  • “Think while fighting.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Starting too fast
  • Forcing exchanges unnecessarily
  • Losing tactical awareness
  • Panicking under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Tactical sparring rounds
  • Pace-control drills
  • Situational management work

Progression

  • Connect to Live Application
  • Connect to Pressure Sparring
