Skill 07 — Cross Kick Entry

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Use rear hand offense to set up kick entries

B — Maintain stance and defensive awareness through transitions

C — Recover balance after striking sequences

Coaching Cues

  • “Cross opens the kick.”
  • “Flow through the combo.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Recover after impact.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overcommitting on the cross
  • Throwing kicks too late
  • Dropping guard during transitions
  • Losing stance after combinations

Drill Ideas

  • Cross-kick pad drills
  • Combination transition rounds
  • Kick entry movement drills

Progression

  • Connect to Hook Kick Counter
  • Connect to Combination Flow
