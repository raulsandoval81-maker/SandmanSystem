Skill 02 — Rhythm

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control pace and movement through rhythm changes

B — Blend offense and defense fluidly during exchanges

C — Maintain composure under live movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Control the pace.”
  • “Change the rhythm.”
  • “Flow, don’t rush.”
  • “Stay relaxed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Moving at one speed constantly
  • Rushing combinations
  • Freezing after exchanges
  • Burning energy too early

Drill Ideas

  • Pace-change sparring
  • Slow-fast combination rounds
  • Rhythm disruption drills

Progression

  • Connect to Feint
  • Connect to Timing
