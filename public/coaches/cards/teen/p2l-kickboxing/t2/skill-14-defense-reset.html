Skill 15 — Combination Flow

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Blend punches, kicks, counters, and movement fluidly

B — Maintain balance and structure through exchanges

C — Recover immediately into movement after combinations

Coaching Cues

  • “Everything connects.”
  • “No pauses.”
  • “Move after combos.”
  • “Flow under pressure.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after combinations
  • Throwing disconnected strikes
  • Losing stance during transitions
  • Standing square after attacks

Drill Ideas

  • Continuous flow rounds
  • Punch-kick-counter pad drills
  • Movement combination sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Ring Position
  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
