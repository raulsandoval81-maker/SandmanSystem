Skill 08 — Hook Kick Counter

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Counter with hook-to-kick transitions under pressure

B — Blend defensive reactions into offense fluidly

C — Maintain composure and balance during counters

Coaching Cues

  • “Counter immediately.”
  • “Flow from hands to kicks.”
  • “Stay sharp.”
  • “Balance after impact.”

Common Mistakes

  • Pausing between strikes
  • Dropping hands during transitions
  • Over-rotating balance
  • Forcing counters too wide

Drill Ideas

  • Counter combination rounds
  • Hook-kick pad drills
  • Defense-return partner work

Progression

  • Connect to Body Head Kick Combo
  • Connect to Live Application
