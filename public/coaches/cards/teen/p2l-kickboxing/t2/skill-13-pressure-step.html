Skill 13 — Pressure Step

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Step forward with control to apply pressure

B — Keep stance, guard, and balance while advancing

C — Pressure without rushing, reaching, or losing structure

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure with balance.”
  • “Step, don’t chase.”
  • “Guard while advancing.”
  • “Cut space smart.”

Common Mistakes

  • Running into range
  • Reaching instead of stepping
  • Dropping guard while pressuring
  • Standing square after entry

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure step shadow rounds
  • Partner space-cutting drills
  • Pressure-entry pad rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Defense Reset
  • Connect to Pressure Sparring
