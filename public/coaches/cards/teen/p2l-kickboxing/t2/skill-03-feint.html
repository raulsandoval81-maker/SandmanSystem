Skill 03 — Feint

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create reactions using believable movement and setup

B — Blend feints naturally into offense and entries

C — Stay balanced and ready to capitalize on reactions

Coaching Cues

  • “Sell the feint.”
  • “Draw the reaction.”
  • “Don’t overdo it.”
  • “Attack the opening.”

Common Mistakes

  • Fake movements with no purpose
  • Overcommitting balance
  • Feinting too large
  • Not following the reaction

Drill Ideas

  • Feint-entry drills
  • Reaction sparring
  • Partner response rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Counter
  • Connect to Double Jab
