Skill 12 — Exit Angle

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Exit exchanges by moving off the center line

B — Maintain stance, guard, and balance while leaving range

C — Reset into position for defense or counter offense

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t back straight up.”
  • “Exit on an angle.”
  • “Move with your guard.”
  • “Reset ready.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing straight out of exchanges
  • Crossing feet while exiting
  • Dropping hands during movement
  • Stopping after the angle

Drill Ideas

  • Combo-and-exit rounds
  • Partner pressure exit drills
  • Angle reset movement rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Pressure Step
  • Connect to Ring Position
