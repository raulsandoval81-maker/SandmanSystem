Skill 09 — Body Head Kick Combo

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Blend level changes into fluid combination offense

B — Maintain stance and posture through transitions

C — Recover balance and defense after exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Change levels smoothly.”
  • “Flow through the combo.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Finish controlled.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing tall between strikes
  • Pausing during transitions
  • Dropping defense while attacking
  • Overcommitting balance

Drill Ideas

  • Body-head-kick pad rounds
  • Level-change combination drills
  • Partner flow combinations

Progression

  • Connect to Combination Flow
  • Connect to Live Application
