Skill 05 — Double Jab

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Use repeated jabs to pressure and create reactions

B — Maintain balance and defensive structure while advancing

C — Flow into follow-up offense after establishing range

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the space.”
  • “Double with purpose.”
  • “Stay behind the jab.”
  • “Flow into offense.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing both jabs identically
  • Standing tall while advancing
  • Dropping rear hand
  • Forcing the second jab

Drill Ideas

  • Double jab movement rounds
  • Pressure-entry drills
  • Double jab into combo work

Progression

  • Connect to Jab Kick Entry
  • Connect to Range Control
