Skill 25 — Ring Awareness

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain awareness of space, pressure, and positioning

B — Adjust movement and tactics based on ring position

C — Stay composed and disciplined during live movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Know where you are.”
  • “Use the space.”
  • “Don’t get trapped.”
  • “Control your position.”

Common Mistakes

  • Ignoring ring position during exchanges
  • Backing into corners repeatedly
  • Standing still under pressure
  • Losing tactical awareness

Drill Ideas

  • Ring-control sparring
  • Boundary movement drills
  • Pressure-and-angle rounds

Progression

  • Connect to T3 Kickboxing Readiness
  • Connect to Advanced Live Strategy
