Skill 06 — Jab Kick Entry

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Blend punches into kick entries fluidly

B — Maintain balance and defensive posture during transitions

C — Recover stance after combinations and exits

Coaching Cues

  • “Hands open the kick.”
  • “Flow between levels.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Finish clean.”

Common Mistakes

  • Rushing into kicks
  • Dropping hands during transitions
  • Losing stance after impact
  • Standing square after entries

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-kick pad rounds
  • Punch-to-kick shadow drills
  • Kick entry partner work

Progression

  • Connect to Cross Kick Entry
  • Connect to Combination Flow
