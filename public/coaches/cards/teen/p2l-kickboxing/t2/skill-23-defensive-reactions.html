Skill 23 — Defensive Reactions

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Defend naturally under pressure and live exchanges

B — Blend movement, guard, checks, and counters together

C — Recover immediately into balanced position

Coaching Cues

  • “React without panic.”
  • “Stay composed.”
  • “Defend and move.”
  • “Recover fast.”

Common Mistakes

  • Freezing during pressure
  • Closing eyes during exchanges
  • Dropping guard while reacting
  • Standing square after defense

Drill Ideas

  • Reaction defense rounds
  • Pressure-defense partner drills
  • Defend-and-return sparring

Progression

  • Connect to Pressure Sparring
  • Connect to Ring Awareness
