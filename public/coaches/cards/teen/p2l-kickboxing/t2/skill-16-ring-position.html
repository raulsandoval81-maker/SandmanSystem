Skill 16 — Ring Position

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Understand center control and ring awareness

B — Use movement and pressure to control space

C — Avoid getting trapped or cornered during exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the center.”
  • “Don’t get trapped.”
  • “Cut angles smart.”
  • “Control the space.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing straight to the edge
  • Standing still under pressure
  • Ignoring ring awareness
  • Panicking near boundaries

Drill Ideas

  • Ring-control sparring
  • Corner escape drills
  • Pressure-and-angle rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Controlled Sparring
  • Connect to Round Management
