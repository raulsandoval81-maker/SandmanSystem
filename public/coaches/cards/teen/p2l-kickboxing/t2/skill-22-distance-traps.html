Skill 22 — Distance Traps

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Draw reactions through distance and positioning

B — Create openings using pressure and timing

C — Stay balanced and ready to counter immediately

Coaching Cues

  • “Set the trap.”
  • “Control the space.”
  • “Make them react.”
  • “Stay patient.”

Common Mistakes

  • Forcing traps too early
  • Standing still while baiting
  • Overcommitting entries
  • Ignoring defensive posture

Drill Ideas

  • Trap-and-counter drills
  • Distance reaction sparring
  • Pressure bait rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Defensive Reactions
  • Connect to Pressure Sparring
