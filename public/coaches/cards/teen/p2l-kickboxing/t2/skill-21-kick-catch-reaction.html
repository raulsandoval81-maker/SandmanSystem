Skill 21 — Kick Catch Reaction

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — React to kicks with controlled catches and balance

B — Maintain posture and defensive awareness during reactions

C — Flow immediately into resets, counters, or movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Catch with control.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Don’t reach.”
  • “React immediately.”

Common Mistakes

  • Reaching too far for catches
  • Losing stance during reactions
  • Holding the catch too long
  • Dropping guard during defense

Drill Ideas

  • Kick catch partner drills
  • Catch-and-counter rounds
  • Reaction timing work

Progression

  • Connect to Distance Traps
  • Connect to Defensive Reactions
