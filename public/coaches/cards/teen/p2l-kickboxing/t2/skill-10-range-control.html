Skill 10 — Range Control

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control striking distance through movement and pressure

B — Blend offense and defense while managing range

C — Maintain composure during live exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the range.”
  • “Don’t give space away.”
  • “Pressure with balance.”
  • “Stay disciplined.”

Common Mistakes

  • Backing straight up
  • Standing too close unnecessarily
  • Overreaching attacks
  • Losing stance under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Range-control sparring
  • Pressure-and-exit drills
  • Distance management rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Pivot
  • Connect to Ring Position
