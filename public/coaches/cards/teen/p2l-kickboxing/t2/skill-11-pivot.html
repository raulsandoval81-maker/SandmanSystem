Skill 11 — Pivot

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T2

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Create angles and escape pressure through pivots

B — Maintain balance and stance during directional movement

C — Recover immediately into offense or defense after movement

Coaching Cues

  • “Turn off line.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Don’t cross your feet.”
  • “Move with purpose.”

Common Mistakes

  • Crossing feet while turning
  • Losing stance after movement
  • Turning without awareness
  • Stopping after the pivot

Drill Ideas

  • Pivot-and-counter rounds
  • Pressure escape drills
  • Angle movement partner work

Progression

  • Connect to Ring Position
  • Connect to Defensive Reactions
