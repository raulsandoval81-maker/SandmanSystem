Skill 07 — Inside Dominance

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control close-range exchanges with composure and pressure

B — Blend offense, defense, posture, and reactions inside the pocket

C — Maintain tactical awareness during heavy pressure exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Own the inside.”
  • “Stay compact.”
  • “Protect while attacking.”
  • “Control the exchange.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing wide strikes inside
  • Standing too tall under pressure
  • Ignoring defensive positioning
  • Panicking in exchanges

Drill Ideas

  • Inside sparring rounds
  • Pressure pocket exchanges
  • Short-range reaction drills

Progression

  • Connect to Chain Pressure
  • Connect to Jab System
