Skill 17 — Live Chaos

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Perform technically during unpredictable live pressure

B — Blend reactions, movement, and composure during chaos

C — Maintain tactical thinking while exhausted or pressured

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay composed in chaos.”
  • “Trust your training.”
  • “Keep structure under pressure.”
  • “Think while fighting.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking under pressure
  • Throwing emotional attacks
  • Breaking posture during exchanges
  • Losing awareness during fatigue

Drill Ideas

  • Chaos sparring rounds
  • Reaction pressure drills
  • Random exchange scenarios

Progression

  • Connect to Strategy Build
  • Connect to Opponent Reading
