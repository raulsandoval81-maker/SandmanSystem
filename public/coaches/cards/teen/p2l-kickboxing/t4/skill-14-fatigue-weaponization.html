Skill 14 — Fatigue Weaponization

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Continue applying pressure effectively while fatigued

B — Use conditioning and composure as tactical advantages

C — Maintain structure and awareness during exhaustion

Coaching Cues

  • “Weaponize your conditioning.”
  • “Stay technical while tired.”
  • “Pressure through fatigue.”
  • “Break them mentally.”

Common Mistakes

  • Breaking posture under fatigue
  • Throwing emotional attacks
  • Stopping movement when tired
  • Losing tactical awareness

Drill Ideas

  • Fatigue sparring rounds
  • Pressure conditioning drills
  • Late-round tactical work

Progression

  • Connect to Mental Pressure
  • Connect to Round Stealing
