Skill 03 — Pressure Control

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control pressure exchanges without emotional reactions

B — Blend pressure, movement, and tactical awareness together

C — Maintain composure while dictating pace and positioning

Coaching Cues

  • “Pressure with intelligence.”
  • “Stay composed under fire.”
  • “Own the pace.”
  • “Control the exchange.”

Common Mistakes

  • Rushing emotionally
  • Breaking structure during pressure
  • Ignoring tactical openings
  • Standing still while attacking

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure sparring
  • Pace-control rounds
  • Pressure reaction drills

Progression

  • Connect to Range Dictation
  • Connect to Counter Pressure
