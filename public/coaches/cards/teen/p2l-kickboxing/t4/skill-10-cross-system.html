Skill 10 — Cross System

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build tactical offense through advanced rear-hand systems

B — Blend setups, counters, and pressure into cross attacks

C — Maintain balance and recovery during live exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Build the shot.”
  • “Drive through clean.”
  • “Recover immediately.”
  • “Stay balanced.”

Common Mistakes

  • Forcing the rear hand emotionally
  • Dropping the lead hand
  • Overcommitting weight forward
  • Standing still after attacks

Drill Ideas

  • Cross-counter sparring
  • Pressure-cross drills
  • Setup-to-cross pad rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Hook System
  • Connect to Kick System
