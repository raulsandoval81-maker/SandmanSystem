Skill 29 — Fight Management

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Manage pace, pressure, positioning, and momentum strategically

B — Blend tactical thinking into live exchanges naturally

C — Maintain composure and awareness throughout the fight

Coaching Cues

  • “Control the fight.”
  • “Manage the pace.”
  • “Think while fighting.”
  • “Stay disciplined.”

Common Mistakes

  • Fighting emotionally
  • Ignoring momentum shifts
  • Overcommitting attacks
  • Losing tactical patience

Drill Ideas

  • Fight-management sparring
  • Tactical pressure rounds
  • Momentum-control drills

Progression

  • Connect to System Mastery
  • Connect to Championship Performance
