Skill 15 — Mental Pressure

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply psychological pressure during live exchanges

B — Blend pace, posture, and pressure into mental control

C — Maintain composure while forcing opponent reactions

Coaching Cues

  • “Control the mind.”
  • “Stay composed under pressure.”
  • “Force mistakes.”
  • “Own the pace.”

Common Mistakes

  • Showing frustration emotionally
  • Rushing attacks recklessly
  • Losing posture during pressure
  • Ignoring tactical patience

Drill Ideas

  • Mental-pressure sparring
  • Pace-control rounds
  • Pressure reaction exchanges

Progression

  • Connect to Round Stealing
  • Connect to Live Chaos
