Skill 12 — Kick System

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build layered kicking systems through setups and reactions

B — Blend kicks naturally into combinations and pressure

C — Maintain balance, posture, and recovery during exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Kick with structure.”
  • “Build the opening.”
  • “Recover immediately.”
  • “Flow through the exchange.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing naked kicks
  • Dropping hands during kicks
  • Losing balance after impact
  • Standing square during recovery

Drill Ideas

  • Kick-chain sparring
  • Kick setup drills
  • Pressure kicking rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Defense To Offense
  • Connect to Fatigue Weaponization
