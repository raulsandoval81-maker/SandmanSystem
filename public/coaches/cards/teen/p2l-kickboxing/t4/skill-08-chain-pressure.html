Skill 08 — Chain Pressure

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply continuous layered pressure during live exchanges

B — Blend offense, movement, and tactical reactions together

C — Maintain composure while forcing mistakes and reactions

Coaching Cues

  • “Keep the chain alive.”
  • “Pressure without rushing.”
  • “Flow from attack to attack.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after combinations
  • Forcing pressure emotionally
  • Ignoring defensive recovery
  • Breaking balance while advancing

Drill Ideas

  • Continuous pressure sparring
  • Flow-combination rounds
  • Layered attack drills

Progression

  • Connect to Jab System
  • Connect to Cross System
