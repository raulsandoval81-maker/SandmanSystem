Skill 22 — Pressure Layering

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Apply layered pressure through movement, pace, and reactions

B — Blend offense and positioning into constant tactical pressure

C — Maintain composure and balance during long exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Layer the pressure.”
  • “Never stop building.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Pressure with purpose.”

Common Mistakes

  • Pressuring recklessly
  • Standing square while attacking
  • Ignoring defensive positioning
  • Breaking posture under fatigue

Drill Ideas

  • Layered pressure sparring
  • Continuous attack rounds
  • Pressure-flow drills

Progression

  • Connect to Weapon Blending
  • Connect to Chain Finishing
