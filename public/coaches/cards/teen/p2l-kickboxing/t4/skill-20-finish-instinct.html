Skill 20 — Finish Instinct

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize finishing opportunities during live exchanges

B — Blend pressure, timing, and tactical aggression intelligently

C — Maintain composure while attacking under pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Recognize the opening.”
  • “Pressure with control.”
  • “Stay composed while finishing.”
  • “Don’t rush emotionally.”

Common Mistakes

  • Overcommitting emotionally
  • Ignoring defensive recovery
  • Throwing reckless combinations
  • Losing tactical awareness

Drill Ideas

  • Finish-pressure rounds
  • Momentum sparring drills
  • Controlled finishing exchanges

Progression

  • Connect to Fight Breaking
  • Connect to Pressure Layering
