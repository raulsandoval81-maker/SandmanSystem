Skill 01 — Kickboxing IQ

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Read patterns, reactions, and openings during live exchanges

B — Blend tactical thinking into offense, defense, and movement

C — Adapt intelligently under pressure and fatigue

Coaching Cues

  • “See the fight clearly.”
  • “Think while moving.”
  • “Recognize patterns.”
  • “Fight intelligently.”

Common Mistakes

  • Fighting emotionally
  • Ignoring tactical openings
  • Repeating predictable attacks
  • Losing awareness under pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Tactical sparring rounds
  • Pattern-reading drills
  • Adaptive reaction exchanges

Progression

  • Connect to Adaptation
  • Connect to Pressure Control
