Skill 16 — Round Stealing

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Shift momentum late in rounds through pressure and control

B — Blend pace, output, and tactical timing intelligently

C — Maintain composure during critical exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Finish the round strong.”
  • “Take momentum late.”
  • “Stay tactical.”
  • “Pressure intelligently.”

Common Mistakes

  • Burning energy too early
  • Forcing exchanges emotionally
  • Losing composure late
  • Ignoring tactical timing

Drill Ideas

  • Late-round sparring
  • Momentum shift drills
  • Pressure finish rounds
