Skill 18 — Strategy Build

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build tactical game plans based on reactions and tendencies

B — Blend setups, pace, pressure, and positioning strategically

C — Maintain composure while adapting during exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Build the strategy.”
  • “Read before reacting.”
  • “Adapt intelligently.”
  • “Fight with purpose.”

Common Mistakes

  • Attacking without planning
  • Ignoring opponent patterns
  • Forcing emotional exchanges
  • Losing tactical awareness

Drill Ideas

  • Strategy sparring rounds
  • Pattern-reading drills
  • Adaptive tactical exchanges

Progression

  • Connect to Opponent Reading
  • Connect to Finish Instinct
