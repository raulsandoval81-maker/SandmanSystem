Skill 04 — Range Dictation

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Control distance and positioning during live exchanges

B — Blend movement, pressure, and timing into range control

C — Force opponents into uncomfortable positions and reactions

Coaching Cues

  • “Control the distance.”
  • “Own the range.”
  • “Make them react.”
  • “Fight from your position.”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing at bad range
  • Backing straight up
  • Allowing free entries
  • Ignoring positioning

Drill Ideas

  • Range-control sparring
  • Distance reaction drills
  • Pressure-and-angle rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Counter Pressure
  • Connect to Corner Traps
