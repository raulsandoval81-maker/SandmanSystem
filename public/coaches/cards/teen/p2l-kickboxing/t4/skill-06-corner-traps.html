Skill 06 — Corner Traps

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Trap opponents near ropes or corners strategically

B — Blend pressure, angles, and tactical positioning together

C — Maintain composure and balance while attacking trapped opponents

Coaching Cues

  • “Cut the exits.”
  • “Trap with control.”
  • “Stay balanced.”
  • “Pressure intelligently.”

Common Mistakes

  • Rushing into corners emotionally
  • Leaving escape lanes open
  • Standing square during pressure
  • Overcommitting combinations

Drill Ideas

  • Corner trap sparring
  • Pressure-and-cutoff drills
  • Boundary control rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Inside Dominance
  • Connect to Chain Pressure
