Skill 19 — Opponent Reading

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Recognize tendencies, habits, and openings during exchanges

B — Blend tactical observation into reactions and counters

C — Maintain composure while processing live information

Coaching Cues

  • “Read the reactions.”
  • “See the habits.”
  • “Fight intelligently.”
  • “Adapt live.”

Common Mistakes

  • Ignoring repeated patterns
  • Fighting emotionally
  • Missing tactical openings
  • Overcommitting attacks blindly

Drill Ideas

  • Reaction observation drills
  • Tactical exchange rounds

Progression
