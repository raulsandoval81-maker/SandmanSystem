Skill 09 — Jab System

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build advanced offensive systems through the jab

B — Blend feints, pressure, timing, and reactions together

C — Maintain defensive awareness while controlling exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “The jab controls the fight.”
  • “Build reactions.”
  • “Pressure behind the jab.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing lazy jabs
  • Standing still after extension
  • Ignoring setups
  • Overreaching range

Drill Ideas

  • Jab-pressure sparring
  • Feint-jab drills
  • Advanced jab pad rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Cross System
  • Connect to Hook System
