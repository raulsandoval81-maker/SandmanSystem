Skill 11 — Hook System

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Build layered offensive systems around hook attacks

B — Blend hooks into pressure, counters, and angle changes

C — Maintain balance and defensive posture during exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Hook with structure.”
  • “Turn with balance.”
  • “Flow into angles.”
  • “Recover immediately.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing wide hooks
  • Over-rotating posture
  • Dropping the opposite hand
  • Standing still after attacks

Drill Ideas

  • Hook-chain sparring
  • Pressure hook drills
  • Angle-hook pad rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Kick System
  • Connect to Defense To Offense
