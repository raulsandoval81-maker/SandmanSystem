Skill 26 — Mental Fatigue

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain tactical thinking while mentally exhausted

B — Blend composure, breathing, and structure under fatigue

C — Continue making intelligent decisions during pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay calm while tired.”
  • “Think under pressure.”
  • “Keep your structure.”
  • “Stay disciplined.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking mentally under fatigue
  • Making emotional decisions
  • Breaking posture while tired
  • Losing tactical awareness

Drill Ideas

  • Fatigue sparring
  • Pressure conditioning rounds
  • Decision-making drills under stress

Progression

  • Connect to High Pressure Rounds
  • Connect to Chaos Composure
