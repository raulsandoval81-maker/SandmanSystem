Skill 21 — Fight Breaking

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Break opponent structure, rhythm, and confidence under pressure

B — Blend pace, pressure, and tactical attacks together

C — Maintain composure while forcing mental and physical mistakes

Coaching Cues

  • “Break their rhythm.”
  • “Pressure intelligently.”
  • “Stay composed.”
  • “Force mistakes.”

Common Mistakes

  • Rushing emotionally
  • Overcommitting offense
  • Ignoring defensive recovery
  • Losing patience during pressure

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure-breaking sparring
  • Momentum-control rounds
  • Mental fatigue exchanges

Progression

  • Connect to Pressure Layering
  • Connect to Weapon Blending
