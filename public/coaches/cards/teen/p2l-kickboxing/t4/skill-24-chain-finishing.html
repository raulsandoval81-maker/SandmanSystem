Skill 24 — Chain Finishing

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Continue attacks through reactions and defensive responses

B — Blend pressure, timing, and tactical finishing together

C — Maintain composure during aggressive exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay on the chain.”
  • “Flow into the next opening.”
  • “Pressure with control.”
  • “Finish intelligently.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after the first attack
  • Overcommitting emotionally
  • Ignoring defensive recovery
  • Throwing reckless combinations

Drill Ideas

  • Chain attack sparring
  • Pressure-finishing rounds
  • Reaction continuation drills

Progression

  • Connect to Corner Dictation
  • Connect to Mental Fatigue
