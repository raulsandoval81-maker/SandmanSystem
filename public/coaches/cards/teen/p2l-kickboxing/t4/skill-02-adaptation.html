Skill 02 — Adaptation

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Adjust tactics and reactions during live exchanges

B — Blend movement, counters, and pressure into adaptive systems

C — Maintain composure while solving problems under pressure

Coaching Cues

  • “Adjust live.”
  • “Read and respond.”
  • “Don’t stay predictable.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Repeating failed attacks
  • Ignoring opponent adjustments
  • Panicking during pressure
  • Forcing one strategy only

Drill Ideas

  • Adaptive sparring rounds
  • Problem-solving exchanges
  • Reaction adjustment drills

Progression

  • Connect to Pressure Control
  • Connect to Range Dictation
