Skill 05 — Counter Pressure

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Counter effectively while under forward pressure

B — Blend defense, movement, and reactions into counter systems

C — Maintain composure and tactical awareness during exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Counter the pressure.”
  • “Stay calm under attack.”
  • “Punish bad entries.”
  • “Defend and return.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking during pressure
  • Throwing blind counters
  • Standing square while reacting
  • Breaking posture under attacks

Drill Ideas

  • Pressure-counter sparring
  • Defend-and-return drills
  • Reaction counter exchanges

Progression

  • Connect to Corner Traps
  • Connect to Inside Dominance
