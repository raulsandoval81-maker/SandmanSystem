Skill 28 — Chaos Composure

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Maintain calm and tactical awareness during chaotic exchanges

B — Blend reactions, movement, and discipline under pressure

C — Continue making intelligent decisions during fatigue and chaos

Coaching Cues

  • “Stay calm in chaos.”
  • “Trust your training.”
  • “Keep your structure.”
  • “Fight intelligently.”

Common Mistakes

  • Panicking during exchanges
  • Breaking posture emotionally
  • Throwing reckless attacks
  • Losing awareness under fatigue

Drill Ideas

  • Chaos sparring
  • Pressure adaptation drills
  • Reaction stress rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Fight Management
  • Connect to System Mastery
