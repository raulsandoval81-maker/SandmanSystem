Skill 13 — Defense To Offense

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Transition instantly from defense into offense

B — Blend reactions, counters, and movement fluidly

C — Maintain composure and tactical awareness during exchanges

Coaching Cues

  • “Defend and return.”
  • “Counter immediately.”
  • “Flow through reactions.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Stopping after defense
  • Countering emotionally
  • Breaking posture during reactions
  • Forgetting movement after counters

Drill Ideas

  • Defend-counter sparring
  • Reaction return drills
  • Counter-flow exchanges

Progression

  • Connect to Fatigue Weaponization
  • Connect to Mental Pressure
