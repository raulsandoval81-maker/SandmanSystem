Skill 23 — Weapon Blending

Journey: Teen P2L — Kickboxing

Tier: T4

Category: Technique

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Blend punches, kicks, knees, and movement seamlessly

B — Build tactical combinations through layered weapon systems

C — Maintain balance and composure during transitions

Coaching Cues

  • “Everything connects.”
  • “Blend the weapons.”
  • “Flow through transitions.”
  • “Stay composed.”

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing predictable attacks
  • Separating strikes unnaturally
  • Breaking posture during transitions
  • Forcing combinations emotionally

Drill Ideas

  • Mixed weapon sparring
  • Combination flow drills
  • Transition pressure rounds

Progression

  • Connect to Chain Finishing
  • Connect to Corner Dictation
