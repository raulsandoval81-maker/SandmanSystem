Skill — Motion Control

Tree: Stance & Motion

Tier: Teen T0 — Apprentice

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Forward / backward

B — Circle left/right

C — Stay in stance

Coaching Cues

  • “Move your feet, not your body”
  • “Stay low”
  • “Don’t click your heels”

Common Mistakes

  • Standing up while moving
  • Crossing feet
  • Moving too big

Drill Ideas

  • Circle drill
  • Shadow wrestling
  • Partner mirror drill

Progression

  • Add level changes
