Skill — Single Leg (Control)

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Teen T0 — Apprentice

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Entry

B — Control

C — Finish

Coaching Cues

  • “Head in the right place”
  • “Lock tight before trying to finish”
  • “Keep posture, don’t hang”

Common Mistakes

  • Loose grip on the leg
  • Bent over with no posture
  • Trying to finish too early

Drill Ideas

  • Entry to hold drill
  • Single-leg balance drill
  • Finish reps from secured single

Progression

  • Add run-the-pipe finish
  • Add re-shot entry
