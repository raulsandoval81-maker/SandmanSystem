Skill — Snap to Go Behind

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Teen T0 — Apprentice

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Snap

B — Control

C — Score

Coaching Cues

  • “Snap with purpose”
  • “Move your feet with the hands”
  • “Get behind, don’t stay in front”

Common Mistakes

  • Snapping straight down and freezing
  • Not circling feet
  • Letting go before getting behind

Drill Ideas

  • Snap and circle drill
  • Front head position reps
  • Go-behind finish drill

Progression

  • Add front headlock control
  • Add snap to spin under live resistance
