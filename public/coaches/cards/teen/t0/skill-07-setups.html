Skill — Setups

Tree: Neutral Offense

Tier: Teen T0 — Apprentice

Structure (A/B/C)

A — Wrist tie / contact

B — Move opponent

C — Attack

Coaching Cues

  • “Don’t shoot cold”
  • “Move him before you go”
  • “Create the lane, then attack”

Common Mistakes

  • Shooting with no setup
  • Touching tie but not moving opponent
  • Attacking too late after reaction

Drill Ideas

  • Wrist tie to shot drill
  • Pop-and-go reps
  • Snap to attack drill

Progression

  • Add multiple setups to same attack
  • Add setup to re-shot chain
