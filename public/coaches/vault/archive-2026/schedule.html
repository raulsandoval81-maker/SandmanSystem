Daily Practice Schedule Horario Diario de Práctica

  • Practice runs Monday–Friday from 3:45 PM to 5:45 PM.
  • Athletes must arrive 15 minutes early.
  • Practices are closed to parents and spectators.
  • Las prácticas son de lunes a viernes de 3:45 PM a 5:45 PM.
  • Los atletas deben llegar 15 minutos antes.
