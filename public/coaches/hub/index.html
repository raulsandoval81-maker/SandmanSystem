Values first. Tools second. This is the compass for how we coach every day.

COACH CREED — TO MYSELF

I will not compromise the standards I expect in this room. I understand that what I allow becomes the environment my athletes grow in. I lead with strength and honor, and I hold the line even when it’s difficult, because my athletes deserve something real to follow.

COACH CODE — TO THE ROOM

We are brothers in combat. We hold strength and honor high. Friend or foe, we show respect. We hold each other accountable and do not turn on each other under pressure. We are on the same side, and we protect the integrity of the room.

COACH GOAL — THE MOMENT

We coach the athlete in front of us. Every rep, every correction, and every decision matters. We do not waste the moment—we use it to build discipline, focus, and ownership. This is where growth happens.

COACH MISSION — THE FUTURE

We are shaping more than athletes. What we build here becomes discipline, resilience, and character they carry into life beyond the mat. Success is not just wins—it is en quiénes se convierten porque los entrenamos de la manera correcta.
