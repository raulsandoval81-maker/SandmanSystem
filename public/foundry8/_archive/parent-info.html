Foundry 8 Progression

Foundry 8 is the youth progression track of the Sandman System. It is built for foundation, discipline, control, and earned identity.

The goal is simple: build young athletes step by step through wrestling-first development. Control comes first. Complexity comes later.

Progression Identity

Badge

The badge marks where the athlete has been. It is history, progress, and earned proof.

Shirt

The shirt marks who the athlete is now. It carries current identity inside the room.

Belt

The belt marks a milestone. It is earned through testing, standards, and readiness.

Ceremony

Ceremony recognizes the transition. Promotion is earned first. Ceremony honors it.

How Foundry 8 Moves

1. Train
Athletes build stance, motion, position, pressure, and body control.
2. Earn XP
Progress is earned through practice, effort, consistency, and standards.
3. Reach Testing
When the athlete reaches the standard, testing confirms readiness.
4. Promote
Passing does not cheapen the process. Promotion is a separate earned transition.
5. Continue
Each tier resets the mountain. The athlete begins again at a higher standard.

Core Rule

Foundry 8 is not a shortcut system. No skipping tiers. No freebies. No empty recognition. The work comes first.
