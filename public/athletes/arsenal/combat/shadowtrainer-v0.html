🥋 ShadowTrainer — Version 0

Micro Starter · Pre-Stripe

Purpose: Introduce movement, posture, balance, and fun. No fakes. No shot entries. No chains. Strictly body control and confidence.

🌱 Foundations

1) Stance Lite

  • Hands up
  • One-foot balance challenge
  • Soft knees

2) Movement Basics

  • Walk in stance
  • Shuffle forward/back
  • Shadow circles (big, slow)

3) Level Touch

  • Touch knee → stand
  • Touch shoelace → stand

4) Reaction Play

  • Coach clap → freeze
  • Coach stomp → circle left
  • Coach snap → circle right

💧 Session Format

  • 3 rounds
  • 30 seconds on / 30 seconds off
  • Goal = fun + discipline

⭐ Progress Markers

  • Holds basic stance
  • Moves without crossing feet
  • Responds to coach signals
  • Stays focused for full round
