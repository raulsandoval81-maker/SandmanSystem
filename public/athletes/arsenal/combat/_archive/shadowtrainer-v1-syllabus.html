Shadow Wrestler 1.0

Recruit Tier Syllabus

Recruit tier introduces the first structured Shadow Wrestler curriculum layer after Shadow 0.0.

🌱 Recruit Curriculum Areas

Stance & Motion Development

  • Stance hold with posture
  • Forward motion
  • Backward motion
  • Circle left and right
  • Reset without crossing feet

Level Change Development

  • Short level change
  • Controlled level change
  • Motion into level change
  • Recover stance after level change

Fake Mechanics

  • Static fake
  • Fake with step
  • Fake with level change
  • Simple setup rhythm

Down-Block Introduction

  • Light sprawl motion
  • Quick hands
  • Recover posture after reaction

⭐ Progress Markers

  • Maintains stance through the round
  • Moves without crossing feet
  • Level change without rising
  • Fakes begin to create reactions
  • Recovers posture after every action
  • Maintains focus through the round

🔒 What This Builds Toward

Recruit curriculum deepens the Shadow layer and prepares athletes for the next Shadow Wrestler standard.
