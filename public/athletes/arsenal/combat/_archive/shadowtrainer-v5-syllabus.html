Shadow Wrestler 4.0

Warrior Tier Syllabus

Warrior tier develops tempo control, chain offense, and extended pressure discipline for advanced competitors.

🌱 Warrior Curriculum Areas

Tempo Control

  • Pace change: slow → burst → recover
  • Angle creation through motion
  • Controlled resets inside movement

Chain Offense

  • Fake → entry → recover → re-attack
  • Shot → reaction → re-shot
  • Spin-behind → re-center

Defensive Reactions

  • Down-block → circle → re-engage
  • Sprawl → angle recovery
  • Defensive reaction into offense

Scramble Awareness

  • Hip-switch recovery
  • Spin-behind opportunities
  • Scramble stabilization

Extended Pressure Control

  • Maintain stance discipline under fatigue
  • Control breathing during longer rounds
  • Finish sequences without posture collapse

⭐ Progress Markers

  • Maintains movement quality for full 3:00 rounds
  • Executes multiple chain sequences smoothly
  • Shows visible pace control
  • Recovers posture quickly after reactions
  • Moves continuously without hesitation
