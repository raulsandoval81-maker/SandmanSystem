Shadow Wrestler 3.0

Competitor Tier Syllabus

Competitor tier develops competitive chain flow, pace switching, and positional awareness under pressure.

🌱 Competitor Curriculum Areas

Chain Flow Development

  • Level change into attack rhythm
  • Reaction → circle → recover
  • Attack into re-attack awareness
  • Chain movement without losing posture

Pace Switching

  • Technical pace control
  • Controlled competition pace
  • Explosive burst pacing
  • Recovery between pace changes

Positional Mastery

  • Stance and motion control
  • Down-block response
  • Sprawl recovery
  • Top pressure awareness
  • Finish angle awareness

Scramble Response

  • Stuffed shot → recovery
  • Spin-behind awareness
  • Re-attack angle recognition
  • Scramble reset without panic

Competitive Pressure

  • Move through longer rounds with control
  • Finish actions without collapsing posture
  • Recover inside pressure
  • Maintain discipline under fatigue

⭐ Progress Markers

  • Links reactions into chain flow
  • Maintains movement quality through two-minute rounds
  • Recovers posture quickly after scramble reactions
  • Controls pace under fatigue
  • Transitions between positions without hesitation

🔒 What This Builds Toward

Prepares athletes for Warrior-level pressure, positional control, and higher-level competitive flow.
