Shadow Wrestler 2.0

Combatant / Apprentice Tier Syllabus

This curriculum layer introduces entry awareness, penetration step control, and stronger fake → level change rhythm.

🌱 Curriculum Areas

Stance → Motion Flow

  • Crisp stance transitions
  • Motion with level change timing
  • Circle and re-center under control
  • Recovery to stance after movement

Entry Awareness

  • Penetration step introduction
  • Knee-over-toe entry awareness
  • Penetration and recovery
  • Entry posture under control

Fake → Level Change → Entry Rhythm

  • Short fake with step
  • Fake into level change
  • Fake into shot touch
  • Re-attack angle awareness

Down Block → Circle Response

  • Quick block reaction
  • Circle and recover
  • Return to stance after defensive reaction

Entry Development

  • Shot position awareness
  • Penetration step control
  • Entry posture and balance
  • Recovery to stance after entry

⭐ Progress Markers

  • Maintains stance through longer rounds
  • Connects fake → level change smoothly
  • Enters shot position with control
  • Circles and recovers instinctively
  • Pace control under fatigue

🔒 What This Builds Toward

Prepares athletes for deeper attack timing and stronger reaction flow in the next Shadow Wrestler layer.
