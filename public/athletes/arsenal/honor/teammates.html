Segment 2 — Teammate, Partner & I

Honor grows through the way athletes train with and treat the people around them. Strong rooms are built through trust, effort, and accountability between partners and teammates.

Support

  • Encourage teammates during practice
  • Help partners learn drills correctly
  • Celebrate improvement across the room

Accountability

  • Train with discipline together
  • Hold each other to standards
  • Protect the culture of the room

Responsibility

  • Be a trustworthy training partner
  • Communicate clearly during drills
  • Strengthen the room through effort and respect
