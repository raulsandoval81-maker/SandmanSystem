Segment 3 — Team, Partner & I

Honor grows when athletes learn that they are part of something bigger than themselves. This segment focuses on how your discipline, effort, and attitude affect the room, your partners, and the team as a whole.

Connection

  • Recognize that your habits affect the whole room
  • Train with awareness of your partners and teammates
  • Contribute to a stronger team environment

Culture

  • Protect the standards of the room
  • Help keep practice focused and disciplined
  • Represent the team with respect

Responsibility

  • Do your part in training every day
  • Support the work of the group
  • Strengthen the room through effort and character
