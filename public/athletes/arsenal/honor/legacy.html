Segment 6 — Legacy & I

Legacy reflects the long-term impact an athlete leaves on their team and community. Honor at this stage focuses on stewardship and carrying the values of the program forward.

Stewardship

  • Protect the culture of the program
  • Support younger athletes
  • Preserve the standards of the room

Impact

  • Represent the team with pride
  • Inspire others through discipline
  • Leave the program stronger

Continuity

  • Carry lessons beyond the mat
  • Support the growth of future athletes
  • Honor the tradition of the team
