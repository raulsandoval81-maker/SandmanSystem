Segment 5 — Leadership & I

Leadership is earned through consistent discipline and example. Athletes who reach this stage influence the culture of the room through their actions and standards.

Example

  • Demonstrate discipline daily
  • Show effort in every practice
  • Maintain strong attitude and respect

Influence

  • Set the tone for the room
  • Encourage strong habits in teammates
  • Strengthen the culture of the team

Responsibility

  • Carry the values of the program
  • Support coaches and teammates
  • Protect the standards of the room
