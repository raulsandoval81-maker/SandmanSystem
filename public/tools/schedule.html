Classes

Foundry 8 — Youth Combat
Elementary / Junior High · Discipline + fundamentals
Youth
Typical focus: wrestling fundamentals, stance/motion, basic chains, safe sparring
What to bring: water, athletic gear, notebook (optional)
XP: attendance + coach awards (live system is private)
Parents: drop-off / pick-up rules will live in the dashboard once enrollment opens.
Foundry 4 — Teen Combat
High School · Path2Legend lane
Teen
Typical focus: live wrestling, conditioning blocks, match IQ, standards
What to bring: shoes, headgear (optional), water, tape
Notes: schedule flexes during season; changes post privately
This is a public “shape” preview, not the official season calendar.
Foundry 4 — Adult Combat
Adults · training lane (not open enrollment yet)
Adult
Typical focus: boxing / kickboxing blocks, strength circuit, controlled sparring
What to bring: wraps, gloves, mouthguard (if sparring)
Access: invite / pilot only during early build
Adults run Foundry 4 standards too — same XP backbone, different responsibility level.
Open Mat + Strength & Honor
All lanes · optional add-on sessions
Optional
Typical focus: drill reps, position rounds, strength block, mindset logging
What to bring: notebook / phone notes (for Honor prompts later)
Purpose: extra reps + long-arc progress
In the live system this becomes “sessions + check-ins” tied to the private dashboard.
